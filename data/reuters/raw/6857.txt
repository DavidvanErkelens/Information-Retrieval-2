TIGER INTERNATIONAL TGR PAYS INTEREST IN STOCK LOS ANGELES March Tiger International Inc said it will pay the interest due April on its pct Sinking Fund debentures by distributing common stock Each debenture holder will receive the number of whole shares of Tiger common stock obtained by dividing the interest payable by pct of the average closing price of the stock from February through March Fractions will be paid in cash The company said about shares of Tiger common stock will be issued in connection with the interest payment Reuter
