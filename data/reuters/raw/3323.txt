CCC ACCEPTS EXPORT BONUS BARLEY MALT TO NIGERIA WASHINGTON March The Commodity Credit Corporation CCC has accepted one bonus offer from an exporter on the sale of tonnes of barley malt to Nigeria the U S Agriculture Department said The department said the bonus awarded was dlrs per tonne and was made to Rahr Malting Co and will be paid in the form of commodities from the inventory stocks of the CCC The barley malt is scheduled for shipment during April An additional tonnes of barley malt are still available to Nigeria under the Export Enhancement Program initiative announced December it said Reuter
