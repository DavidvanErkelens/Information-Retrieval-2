NORWAY S HELIKOPTER SERVICE TO BUILD BELL HELICOPTERS STAVANGER March Helikopter Service A S Norway s biggest helicopter operator will assemble Bell SP helicopters for Norwegian Defence under license from Bell Helicopters Textron of the U S The company said The medium range helicopters worth mln crowns will be assembled at the company s facilities at Sola airport in Stavanger Helikopter Service technical director Trygve Eidem said the mln crown assembly contract will help the company diversify its operations Helikopter Service s main income is from chartered flights between Norway s North Sea oil fields and mainland heliports REUTER
