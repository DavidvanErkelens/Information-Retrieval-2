ANIMAL FEED SHIP ON FIRE AGAIN AT CHINESE PORT LONDON Oct The Cyprus vessel Fearless tonnes dw which was on fire grounded then towed to Yantai China in August had all its cargo reloaded but the cargo in the no hold caught fire on October The fire was put out with salt water and water from the no hold has spread over most of the cargo Some water is also in the no hold Bottom patching was reported complete but only the no starboard wing tank has been pumped out and remains dry The engine room is flooded to about three metres The ship was originally loaded with tonnes of animal feed REUTER
