OLIN OLN SUBORDINATED NOTES YIELD PCT NEW YORK June Olin Corp is raising mln dlrs via an offering of year subordinated notes yielding pct said lead manager Morgan Stanley and Co Inc The notes have a pct coupon and were priced at to yield basis points more than comparable Treasury securities Non callable for life the issue is rated Baa by Moody s and BBB minus by Standard and Poor s The gross spread is seven dlrs the selling concession is four dlrs and the reallowance is dlrs Salomon Brothers co managed the deal Reuter
