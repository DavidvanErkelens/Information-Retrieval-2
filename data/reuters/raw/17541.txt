BRAMPTON BRICK BBLA TO UPS EARNINGS FORECAST BRAMPTON Ontario June Brampton Brick Ltd said it increased its earnings forecast to cts a share from the cts a share predicted in its October public share offering prospectus It said increased earnings will result from continuing production at its Toronto Don Valley plant past the original termination date of June which will double output In addition annual capacity at unit Brique Citadelle was increased by about pct to mln bricks The revised earnings forecast is after a May agreement to issue class A subordinate voting shares Reuter
