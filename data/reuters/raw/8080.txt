FRANCE TO SIGN DISNEYLAND CONTRACT ON TUESDAY PARIS March The French government said it would sign a contract with Walt Disney Co on Tuesday for the construction of Europe s first Disneyland amusement park A statement from the office of Prime Minister Jacques Chirac said the project which will cost some billion francs would create up to jobs and be built outside Paris Funding will be provided by a combination of bank loans preferential financing by the French state and private investment France has also agreed to invest two billion francs to extend rail and road links REUTER
