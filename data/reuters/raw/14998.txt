EQUITABLE RESOURCES EQT FILES UNIT OFFERING NEW YORK April Equitable Resources Inc said it filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission a registration statement covering a mln dlr issue of units Each unit will consist of a dlr face amount year debenture with up to five year warrants to purchase the company s common stock Each warrant will equal one share The debentures will be non refundable for years Proceeds will be used to repay short term loans incurred to finance part of Equitable s capital expenditure program and the redemption of pct and pct first mortgage bonds of First Boston will manage the issue Reuter
