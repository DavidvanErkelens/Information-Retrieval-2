CANADA LAUNCHES BILLION DLR BOND ISSUE OTTAWA March Canada will offer a billion dlr four part bond issue tomorrow which will be dated March and delivered March the finance department said The issue will consist of four maturities pct bonds due July pct bonds due March pct bonds due March pct bonds due June The maturity will be issued to a maximum of mln dlrs The Bank of Canada will buy mln dlrs of the new issue including mln dlrs of the maturity Reuter
