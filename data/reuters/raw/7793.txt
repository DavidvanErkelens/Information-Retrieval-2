CHAD SAYS IT KILLED LIBYANS IN BATTLE PARIS March Chadian troops killed Libyans and captured in fighting near the key Libyan air base of Ouadi Doum in northern Chad a Chadian government statement said The statement issued by Chad s Paris embassy said government troops had completely annihilated a Libyan column yesterday some km miles from the Libyan base It was the highest number of Libyan casualties reported since January when Chadian troops overran an important garrison at the oasis of Fada with the loss of more than Libyan troops Ouadi Doumi central northern Chad is the site of Libya s main airfield in the country and several clashes have been reported over the past week in the surrounding desert between troops loyal to President Hissene Habre and Libyan forces Reuter
