COMMONWEALTH ALUMINUM PUTS PLANT ON THE BLOCK GOLDENDALE Wash April Commonwealth Aluminum Comalco said it put its Goldendale Wash smelter back on the market after would be buyer Columbia Aluminum Corp of Hermiston Ore failed to pull together financing by an April one deadline The plant which has an asking price of mln dlrs plus several mln more dlrs for inventory it said Commonwealth said it is continuing talks with Columbia but has also opened talks with other interested parties Commonwealth bought the plant in January and closed it Feb leaving about workers jobless Reuter
