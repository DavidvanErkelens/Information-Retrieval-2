CHEVRON CHV CUTS UNC UNC STAKE TO PCT WASHINGTON June Chevron Corp said it sold mln shares of UNC Inc common stock on June leaving it with mln UNC common shares or pct of the total In a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission Chevron said it sold the shares for dlrs each or a total of mln dlrs under an agreement it had with UNC The stock was sold to an underwriting syndicate managed by Dillon Read and Co Inc and Donaldson Lufkin and Jenrette Securities Corp with the underwriters paying mln dlrs and UNC paying mln it said Reuter
