INDIA BOUGHT TONNES OF RBD OLEIN AT TENDER LONDON March The Indian State Trading Corporation STC bought four cargoes of rbd palm olein totalling tonnes at its vegetable oil import tender last week traders said Market reports on Friday said the STC had booked two cargoes The business comprised three tonne cargoes for June at dlrs and tonnes for July at dlrs per tonne cif It also secured a tonne cargo of optional origin rapeseed oil for May Jun shipment at dlrs cif REUTER
