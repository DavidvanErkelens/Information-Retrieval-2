FORMER SANTA FE EXEC ADMITS INSIDER TRADING NEW YORK March Darius N Keaton former member of the board of Santa Fe International Corp pleaded guilty to an insider trading scheme that netted him dlrs in profit In Manhattan Federal Court Keaton pleaded guilty to trading on non public information involving his purchase of shares of the company s stock in September shortly before the company was acquired by Kuwait Petroleum Co in for two billion dlrs Keaton of Monterey Calif admitted he failed to report the purchase to the Securities and Exchange Commission and admitted commiting wire fraud stemming from his transaction He faces up to years in prison on both counts and a fine of dlrs when he is sentenced by Judge Whitman Knapp on June Reuter
