CCC ACEPTS BONUS BID ON WHEAT TO ZAIRE USDA WASHINGTON April The Commodity Credit Crporation has accepted a bid for an export bonus to cover the sale of tonnes of hard red winter wheat to Zaire the U S Agriculture Department said The wheat is scheduled for shipment May June the department said The bonus to Continental Grain Co was dlrs per tonne and will be paid in the form of commodities from CCC stocks An additional tonnes of wheat are still available to Zaire under the Export Enhancement program initiative announced on October it said Reuter
