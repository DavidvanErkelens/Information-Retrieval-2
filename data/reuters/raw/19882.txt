BELDEN BBE TO SELL UNITS INTERNATIONALLY NORTH CANTON Ohio June Belden and Blake Energy Co a master limited partnership said it entered into an agreement to sell of its units for two mln dlrs in cash The company said Great Pacific Capital S A of Geneva Switzerland is acting as lead manager for the private placement of the units with international investors Under the agreement it said an option has also been granted to buy up to additional units at five dlrs per unit on or before July Reuter
