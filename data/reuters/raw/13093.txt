CCC ACCEPTS EXPORT BONUS FOR WHEAT TO SRI LANKA WASHINGTON April The U S Agriculture Department said it has accepted a bid for an export bonus to cover the sale of tonnes of hard red winter wheat to Sri Lanka A bonus of dlrs per tonne was awarded to Continental Grain Co on the shipment scheduled for April Melvin Sims USDA general sales manager said An additional tonnes of wheat are still available to Sri Lanka under the export enhancement program Sims said Reuter
