HELABA UNIT ISSUES LUXEMBOURG FRANC PLACEMENT LUXEMBOURG March Helaba Luxembourg Hessische Landesbank International has issued a mln Luxembourg franc seven year private placement with a coupon of pct and priced at par lead manager Banque Generale du Luxembourg said A spokesman said the issue had already been placed in full Fees total pct with pct for co managers The management group includes Banque Paribas Luxembourg Kredietbank SA Luxembourgeoise and Banque de Luxembourg SA The borrower is an affiliate of West Germany s Hessische Landesbank Girozentral REUTER
