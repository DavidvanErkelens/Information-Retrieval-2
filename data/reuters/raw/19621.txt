PENNWALT PSM INTRODUCES NEW REFRIGERANT PHILADELPHIA June Pennwalt Corp said it is making a new refrigerant available as an alternative to chlorofluorocarbon CFC a product suspected of depleting the earth s ozone layer The company said the new product called isotron b is a blend of partially halogenated CFCs and has less than five pct of the ozone depletion potential of CFC which is fully halogenated According to the company the blend is currently used to replace CFC as a propellant in aerosol containers and has been successfully tested as a refrigerant Pennwalt manufactures chemicals pharmaceuticals and precision equipment Reuter
