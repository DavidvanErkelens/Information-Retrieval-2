ENCOR PLANS MLN DLR DEBENTURE OFFERING CALGARY Alberta March Encor Energy Corp Inc said it planned an issue in Canada of mln dlrs of pct subordinated debentures convertible any time into Encor common shares for a price of dlrs a share Encor pct owned by Dome Petroleum Ltd DMP said investment dealers McLeod Young Weir Ltd and Pemberton Houston Willoughby Bell Gouinlock Inc agreed to purchase the issue for resale to the public It said the debentures would pay interest semi annually and be callable after five years at par plus accrued interest Encor earlier reported a net loss of mln dlrs Encor last year earned mln dlrs The net loss included a mln dlr asset writedown before a mln dlr recovery of deferred taxes The company said results were seriously affected by lower oil and gas prices but did not reflect its recent acquisition of Aberford Resources Ltd It said that while oil prices had improved in early it would remain cautious and base capital spending on conservative price assumptions Encor will use proceeds from the debenture offer to retire bank debt incurred in the Aberford deal it said Reuter
