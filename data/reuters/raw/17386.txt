GREEK BULK ORE CARRIER AGROUND IN ORINOCO RIVER LONDON April A Greek bulk carrier loaded with iron ore has run aground in Venezuela s Orinoco river Lloyd s shipping services reports The dwt Andromachi is reported to have run aground near mile on Monday and attempts to refloat the vessel using tugs has so far been unsuccessful Lloyd s said The Andromachi is managed by Theodore and Angelos Efstathiou of Piraeus Reuter
