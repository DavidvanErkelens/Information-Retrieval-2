FRENCH AGRICULTURAL BANK ISSUES MLN FRANC BOND PARIS March Banque Francaise de l Agriculture said it is issuing for its own account a mln franc variable rate seven year and day domestic bond which it will lead along with l Union de Garantie et de Placement The bond issued at pct will have interest based on the annualised money market rate TAM with the first coupon paid on April Denominations are of francs nominal and the issue will be quoted on the Paris Bourse REUTER
