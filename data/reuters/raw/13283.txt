U K RETAIL SALES RISE FINAL PCT IN FEBRUARY LONDON April The volume of U K Retail sales rose a final seasonally adjusted pct in February after falling a final pct in January Department of Trade and Industry figures show The February all retailers sales index base was put at a final after in January In the three months from December to February the level of sales was unchanged from the previous three months but was up by almost pct from the same period On a non seasonally adjusted basis retail sales in February were pct higher than a year earlier the Department said REUTER
