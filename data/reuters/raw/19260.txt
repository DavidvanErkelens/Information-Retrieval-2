SCI HOLDINGS MAY BE UPGRADED BY MOODY S NEW YORK June Moody s Investors Service Inc said it may upgrade the ratings on billion dlrs of debt of SCI Holdings Inc The agency cited SCI s announcement that it agreed to sell for billion dlrs six television stations and related properties to a joint venture formed by SCI and Gillett Holdings Inc While financing details are not available the agency said SCI s debt should decline as a result of the sale BCI currently carries Ba zero coupon senior notes and B senior subordinated debentures The BCI unit Storer Communications Inc has B subordinated debentures Reuter
