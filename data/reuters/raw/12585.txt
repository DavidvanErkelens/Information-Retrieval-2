FLORIDA INVESTMENT FIRM SHUT BY SEC MIAMI April The Securities and Exchange Commission SEC had Elliott Enterprises an investment firm in Naples Fla shut down accusing it of defrauding investors and misusing funds An SEC spokesman in Miami said the agency closed the investment holding company and two related firms Elliott Securities and Elliott Mortgage Co In a complaint filed in federal court in Miami the SEC said the firms had at least customers who invested around mln dlrs U S District Judge William Hoeveler ordered the company closed appointed a receiver and froze the assets of the firm s executives Reuter
