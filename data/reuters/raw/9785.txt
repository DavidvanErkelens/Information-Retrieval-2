EC SELLS BUTTER STOCKS TO SOVIET UNION BRUSSELS March The European Community today sold tonnes of ageing butter to the Soviet Union at a special knockdown price a spokesman for the EC Commission said He said the EC s Dairy Management Committee accepted bids from operators to export the butter mainly from intervention stores in Belgium West Germany and the Netherlands at a price of European currency units per kilos The butter was bought into Community stores at Ecus per kilos The sales are part of the EC s process of attempting to run down its butter surplus REUTER
