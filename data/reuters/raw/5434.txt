MIM TO ACQUIRE STAKE IN GERMAN COPPER PRODUCER FRANKFURT March Mount Isa Mines Holding Ltd plans to acquire a pct stake in Europe s largest primary copper producer Norddeutsche Affinerie AG a spokesman for Metallgesellschaft AG said MIM intends to take Preussag AG s total pct share in the copper producer in exchange for some three pct of MIM s share capital MIM will also take another pct now held by Degussa AG reducing Degussa s share to pct from Metallgesellschaft s share will remain at pct The move is subject to approval of the federal cartel office and supervisory boards of the companies involved Reuter
