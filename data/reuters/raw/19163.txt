SUDAN BOOSTS BUDGET CASH FOR DEBT SERVICING KHARTOUM June Sudan has set aside mln dlrs to service foreign debts in according to budget plans presented to parliament This compares with mln dlrs earmarked for debt repayment in the year ending June Sudan has a foreign debt totalling billion dlrs Finance Minister Beshir Omar presenting plans to parliament last night put the budget deficit at billion pounds against billion in He said the budget would be the first in a four year economic recovery plan to be announced by the end of December Of the total set aside for debt servicing Omar said mln dlrs would go to principal and mln to interest payments Sudan has an annual debt liability of mln dlrs Khartoum based Western experts say Omar said last March it would be difficult if not impossible for Sudan to meet scheduled debt repayments of billion dlrs over the next five years He said last night Sudan was billion dlrs in arrears on debt repayments in the first half of this year The experts say Sudan owes pct of its foreign debt to Western government creditors pct to governments of Eastern bloc and Arab countries and pct to commercial banks with most of the rest owed to multilateral sources Sudan unable to fully service its debts since and declared ineligible last year by the International Monetary Fund for fresh loans has been servicing only creditors banned from extending fresh loans to recipients in arrears Omar said government revenue in would total billion pounds Expenditure was put at billion pounds He said the budget deficit would be fully covered by loans government deposits and other sources Omar said mln pounds would be spent on subsidising sugar and wheat flour prices in He said some of Sudan s Western backers were witholding aid until an agreement with the IMF was reached Sudan which owes the IMF to mln dlrs in arrears is scheduled to hold fresh talks with the Fund in late July or August on reforming its economy and clearing arrears REUTER
