INTERCO ESTABLISHES MLN DLR REVOLVING FACILITY LONDON March Missouri based Interco Inc is establishing a mln dlr five year revolving credit facility Swiss Bank Corp International Ltd said as arranger The facility allows for eurodollar and swingline advances up to mln dlrs and also has an uncommitted option for short term multi currency advances The margin for eurodollar advances is basis points over London Interbank Offered Rates LIBOR and there is a utilisation fee of five basis points per annum if utilisation exceeds pct Swiss Bank Corp said that the facility pays a commitment fee of basis points per annum on undrawn amounts It has been syndicated on a club basis and signing is planned by the end of March Interco is a manufacturer and retailer of consumer products and services REUTER
