CALUMET CALI GETS MLN DLRS FROM WARRANTS CHICAGO March Calumet Industries Inc said it raised mln dlrs from holders exercising company warrants The program expired February It issued shares to holders who had exercised options to exchange four warrants and dlrs for each share of common stock About pct of its warrants were exercised These proceeds and another mln dlrs from the sale of notes will provide long term financing for its HydroCal heavy hydrotreating system under construction at its Princeton La refinery Reuter
