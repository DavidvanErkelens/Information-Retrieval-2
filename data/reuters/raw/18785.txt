STORAGE STK SAYS COURT CLEARS REORGANIZATION LOUISVILLE Colo June Storage Technology Corp said the U S Bankruptcy Court has approved its plan of reorganization and its emergence from Chapter bankruptcy Under the terms of the plan the company said all approved creditors claims will be paid in full in cash equity and notes It said it will settle about mln dlrs in liabilities through the payment of about mln dlrs in cash mln dlrs of year pct notes and mln additional common shares The company said existing common shareholders will hold about pct of the company s shares after the distribution The company said the distribution of the cash equity and notes to creditors will start in July Reuter
