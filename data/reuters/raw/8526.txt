BORDEN BN PLANT FINED ON POLLUTION VIOLATIONS NEW YORK March The U S Environmental Protection Agency said Borden Inc was fined dlrs for the delay of the installation of pollution controls in its canning plant in Lyons N Y The EPA said the U S District Court in Rochester N Y assessed the penalty in connection with its failure to comply with EPA approved air quality regulations by July After Borden sought relief through the courts a final compliance date was set for Dec the EPA said The EPA said that Borden did not adequately correct the problems by the date and rejected Borden s argument that it had made a good faith effort to rectify the problems Borden sold the facility to C B Foods in January the EPA said Reuter
