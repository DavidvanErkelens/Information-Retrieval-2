JAPANESE BANKS CONFIRM ARGENTINA LOAN COMMITMENT TOKYO June Japanese commercial creditor banks confirmed an earlier commitment to extend some mln dlrs in new loans to Argentina banking sources said The commitment will represent a part of the billion dlrs in new loans to Argentina agreed to by banks worldwide last April the sources said The banks were supposed to confirm their Argentina loan commitments by June About pct of them have already done so but it may take some more time before all have confirmed their intentions the sources added REUTER
