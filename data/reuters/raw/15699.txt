GENCORP GY EXTENDS SHAREHOLDER RIGHT PLAN AKRON Ohio April GenCorp Inc said its board extended to April the date the rights issued under the company s shareholder rights plan will trade separately from the common stock The company said the plan originally adopted February has been extended several times since its adoption GenCorp said the rights plan makes it more difficult for an outside company or person to takeover the company The plan has been extended indefinitely and to no person acquiring pct or more of the common stock prior to April the company said The plan calls for the attachment of one preferred stock purchase right to each common share outstanding On Monday GenCorp announced it will buy up to pct of its own outstanding common shares for dlrs per share It said details of the plan will be announced by the end of the week Reuter
