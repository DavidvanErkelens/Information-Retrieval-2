CHRISTIANIA BANK ISSUES EUROYEN BOND LONDON March Christiania Bank og Kreditkasse is issuing a billion yen eurobond due March paying pct and priced at pct lead manager Yamaichi International Europe Ltd said The bond is available in denominations of mln yen and will be listed in London Fees comprise pct selling concession and pct management and underwriting combined The redemption rate will be calculated according to a complex formula tied to the spot yen dlr currency rate Yamaichi said Mitsui Trust International Ltd is co lead REUTER
