STOP AND SHOP S SHP BRADLEES FEBRUARY SALES UP BOSTON March Stop and Shop Cos Inc said sales for the four weeks ended February for its Bradlees Discount Department Stores Division were up six pct to mln dlrs from mln dlrs a year before with same store sales up three pct The company said the modest comparable store sales increase was due to a combination of difficult weather conditions in the Northeast a later Easter this year and a possible slowing in consumer demand Reuter
