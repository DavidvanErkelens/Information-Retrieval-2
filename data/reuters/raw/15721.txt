CORROON BLACK CBL SAYS UNIT IN LLOYD S FUND NEW YORK April Corroon and Black Corp said Minet Holdings PLC of which it owns pct agreed to participate in a mln stg fund set up by Lloyd s of London to cover part of the claims faced by PCW underwriting agencies The company said Minet and several other companies were involved in the syndicate which faces mln stg in claims due to fraud and mismanagement The fund was proposed by Lloyd s to cover a portion of the claims in return for a contribution of mln stg from PCW members The offer is subject to acceptance by pct of the PCW members by May Lloyd s mln stg offer including mln stg from Lloyd s and mln stg from brokers is designed to cover the reinsurance costs on the mln stg of net liabilities Minet was unable to disclose the amount of its proposed contribution to the fund Caroon and Black said However Minet was confident that the losses could be funded from existing cash resources Under the terms proposed Lloyd s would take over all members future obligations connected with PCW and members would agree not to pursue the matter in court Reuter
