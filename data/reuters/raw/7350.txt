ITALIAN TREASURY OFFER HAS LOWER RATES ROME March The Italian Treasury said it would offer billion lire of short term Treasury bills BOTs at net rates lower than on the preceding issue Three month bills worth billion lire will be offered for competitive tender at a base price of pct giving an effective annualised compound yield after tax of pct Net rate on the preceding issue end February was pct The Treasury will also offer billion lire of six month bills at a base price of pct for an effective annualized net yield of pct The net rate on the preceding issue was pct A total of billion lire of month paper will be offered at a base price of pct for an effective annualized net yield of pct Rate on the preceding issue mid February was pct The bills replace maturing paper worth billion lire of which was held by operators Subscriptions to the offer close March Reuter
