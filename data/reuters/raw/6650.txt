COCA COLA ENTERPRISES CCE TO SELL DEBT NEW YORK March Coca Cola Enterprises Inc said it filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission a registration statement covering mln dlrs of debt The company said it plans to sell mln dlrs of notes due and an equal amount of debentures due Proceeds will be used to refinance outstanding indebtedness Coca Cola Enterprises said The company named Allen and Co Inc as lead manager and Merrill Lynch Capital Markets and Salomon Brothers Inc as co managers of the offerings Reuter
