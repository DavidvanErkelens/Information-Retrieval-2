PACIFIC RESOURCES PRI NAMED IN LAWSUIT NEW YORK March Pacific Resources Inc said it has been named in a lawsuit filed in California by Aetna Casualty and Surety Co AET relating to the Chapter bankruptcy of Paramount Petroleum Corp PPC in which a PRI subsidiary had a pct stake Aetna is claiming up to mln dlrs in compensatory damages and mln dlrs in punitive damages against the defendants as a result of alleged acts to fraudulently induce Aetna to issue surety bonds for the benefit of PPC and its predecessor Pacific Oassis PRI said that the legal action against it lacks merit Reuter
