HOUSE COMMITTEE SLASHES STAR WARS FUNDING WASHINGTON April The House Armed Services Committee slashed President Reagan s funding request for the Strategic Defense Initiative SDI approving billion dlrs less than he had proposed for the anti missile program The committee during its consideration of the defense authorization bill also cut by more than half Reagan s request for a rail system for strategic missiles The proposals must be adopted by the full House and Senate before becoming law The committee approved billion dlrs for SDI a land and space based anti missile defense program commonly known as Star Wars The amount was billion dlrs less than the administration request of billion dlrs The Reagan administration has requested a total of billion dlrs for defense in but congressional leaders have said that will be cut as part of a budget trimming effort to reduce the huge federal deficit In other action today the committee approved mln dlrs for a rail system for inter continental ballistic missiles but did not specify which missile system Supporters have said missiles on such a rail network would be less vulernable to attack than those in fixed underground silos The administration requested mln dlrs specifically for a rail mobile system for the warhead MX missile But many congressmen including House Armed Services Chairman Les Aspin a Wisconsin Democrat favor a smaller mobile missile dubbed the Midgetman Reuter
