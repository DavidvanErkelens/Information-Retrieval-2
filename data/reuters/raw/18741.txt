IMF LOANS UGANDA MLN DLRS WASHINGTON June The International Monetary Fund said it has loaned Uganda mln dlrs to aid in economic recovery The fund said mln dlrs of the loan is available through the so called structural adjustment facility SAF over the next three years The fund said mln dlrs will be available immediately under the SAF and a separate financing arrangement After expanding in the early s Uganda s economy has suffered setbacks since of falling industrial and agricultural output and accelerating inflation the fund said The current economic downturn has been worsened by the sharp decline in the world price of coffee Uganda s major export the fund said Reuter
