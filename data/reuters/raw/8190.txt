UNITED SCIENTIFIC UNIT LANDS MLN DLR CONTRACT LONDON March Electro optronic equipment makers United Scientific Holdings Plc said the Advanced Products Division of its U S Subsidiary OEC Dallas Texas has been awarded a mln dlr contract by the U S Army The contract is for the production of high energy laser protection filters for binoculars The three year contract already includes options to the value of eight mln dlrs United reported pretax profit of mln stg on turnover of mln stg in the year ended September The company s shares were unchanged on Friday s close of p following today s announcement REUTER
