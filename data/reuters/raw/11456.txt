U S OIL USOL TO SELL SHARES NEW YORK March U S Oil Co said V di Guevara Suardo Fabbri chairman chief executive and controlling shareholder signed an agreement to buy up to shares of a new series C preferred stock at dlrs per share Under terms of the agreement the board can by unanimous consent call for payment on any portion of the shares at any time with payment to be made within days U S Oil said the financing structure was established to facilitate additional capital contribution by Fabbri The shares which have no voting or conversion rights are redeemable at the option of U S Oil and pay a pct accruing dividend payable upon redemption The agreement remains in effect until December unless terminated under certain conditions It is not anticipated that U S Oil will call shares for payment unless the proceeds are needed for a specific transaction Reuter
