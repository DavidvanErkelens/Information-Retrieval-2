SWEDISH EXPORT CREDIT LAUNCHES AUSTRALIAN DLR BOND LONDON March Swedish Export Credit SEK has launched a novel mln Australian dlr bond which will give the investor the option of receiving interest and principal in Australian dollars or marks Warburg Securities said as lead manager The issue is a five year bullet maturing March and carries a coupon of pct and is priced at The coupon will be paid pct in Australian dlrs with the investor having the option to receive the balance in Australian dlrs or marks Redemption at maturity is at par when pct will be paid in Australian dlrs and again the investor will be able to opt for the balance in one of the two currencies The exchange rate has been fixed at Australian dlrs to the mark Fees total two pct and are divided pct for selling and pct for management and underwriting combined The bonds will be sold in denominations of Australian dlrs and listing will be in Luxembourg Payment is March The lead manager noted that the issue already has been pre placed in Europe and the Far East It is believed to be the largest ever issue denominated in Australian dlrs REUTER
