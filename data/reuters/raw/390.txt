OCCIDENTAL OXY UNIT TO REDEEM DEBENTURES LOS ANGELES March Occidental Petroleum Corp said its MidCon Corp subsidiary will redeem on March all dlrs of its outstanding pct convertible subordinated debentures due at pct of par It said interest payable March will be paid in the usual manner to holders of record on March The debentures convert to common stock at dlrs per share or shares per dlrs principal amount through March Holders converting through March will be entitled to receive Occidental s regular quarterly dividend of cts per share on common stock that is payable April The company said any holders surrendering debentures for conversion after March other than those surrendering for conversion on March will be required to pay to the conversion agent an amount equal to the interest paytable on the debentures on March Reuter
