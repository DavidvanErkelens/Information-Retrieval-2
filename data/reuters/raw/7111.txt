DOME MINES DM SELLS MLN COMMON SHARES TORONTO March Dome Mines Ltd said it sold seven mln common shares priced at dlrs each to an underwriting group for net proceeds to the company of about dlrs after underwriting fees and issue expenses About mln dlrs of the proceeds will be used to repay outstanding bank debt and the balance will be used to strengthen the company s cash position Dome Mines said The underwriting group includes Dominion Securities Inc Burns Fry Ltd Wood Gundy Inc and Gordon Capital Corp Reuter
