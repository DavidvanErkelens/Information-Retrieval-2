GKN REORGANISES JOINT VENTURE LONDON March GKN Plc said it and Zahnradfabrik Friedrichshafen AG would reorganise the ownership structure of their Viscodrive GmbH joint venture Under the agreement GKN s Uni Cardan AG unit would increase its stake in Viscodrive to pct from pct while ZF s stake would drop to pct At the same time ZF would take an pct stake in a new company Steertec Lenkungen GmbH leaving Uni Cardan with pct Viscodrive manufactures viscous control units while Steertec will develop power steering systems REUTER
