GROUP SELLS MOST OF STAKE IN CCX INC CCX WASHINGTON Oct A shareholder group including Far Hills N J attorney Natalie Koether said it reduced its stake in CCX Inc common stock to shares or less than one pct of the company s common stock outstanding from a previous stake of about ten pct In a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission the group said it sold CCX common shares on October at four dlrs a share The group gave no reason for the sales Reuter
