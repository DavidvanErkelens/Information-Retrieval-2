BANK OF SPAIN SUSPENDS ASSISTANCE MADRID March The Bank of Spain suspended its daily money market assistance obliging borrowers to take funds from the second window where on Wednesday rates were raised to pct compared with pct for normal overnight funds Money market sources said in view of high borrower demand the suspension was likely to remain in effect until April the start of the next day accounting period for reserve requirements The suspension comes after the Bank yesterday gave billion pesetas assistance a record high for this year It said billion pesetas was provided at the second window REUTER
