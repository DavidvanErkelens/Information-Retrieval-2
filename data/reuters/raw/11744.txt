GULF STATES GSU SUBMITS FINANCING PLAN AUSTIN Texas March Gulf States Utilities Co said it filed a mln dlr financing plan with Texas regulators and asked for quick approval so that it can implement a previously approved mln dlr interim rate increase In the filing with the Texas Public Utilities Commission Gulf States said it listed six sources of funding that it believes meet the commission s requirement that it obtain mln dlrs in credit and other financing before it can institute the interim rate hike Reuter
