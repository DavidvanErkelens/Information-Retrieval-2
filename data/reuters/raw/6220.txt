HONDURAS AUTHORIZED TO BUY PL COMMODITIES WASHINGTON March Honduras has been authorized to buy about tonnes of U S wheat about tonnes of U S corn and about tonnes of U S tallow under an existing PL agreement the U S Agriculture Department said The department said it may buy the wheat valued at mln dlrs the corn valued at mln and the tallow valued at mln dlrs between March and August and ship it from U S ports and or Canadian transshipment points by this September The purchase authorizations cover the entire quantity provided under the agreement signed March Reuter
