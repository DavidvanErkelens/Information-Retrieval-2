CARIBBEAN SELECT CSEL TO REDEEM WARRANTS TAMPA Fla March Caribbean Select Inc said it has elected to redeem on April all its Class A warrants and all Class B warrants at ct each At the same time it said its board has decided to reduce the exercise price of the Class B warrants to dlrs per common share from four dlrs to encourage the exercise of the warrants Each Class B warrant allows the purchase of one common share It said each Class A warrant is still exercisable into one common share at two dlrs each Reuter
