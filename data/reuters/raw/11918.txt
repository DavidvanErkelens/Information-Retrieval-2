FRENCH GROUP ISSUES TWO BONDS FOR MLN FRANCS PARIS April Groupement pour le financement des Ouvrages de Batiment Travaux Publics et activites annexes GOBTP plans to launch two variable rate domestic bonds totalling mln francs lead managers Credit Commercial de France CCF and Caisse Centrale des Banques Populaires CCBP said The first mln franc year bond led by CCF will be issued at pct with interest based on the annualised money market rate TAM and payable on April every year with the first coupon paid next year The second mln franc year bond led by CCBP will be issued at pct with interest also based on TAM with the same coupon payment date Both bonds will in principle be redeemable at par on April REUTER
