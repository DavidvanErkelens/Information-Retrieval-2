WORLD BANK LOANS INDIA MLN DLRS WASHINGTON June The World Bank said it has extended three loans to India totalling mln dlrs for two projects in the power sector and one for water supply and sewage One power project will be supported by a mln dlr loan the bank said The other power project supported by a mln dlr loan plans to aid in the first phase of a power generation complex in eastern India the bank said The third project which is being supported by a bank loan of mln dlrs and an International Development Association loan of mln dlrs hopes to increase water supply to Madras Reuter
