TEXTRON S TXT BILLION DLR CONTRACT FINAL STARTFORD April Textron Inc s Avco Lycoming Textron subsidiary said it finalized a billion dlr five year contract with the U S Army for M Abrams main battle tank gas turbine engines Under the contract Avco said the army will procure installed engines plus spare engines and a combination of modules and gear boxes It also said the agreement includes an initial purchase of engines by the U S Marines under a program that will result in a fleet of M tanks Reuter
