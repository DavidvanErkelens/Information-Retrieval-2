UBS UNIT ISSUES BILLION YEN EUROBOND LONDON April Union Bank of Switzerland NV is issuing a billion yen eurobond due June paying pct and priced at pct lead manager Union Bank of Switzerland Securities Ltd said The issue has a call option and put option after four years at par and is guaranteed by Union Bank of Switzerland The selling concession is one pct while management and underwriting combined pays pct The issue is available in denominations of one mln yen and will be listed in Luxembourg The payment date is June REUTER
