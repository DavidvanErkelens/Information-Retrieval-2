CITIBANK LAUNCHES CURRENCY WARRANTS LONDON March Citibank NA is issuing up to currency warrants that give the holder a call right on U S Dollars from marks or yen Citicorp Investment Bank Ltd said as sole manager Each warrant priced at dlrs gives the holder the right to purchase a nominal sum of dlrs for either marks or yen between March and March The warrants will be listed in Luxembourg and pay date is March Exercise requiring one day s notice must be for a minimum of warrants through one currency only REUTER
