FRUIT OF THE LOOM OFFERS DEBENTURES NOTES NEW YORK March Fruit of the Loom Inc said it is offering mln dlrs of pct convertible subordinated debentures due March and mln dlrs of pct senior subordinated notes due July Both issues are priced at par The convertible debentures may be exchanged for class A common stock at the holder s option at dlrs Drexel Burnham Lambert Inc is lead manager for both offerings The convertible debentures are rated B by Moody s Investors Service Inc and CCC plus by Standard and Poor s Corp The notes are rates B by Moody s and CCC plus by S P Reuter
