ECUADOR ACCEPTS EXPLORATION BIDS QUITO June Ecuador is accepting bids from companies wishing to explore for crude oil in a million hectare area in the southeast of the country a spokesman for the Ecuadorian State Oil Company CEPE said The spokesman said companies have until October to present their bids and until the middle of next year to sign contracts Competitors may bid for six alternative hectare areas in the southeast forest bordering Peru and in the western coastal plain around the Pacific Petrocanada will start by drilling two exploratory wells in a hectare area Reuter
