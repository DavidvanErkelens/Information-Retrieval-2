USDA SAYS WHEAT PURCHASED FROM FOREIGN SELLERS WASHINGTON March The U S Agriculture Department said private U S exporters reported purchases from foreign sellers of tonnes of wheat for delivery to unknown destinations The wheat consisting of tonnes of hard red winter and tones of soft red winter is for delivery during the season which begins June A purchase from a foreign seller is a transaction in which a U S exporter contracts to buy U S produced commodities from a foreign firm the department said Reuter
