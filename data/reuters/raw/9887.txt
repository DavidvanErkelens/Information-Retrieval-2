OAKWOOD HOMES OH SELLS CONVERTIBLE DEBENTURES NEW YORK March Oakwood Homes Corp is raising mln dlrs via an offering of convertible subordinated debentures due with a pct coupon and par pricing said lead manager Donaldson Lufkin and Jenrette Securities Corp The debentures are convertible into the company s common stock at dlrs per share representing a premium of pct over the stock price when terms on the debt were set Non callable for three years the issue is rated B by Moody s Investors and B by Standard and Poor s J C Bradford and Legg Mason co managed the deal Reuter
