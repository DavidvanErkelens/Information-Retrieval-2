RURAL BANKING AND FINANCE ISSUES ZERO COUPON BOND LONDON April The Rural Banking and Finance Corporation of New Zealand is issuing a zero coupon eurobond with a total redemption amount of billion yen lead manager Nomura International Ltd said The issue matures on April and is priced at pct The deal is being sold through the Bank of New Zealand and is guaranteed by New Zealand The selling concession is basis points while management pays basis points The payment date is April while listing will be in Luxembourg REUTER
