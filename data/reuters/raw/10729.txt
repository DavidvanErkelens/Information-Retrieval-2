KANSALLIS OSAKE PANKKI LAUNCHES MLN DLR BONDS HELSINKI March Finland s Kansallis Osake Pankki said in a statement it is issuing mln dlrs subordinated bonds due with equity warrants in international markets with maximum interest rate of pct Pricing will probably be April the statement said The new issue could raise the bank s share capital by mln Finnish marks from the present billion it said The statement said the issue followed up the tender issuance involving the first non restricted or free shares which can be held by aliens The warrants entitle holders to subscribe for a maximum of mln free shares Eight mln of the Bank s current mln shares are free shares a spokeswoman said The float follows the signing by Finnish President Mauno Koivisto last Friday of legislation entitling aliens with effect from next June to own up to two fifths of the shares in Finnish companies with consent of the Council of State as against one fifth although voting rights will be restricted REUTER
