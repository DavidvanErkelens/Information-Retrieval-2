N ZEALAND RESERVE BANK GIVES BOND TENDER DETAILS WELLINGTON April The Reserve Bank said it will offer mln N Z Dlrs in government bonds of two differing maturities at a pct coupon at a bond tender on April The bank said it will offer mln dlrs of bonds maturing March and mln dlrs of bonds maturing July The bond tender is the first for the fiscal year ending on March A full bond tender program for the year has not yet been announced REUTER
