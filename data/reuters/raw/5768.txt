JAPAN WHOLESALE PRICES FALL PCT IN FEBRUARY TOKYO March Japan s overall wholesale price index base fell pct in February from a month earlier to for the second consecutive month on month fall the Bank of Japan said Wholesale prices fell pct in January from December The index fell pct from a year earlier for the st straight year on year drop the central bank said The reduced month on month drop mainly stemmed from the recovery in world crude oil prices it said On a customs cleared basis crude oil prices rose to around dlrs a barrel in February from about dlrs in January The average value of the yen against the dollar rose pct to in February from in January and was up pct from a year earlier the bank said The export price index in February fell pct from a month earlier reflecting price cuts in export products like cars and precision instruments due to severe foreign competition The index was down pct from a year earlier The import price index rose pct from a month earlier to for the sixth successive month on month gain mainly due to the continued strength of world crude oil prices But the index was down pct from a year earlier The domestic price index fell pct to in February its th straight monthly drop mainly reflecting falls in prices of home appliances such as colour televisions due to severe sales competition at home Lower edible oil prices added to the decline The index was down pct from a year earlier The domestic index marked its largest year on year drop of pct in January since the central bank started compiling such statistics in under its current system Wholesale prices are likely to turn upward in March as higher world crude oil and raw material prices are expected the bank said REUTER
