PAPER SAYS U S TRADE DEFICIT CUT TO BILLION WASHINGTON April The Washington Post said the U S Commerce Department yesterday issued a new report showing that the U S Merchandise trade deficit was billion dollars in February a reduction of billion dlrs from the billion figure the department reported only two days earlier The newspaper said News of the unexpectedly large billion deficit helped batter the dollar s value on foreign exchange markets and boosted U S Interest rates However the new report went largely unnoticed by financial markets since such a downward revision in the deficit is a monthly occurrence The Washington Post said By law the department must first publish what a top commerce official agreed are misleading trade figures and then wait hours before putting out the more accurate ones No one was immediately available at the Commerce Department for comment on the Washington Post report REUTER
