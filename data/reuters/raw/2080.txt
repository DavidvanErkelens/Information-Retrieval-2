NOEL INDUSTRIES NOL BOARD APPROVES FINANCING NEW YORK March Noel Industries Inc said its board approved in principle a private placement of units each unit consisting of dlrs of nine pct senior subordinated convertible debentures due Marcxh and warrants to purchase Noel common The company said chief executive officer Leon Ruchlamer has supplemented the planned funding with dlrs It said the investment package is subject to shareholder approval and will be presented to its adjourned shareholder meeting on March Noel said proceeds will be used for additional working capital and expanding its factory in Kingston Jamaica It said the debentures which will be priced at pct will have interest payable semi annually and be convertible into common after April at seven dlrs a share Each warrant will be exercisable after April at dlrs a share the company added It said holders of pct of the units may request one registration by the company kof the underlying common shares any time after Jan Holders of the debentures and warrants will also have piggyback registration rights Reuter
