MACMILLAN BLOEDEL MMBLF RAISES NEWSPRINT PRICE VANCOUVER British Columbia March MacMillan Bloedel Ltd said it increased its current newsprint list price for customers in the United States by U S dlrs a tonne effective July Standard white pound newsprint sold through sales unit Powell River Alberni Sales Corp will increase to U S dlrs a tonne from U S dlrs a ton MacMillan Bloedel said It also said a five pct temporary competitive allowance introduced on Dec will be reduced to three pct while a five pct contract performance incentive program introduced July will remain in effect MacMillan Bloedel said customers who annually purchase pct against their contract will receive the five pct discount under the contract performance incentive program plus the three pct discount under the temporary competitive allowance against the new U S dlr a tonne newsprint price The company said it is restoring margins on newsprint and groundwood printing papers so it can continue expanding and upgrading its pulp and paper facilities Reuter
