MAJOR HOME APPLIANCE SHIPMENTS UP IN FEBRUARY CHICAGO March Shipments of major home appliances in February rose pct to mln up from mln a year ago reported the Association of Home Appliance Manufacturers It said year to date total major appliance shipments rose pct to mln from mln a year earlier It said strong shipment gains in four of the five appliance categories resulted in the unprecedented monthly levels with records set for shipments of refrigerators automatic washers disposers dryers and dishwashers Reuter
