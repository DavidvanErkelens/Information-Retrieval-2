CAMBRIAN AND GENERAL NOTES RELISTED IN LONDON LONDON March Cambrian and General Securities Plc said the London Stock Exchange has restored the listing of its mln dlrs of secured floating rate notes dated at the company s request The notes are fully secured and therefore have a prior claim over Cambrian s assets in the event of other claims arising from litigation it added Dealings in Cambrian s securities have been suspended since late following the disclosure of insider trading activities by former chairman Ivan Boesky Cambrian officials were not immediately available for further comment on today s statement REUTER
