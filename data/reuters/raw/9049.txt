NASD TO BEGIN SEARCH FOR NEW PRESIDENT NEW YORK March The National Association of Securities Dealers which will launch a search for a new president later this week will probably concentrate on candidates with an extensive background in the financial markets officials of the organization said today Gordon Macklin who has served as president for years today announced his decision to leave the organization of broker dealers Macklin will join Hambrecht and Quist as chairman and co chief executive officer Association chairman Joseph Hardiman will appoint a search committee later this week The committee will have its first meeting as early as next week The new president is expected to be someone knowledgable about the over the counter markets and committed to the concept of self regulation Macklin joined the association as president in leaving a position at McDonald and Co The National Association of Securities Dealers Automated Quotations System which currently lists securities of companies was ushered in months after Macklin s arrival Reuter
