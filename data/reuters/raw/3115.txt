KINGDOM OF SWEDEN ISSUES MLN STG BOND LONDON March Kingdom of Sweden is issuing a mln stg bond due April carrying a coupon of pct and priced at said Baring Brothers and Co Ltd as lead manager The issue is payable on April The bond is callable after seven years at pct and it will be listed on the London Stock Exchange Fees are a pct selling concession and pct combined management and underwriting fee REUTER
