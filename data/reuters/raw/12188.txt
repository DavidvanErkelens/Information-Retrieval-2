TOPPS CO INC FILES FOR INITIAL OFFERING NEW YORK April Topps Co Inc said it has filed for an initial public offering of common shares including shares to be sold by shareholders It said lead underwriters are Goldman Sachs and Co and Alex Brown and Sons Inc ABSB and the underwriting group has been granted an option to buy up to more shares to cover overallotments The initial offering price is expected to be to dlrs a share and proceeds will be used for debt reduction it said Topps had been public once before but went private in a leveraged buyout in Reuter
