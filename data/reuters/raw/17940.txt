MITSUBISHI CHEMICAL ISSUES EQUITY WARRANT BOND LONDON June Mitsubishi Chemical Industries Ltd MCIT T is issuing a mln dlr equity warrant eurobond due June with an indicated coupon of pct and par pricing lead manager Yamaichi International Europe Ltd said Final terms for the issue will be set on June The selling concession is pct while management and underwriting each pay pct The issue is available in denominations of dlrs and will be listed in Luxembourg The payment date is June and the warrants are exercisable from July until June REUTER
