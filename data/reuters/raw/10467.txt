WEYERHAEUSER WY OFFERS PREFERRED SHARES NEW YORK March Weyerhaeuser Co is offering mln dlrs of convertible exchangeable preferred stock at dlrs a share said Morgan Stanley and Co underwriter Morgan Stanley said each share of preferred may be converted at any time into share of the company s common stock Morgan Stanley said Weyerhaeuser may exchange the preferred on any dividend date beginning June for its pct convertible subordinated debentures due Weyerhaeuser will use proceeds from the offering to reduce outstanding commercial paper Morgan Stanley said the Weyerhaeuser preferred stock will not be redeemable prior to June unless the closing price of the common stock reaches or exceeds pct of the then effective conversion price for at least trading days within consecutive trading days that end within five trading days prior to the notice of redemption The redemption price will be dlrs a share declining to dlrs a share on or after June plus accrued and unpaid dividends the underwriter said Reuter
