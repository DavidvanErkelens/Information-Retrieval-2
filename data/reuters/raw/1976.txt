MATSUSHITA ELECTRIC TRADING ISSUES WARRANT BOND LONDON March Matushita Electric Trading Co Ltd is issuing a mln dlr equity warrant eurobond due March paying an indicated coupon of pct and priced at par lead manager Nomura International Ltd said Final terms on the non callable bond will be fixed on March The selling concession is pct while management and underwriting combined pays pct The payment date is March while listing will be in Luxembourg The issue is available in denominations of dlrs The warrant exercise period is from April until March REUTER
