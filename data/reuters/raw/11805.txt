CNT ISSUES BILLION YEN EUROBOND LONDON April Caisse Nationale des Telecommunications is issuing a billion yen eurobond due May paying pct and priced at pct lead manager IBJ International Ltd said The non callable bond is available in denominations of one mln yen and is guaranteed by France The selling concession is pct while management and underwriting combined pays pct The payment date is May and the issue will be listed in Luxembourg REUTER
