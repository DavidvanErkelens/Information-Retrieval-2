USX X UNIT RAISES SOME STEEL PRICES PITTSBURGH March USX Corp said its USS steelmaking division increased prices on plate and H pile products Effective with May three shipments it said the price base on carbon and high strength low alloy or HSLA H piles will increase by cts per pound Effective with shipments beginning June the price on carbon HSLA and alloy plates will increase by cts per pound Strip mill plate prices will rise by cts per pound It said the price increases on plate and H pile products will apply to all shipments from USS plants Reuter
