DENMARK IN MLN CROWN EUROBOND DEAL COPENHAGEN March One Luxembourg and three Danish banks are tapping the Euro Danish crown market in a three tranche zero coupon eurobond deal of mln Danish crowns nominal for Denmark senior vice president Lars Thuesen of Privatbanken which is the lead manager told Reuters The three mln crown tranches are tranche A due May priced at tranche B due May priced at and tranche C due May priced Fees are pct for selling and pct for management and underwriting combined with pct praecipium The payment date was given as April with denominations at crowns and listing in Luxembourg The co leaders in the new Danish eurobond deal are Copenhagen Handelsbank Den Danske Bank and Kredietbank Luxembourg Thuesen added REUTER
