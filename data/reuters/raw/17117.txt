U S MINT ANNOUNCES COPPER NICKEL AWARDS WASHINGTON April The U S Mint said it awarded contracts to Philipp Brothers N Y and Sidney Danziger a New York metals merchant to procure lbs of electrolytic copper and lbs electrolytic cut nickel cathodes or briquettes The Mint said Philipp Brothers will supply the entire lbs of copper at a cost of dlrs per lb Sidney Danziger will furnish lbs of the nickel at dlrs per lb while Phibro will provide lbs at dlrs per lb the Mint said Reuter
