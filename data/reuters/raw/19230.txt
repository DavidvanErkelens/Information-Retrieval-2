DIGICON DGC STARTS EXCHANGE OFFER FOR DEBT HOUSTON June Digicon Inc said it started an offer to exchange a package of new securities for all dlrs of its outstanding subordinated debentures The exchange includes its pct senior subordinated notes pct convertible subordinated debentures and the pct convertible subordinated debentures of Digicon Finance N V It said the new securities to be issued in exchange offers include up to dlrs of Digicon s pct senior subordinated notes due up to dlrs of Digicon pct convertible subordinated debentures due and up to shares of Digicon common Digicon said in addition to the new notes new debentures and common stock it also will issue to exchangeing holders up to dlrs of contingently extinguishable debt certificates It said the exchange offers are being made only by prospectus Reuter
