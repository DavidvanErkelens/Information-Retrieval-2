GERMAN ENGINEERS WILL HOLD WARNING STRIKES MARCH FRANKFURT March West German engineering union IG Metall will hold nationwide warning strikes on March to press demands for a hour week and a five pct wage increase a union spokeswoman said Further details of the time and place of the strikes will be given at a press conference on the same day she added In mid February IG Metall chairman Franz Steinkuehler called for the strikes after unsuccessful rounds of talks IG Metall shut down the entire auto industry for seven weeks in summer to press employers to cut the working week to hours from A compromise of hours was agreed REUTER
