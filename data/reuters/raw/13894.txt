WORLD BANK LOAN RAISED TO MLN GUILDERS AMSTERDAM April A pct year loan for the World Bank originally mln guilders has been raised by mln guilders at an issue price of pct lead manager Algemene Bank Nederland NV said Subscriptions for this second tranche close April and payment date is April Redemption is on March the same date as the first tranche which was also issued at pct Complete details on the syndicate were not available Amsterdam Rotterdam Bank NV is co lead and a number of Dutch and British banks will complete the syndicate REUTER
