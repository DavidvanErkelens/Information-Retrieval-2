COMPUTER TERMINAL SYSTEMS CPML COMPLETES SALE COMMACK N Y Feb Computer Terminal Systems Inc said it has completed the sale of shares of its common stock and warrants to acquire an additional one mln shares to Sedio N V of Lugano Switzerland for dlrs The company said the warrants are exercisable for five years at a purchase price of dlrs per share Computer Terminal said Sedio also has the right to buy additional shares and increase its total holdings up to pct of the Computer Terminal s outstanding common stock under certain circumstances involving change of control at the company The company said if the conditions occur the warrants would be exercisable at a price equal to pct of its common stock s market price at the time not to exceed dlrs per share Computer Terminal also said it sold the technolgy rights to its Dot Matrix impact technology including any future improvements to Woodco Inc of Houston Tex for dlrs But it said it would continue to be the exclusive worldwide licensee of the technology for Woodco The company said the moves were part of its reorganization plan and would help pay current operation costs and ensure product delivery Computer Terminal makes computer generated labels forms tags and ticket printers and terminals Reuter
