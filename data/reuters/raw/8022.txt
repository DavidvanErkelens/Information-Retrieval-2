ROGERS ROG ADOPTS RIGHTS PLAN ROGERS Conn March Rogers Corp said its board approved a shareholder rights plan designed to protect its shareholders in the event of an attempted hostile takeover Rogers said the plan is not being adopted in response to any specific takeover attempt Under the plan shareholders may buy one share of common stock at dlrs for each share held The rights will be exercisable only if a person or group acquires pct or more of Rogers shares or announces an offer for pct or more The dividend distribution will be made March to holders or record on that date Reuter
