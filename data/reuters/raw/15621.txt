CREDIT NATIONAL ISSUES BILLION FRANC CONVERTIBLE PARIS April Credit National is issuing a one billion franc convertible bond issue in two tranches both paying pct and in franc units lead managers Caisse Nationale de Credit Agricole Union de Garantie et de Placement and Morgan et Cie said Both tranches will be redeemable at the end of their lives and both have a payment date of April One seven year tranche will be priced at pct Each bond will be convertible on a one for one basis for a year pct bond priced at pct redeemable at the end of its life This bond will pay a first coupon of pct on April next year and the payment date will be October A year tranche priced at pct will also be convertible until October on a one for one basis for a pct year year bond priced at pct This bond will pay a first coupon of francs on October next year and the payment date will be October this year REUTER
