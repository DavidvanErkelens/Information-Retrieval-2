FLUOR CORP FLR WINS MLN DLR ARMY CONTRACT IRVINE Calif March Fluor Corp said it won a mln dlr contract from the U S Army Engineer District with a follow on option for an additional mln dlrs Fluor s Fluor Constructors Inc unit will perform procurement and construction for Ground Based Free Electron Laser Technology Integrated Experiment Project at the White Sands Missile Range in New Mexico Phase I Construction will begin this month and is scheduled for completion in September Based on its results a Phase II follow on option may be implemented for an additional two years the company said Reuter
