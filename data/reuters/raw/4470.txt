ANHEUSER BUSCH BUD DEBENTURES ISSUED ST LOUIS March Anheuser Busch Cos Inc said it will issue mln dlrs in principal amount of pct sinking fund debentures due March It said the offering will be through an underwriting syndicate managed by Dillon Read and Co Inc They debentures will be offered at pct of the face amount to yield pct to maturity It said the debentures are being issued under a previously announced shelf registration Reuter
