AIRCOA AIRC FORMS LIMITED PARTNERSHIP DENVER March AIRCOA said it formed AIRCOA Hotel Partners LP a limited partnership formed to acquire and operate hotel and resort properties AIRCOA said it holds a one pct general partner interest in the partnership The remaining pct interest is held by AIRCOA affiliates it said AIRCOA said it plans to file a registration statement for the public offering of limited partnership interests Net proceeds would be used to refinance the partnerships unsecured debt and for capital improvements to hotels it said AIRCOA said the partnership bought seven hotel and resort properties from its affiliates It said financing for the acquisitions was provided by a mln dlr mortgage loan from Bankers Trust Co and Cassa Di Risparmio Di Torino of New York The partnership also received mln dlrs of unsecured loans from a group of institutional lenders and mln dlrs in subordinated debt from AIRCOA the company said Reuter
