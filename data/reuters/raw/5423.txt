BFCE ISSUES THREE BILLION FRANC DOMESTIC BOND PARIS March Banque Francaise du Commerce Exterieur is issuing a three billion franc pct years and days domestic bond with warrants at an issue price of pct lead manager Banque Nationale de Paris said The bonds of a nominal francs are identical to those of the BFCE five billion franc pct January issue bringing the total of the issue to eight billion francs Interest will be paid for the first time on February and on the same date of each subsequent year Redemption will be in three tranches on February and Each bond will carry a warrant and two warrants will give the right to subscribe to an pct nominal bond between December and January The exercise price of the subscription warrants will be pct BNP said that if all the warrants were utilised the total of the January issue would rise to billion francs REUTER
