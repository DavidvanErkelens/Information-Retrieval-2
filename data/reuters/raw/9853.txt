ESSEX CHEMICAL ESX SELLS CONVERTIBLE DEBT NEW YORK March Essex Chemical Corp is raising mln dlrs via an offering of convertible subordinated debentures due with a six pct coupon and par pricing said lead manager Thomson McKinnon Securities Inc The debentures are convertible into the company s common stock at dlrs per share representing a premium of pct over the stock price when terms on the debt were set Non callable for two years the issue is rated B by Moody s Investors Service Inc and B by Standard and Poor s Corp PaineWebber Inc co managed the deal Reuter
