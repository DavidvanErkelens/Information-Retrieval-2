XYVISION ISSUES CONVERTIBLE EUROBOND LONDON April XYVision Inc is issuing a mln dlr convertible eurobond due May paying an indicated coupon of between and six pct and priced at par lead manager Credit Suisse First Boston Ltd said The issue is callable after days at pct declining by one pct per annum to par thereafter It is not callable for three years unless the conversion price exceeds the stock price by pct The selling concession is pct while management and underwriting each pay pct Final terms will be set on or before April The issue is available in bearer and registered form in denominations of dlrs It will be listed in Luxembourg while the payment date is May REUTER
