ACS SYSTEMS COMPLETES INITIAL PUBLIC OFFERING HERDON Va March ACS Systems Inc said it has completed an initial public offering of units consisting of one common share and one warrant per unit ACS said two warrants entitle a holder to purchase one share of common stock at dlrs a share exercisable days from the effective date for a month period The company said total gross proceeds from the offering were dlrs and will be used for general corporate purposes ACS said the units shares and warrants currently are traded over the counter but the company will apply to list them on the NASDAQ system in the next few days Reuter
