FIRST INTERSTATE BANCORP I FILES DEBT OFFER LOS ANGELES March First Interstate Bancorp said it has filed a shelf registration with the Securities and Exchange Commission for up to billion dlrs principal amount of its debt securities The filing consists of billion dlrs of senior debt securities and mln dlrs of subordinated debt securities including capital securities The securities may be offered from time to time through underwriters or by the company directly or through agents it said First Interstate said net proceeds would be used principally to fund investments in or extensions of credit to the company s subsidiaries or to repay loans incurred for such purposes It called the filing part of the company s regularly scheduled funding activities First Interstate said the filing brings to billion dlrs the total amount of debt securities covered under this and eight earlier shelf registrations Reuter
