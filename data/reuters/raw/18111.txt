TANDY TAN DEBT MAY BE UPGRADED BY MOODY S NEW YORK June Moody s Investors Service Inc said it may upgrade Tandy Corp s mln dlrs of long term debt The agency said the review would focus on Tandy s capacity to sustain its current earnings recovery and on the company s ability to further improve its cash flow performance by greater operating efficiencies Moody s said it would also evaluate Tandy s capital structure That structure has changed significantly as a result of the retirement of about mln dlrs of debt in the past year Under review are Tandy s A senior debt and Baa subordinated debt Reuter
