TANZANIA WANTS TO EXPORT TONNES OF MAIZE DAR ES SALAAM March Tanzania seeks to export a surplus of tonnes of maize from last year s bumper harvest agriculture minister Paul Bomani said yesterday The maize crop was officially estimated at mln tonnes but only a fraction of this was marketed with most grain consumed by the farmers who grew it The state owned National Milling Corp NMC meanwhile said it is trying to sell tonnes of maize stored in different parts of the country NMC acting general manager John Rubibira said Tanzania has only tonnes of silo storage capacity concentrated in Dar es Salaam Arusha in the north and Iringa in central Tanzania In addition the country has tonnes of flat storage capacity he added Rubibira said the government is planning to build new silos in the main maize producing areas of Iringa Mbeya Ruvuma and Rukwa REUTER
