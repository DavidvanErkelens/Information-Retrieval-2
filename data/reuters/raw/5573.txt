U S TURKEY SIGN AID AGREEMENT WASHINGTON March The United States and Turkey signed a five year defence and economic agreement DECA that will channel hundreds of millions of dollars a year in aid to Ankara The agreement was signed by Secretary of State George Shultz and Foreign Minister Vahit Halefoglu after months of negotiations that won Ankara considerably less than it wanted Under the agreement the U S government pledged it would use vigor and determination to persuade Congress to vote the money to build up Turkish defenses in return for U S air bases and intelligence gathering facilities Under today s agreement which runs until December Turkey will receive nearly mln dlrs in military and economic aid this year Next year the U S government has promised to ask Congress for mln dlrs under an agreement that prompted domestic crticism in Turkey for not winning enough aid Reuter
