PENNWALT PSM OFFERS ONE MLN SHARES PHILADELPHIA March Pennwalt Corp said it offered one mln shares of common stock at dlrs a share Pennwalt said it plans to use the net proceeds of the public offering which is managed by Goldman Sachs and Co to buy all the outstanding shares of its Third Series cumulative convertible preferred stock for about mln dlrs Goldman Sachs was granted an over allotment option of shares the company added Reuter
