THERMO ELECTRON TMO SELLS CONVERTIBLE DEBT NEW YORK April Thermo Electron Corp is raising mln dlrs via an offering of convertible subordinated debentures due with a pct coupon and par pricing said lead manager Shearson Lehman Brothers Inc The debentures are convertible into the company s stock at dlrs per share representing a premium of pct over the stock price when terms on the debt were set Non callable for two years the issue is rated Ba by Moody s Investors and BB by Standard and Poor s Drexel Burnham and Tucker Anthony co managed the deal Reuter
