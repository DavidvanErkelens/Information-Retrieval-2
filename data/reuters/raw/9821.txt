FRENCH RESERVES FALL ON DEBT REPAYMENT PARIS March French reserves fell in the week ended March following repayment of the bulk of the debt contracted during January with the European Monetary Cooperation Fund the Bank of France said in its weekly statement The repayment of capital and interest on this loan taken out during the strong pressure on the franc which preceded the European Monetary System EMS realignment and the subsequent Group of Five meeting in Paris took place on March It comprised the repayment of billion francs worth of European Currency Units ECUs billion francs worth of foreign currency and billion francs worth of special drawing rights SDRs the Bank said As a result foreign currency reserves fell to billion francs on March from billion on March while ECU reserves fell to billion francs from billion Gold reserves remained stable at billion francs REUTER
