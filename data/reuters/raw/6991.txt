DRG ISSUES MLN STG CONVERTIBLE BOND LONDON March DRG Plc DRGL L is issuing a mln stg convertible eurobond due September with an indicated coupon of six to pct and priced at par lead manager Baring Brothers and Co Ltd said Pricing will take place on March with conversion set at a premium of to pct over the closing price on that day Fees of pct comprise pct for selling and one pct for management and underwriting combined The bonds will be issued in denominations of stg and will be listed in London The pay date is April with a short first coupon J Henry Schroder Wagg and Co Ltd is co lead REUTER
