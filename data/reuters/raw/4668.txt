CBS ISSUES MLN DLRS IN CONVERTIBLE DEBT LONDON March CBS Inc CBS N is issuing mln dlrs of convertible debt due April with a coupon of five pct and priced at par said Morgan Stanley Ltd as lead manager The securities are convertible into shares of CBS at a price of dlrs per share which represents a premium of pct over the company s closing stock price yesterday of dlrs on the New York Stock Exchange The issue is callable in the first three years only if the stock prices rises abouve pct of the conversion level There is a pct selling concession and a one pct combined management and underwriting fee REUTER
