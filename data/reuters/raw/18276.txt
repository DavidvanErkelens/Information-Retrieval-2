CCC ACCEPTS BONUS ON POULTRY TO EGYPT USDA WASHINGTON June The Commodity Credit Corporation CCC has accepted a bid for two bonus offers to cover the sale of tonnes of frozen poultry to Egypt the U S Agriculture Department said The poultry is for shipment November through August and the bonus was dlrs per tonne for the frozen fryer legs The bonuses were made to Gress Foods Inc tonnes and Serva International Ltd tonnes The subsidies will be paid to the exporters in the form of commodities from CCC stocks An additional tonnes of frozen poultry remain available to Egypt under the Export Enhancement Program initiative announced Dec and Feb the department said Reuter
