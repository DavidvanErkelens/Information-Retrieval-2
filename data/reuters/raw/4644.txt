NATIONAL AUSTRALIA BANK PLANS MLN DLR CD ISSUE HONG KONG March The National Australia Bank s Hong Kong branch is planning a mln Australian dlr floating rate certificate of deposit CD issue banking sources said The three year issue which matures April is in two tranches Tranche A of mln dlrs carries interest at basis points below one month bank bill rate payable monthly and tranche B of mln dlrs carries interest at basis points below three month bank bill rate payable quarterly Fee for co managers is pct Lead manager is BT Asia Ltd and syndication is expected to close on March REUTER
