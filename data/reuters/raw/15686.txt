COLOMBIA SECURES BILLION DLRS OF DEBT BOGOTA April Colombia has secured total borrowings this year for billion dlrs of which billion correspond to loans arranged before Director of Public Credit Mauricio Cabrera said Cabrera said about half of the borrowings would come from the World Bank and the Inter American Development Bank He was speaking at a forum for industrialists upon his return from London where he attended the signing of a mln dlr floating rate note Colombia s first such offering on the euromarket Cabrera reaffirmed that Colombia has no intention of rescheduling its foreign debt which he put at billion dlrs of which billion are from the public sector For the international banking community Colombia continues to be the best house in spite of being in the worst neighbourhood he said He said debt servicing will remain a burden in and will amount to pct of total exports REUTER
