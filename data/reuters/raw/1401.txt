BORDEN INC BN TO REDEEM PCT DEBENTURES NEW YORK March Borden Inc called for redemption on March of all its outstanding pct sinking fund debentures due The company said the redemption price is at pct of face value plus dlrs accrued interest from October to the redemption date making for a total of dlrs for each dlrs face value The trustee is Bank of America National Trust and Savings Association in San Francisco and the paying agent is Bankers Trust Co New York Reuter
