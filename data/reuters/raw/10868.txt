WESTINGHOUSE WX TO MOVE POWER GENERATION UNIT PITTSBURGH March Westinghouse Electric Corp said it has relocated its power generation service division headquarters to locations of existing Westinghouse operations in Monroeville Pa and Orlando Fla The company said about of the employees at the Broomall site will be moved to Monroeville and to Orlando with the remaining having their employment terminated It said terminated employees will receive enhanced separation benefits Reuter
