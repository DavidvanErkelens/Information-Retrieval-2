BRAZIL BUYS FRENCH WHEAT AT TENDER RIO DE JANEIRO June Brazil has bought tonnes of French wheat at tender a Brazilian Wheat Board spokesman said He said the Board accepted offers for tonnes of wheat from grain firm J Souffle at dlrs per tonne Fob for August shipment For September shipment the Board bought tonnes from Graniere at dlrs per tonne Fob and for October shipment it accepted tonnes from Andre and Companie at dlrs per tonne Fob The next tender for Aug Sept Oct shipment was set for June the spokesman said Reuter
