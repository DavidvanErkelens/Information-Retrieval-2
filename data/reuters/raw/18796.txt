WORLD BANK LOANS MEXICO MLN DLRS WASHINGTON June The World Bank said it has loaned Mexico mln dlrs to help support an agricultural credit project The project hopes to increase agricultural productivity exports and real farm income by providing credit to private farmers ranchers and agro industries the bank said The loan will be used for lending mainly by commercial banks and the National Rural Credit Bank Mexico s largest agricultural credit lender the bank said It also said the project hopes to aid in reducing the government s interest rate and credit subsidies The project will also support improvement of the agricultural credit system through reduction of subsidies for credit and crop insurance and increased domestic resource mobilization the bank said The loan guaranteed by the government is for years including three years of grace at a variable interest rate currently pct the bank said Reuter
