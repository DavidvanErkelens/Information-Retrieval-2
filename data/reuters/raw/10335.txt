COMMONWEALTH BANK ISSUES DUAL CURRENCY BOND LONDON March Commonwealth Bank of Australia is issuing a billion yen dual currency bond due April with an eight pct coupon and priced at pct Salomon Brothers International said as lead manager The issue is payable and redeemable in Australian dollars although coupon payments will be in yen Upon redemption holders will be repaid at a rate of yen per Australian dollar Salomon said that the current exchange rate is yen to the Australian dollar Payment date is April and the securities are available in denominations of five mln yen They will be listed on the London Stock Exchange There is a pct selling concession and a pct combined management and underwriting fee REUTER
