TEXAS AIR CORP TEX UNIT FILES FOR DEBT OFFER NEW YORK March Continental Airlines Inc a unit of Texas Air Corp said it filed a registration statement with the Securities and Exchange Commission for an offering of mln dlrs of equipment certificates Continental plans to raise mln dlrs through an issue of first priority secured equipment certificates due mln dlrs through second priority certificates due and mln dlrs via third priority certificates due Interest will be payable March and September starting September Lead manager will be Drexel Burnham Lambert Inc It also plans a filing of mln dlrs of year notes Reuter
