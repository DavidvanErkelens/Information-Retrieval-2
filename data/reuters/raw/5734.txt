ZIMMER ZIM BOOSTS CREDIT LINE BY MLN DLRS BOCA RATON Fla March Zimmer Corp said it reached an agreement with its banks to boost its credit line by mln dlrs It currently has dlrs in outstanding debt Under the terms of the accord the banks will receive a security interest of nearly all the company s assets excluding inventory The pact also calls for repayment of the debt over months and the application of cash proceeds from the sale of its Black Fin Yacht Corp unit It said the credit agreement and sale of Black Fin is expected to relieve its liquidity problems Reuter
