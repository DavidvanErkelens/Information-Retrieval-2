KEIHIN ELECTRIC EXPRESS ISSUES MLN DLR BOND LONDON March Keihin Electric Express Railway Co Ltd is issuing a mln dlr eurobond with equity warrants due April with an indicated coupon of pct and priced at par lead manager Yamaichi International Europe Ltd said The non callable bonds are guaranteed by Fuji Bank Ltd Pricing will take place on April and the exercise period is between May and April The indicated premium is pct Total fees of pct comprise pct for selling and pct for management and underwriting The bonds will be issued in dlr denominations and listed in Luxembourg REUTER
