U S DURUM WHEAT BONUS BID TO ALGERIA ACCEPTED WASHINGTON April The U S Agriculture Department said it has accepted a bid for an export bonus to cover the sale of tonnes of U S durum wheat to Algeria A bonus of dlrs per tonne was awarded to Cam USA Inc on the shipment scheduled for June Melvin Sims USDA general sales manager said An additional tonnes of durum wheat are available to Algeria under the department s export enhancement program Sims said Reuter
