AANCOR UNIT FILES AMENDED REGISTRATION STATEMENT NEW YORK March National Gypsum Co a unit of Aancor Holdings Inc said it filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission an amended registration statement covering up to mln dlrs of year priority senior subordinated notes It previously filed a statement covering up to mln dlrs of such notes National Gypsum said The notes are part of a refinancing plan that includes a new credit agreement with domestic and foreign banks for mln dlrs Proceeds from the new notes and bank facility will be used to terminate the company s current revolving credit facility by repaying the approximate mln dlr balance it said A total of mln dlrs was originally drawn down under the revolving credit facility on April when National Gypsum was acquired by a management led investors group in a leveraged buyout the company said National Gypsum said that the new credit agreement will convert in to a term loan that will be amortized over four years It said the new notes will rank senior to the company s existing pct senior subordinated debentures of and pct discount debentures due Reuter
