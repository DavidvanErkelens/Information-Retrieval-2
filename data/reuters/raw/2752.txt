MOET HENNESSY ISSUING CONVERTIBLE EUROBOND PARIS March Moet Hennessy MHSP PA is issuing an mln French franc year eurobond with share warrants lead manager Lazard Freres et Cie said The issue will be priced at par with a one pct coupon and each franc bond will carry warrants each giving the right to subscribe to Moet Hennessy shares over a three year period at francs each The high equity content of the issue accounts for the low coupon Moet Hennessy shares closed on the Paris Bourse today at francs up from yesterday s close The issue is being co lead managed by Credit Lyonnais Banque Nationale de Paris BNP and Credit Suisse First Boston Payment date will be April REUTER
