SCHLESWIG HOLSTEIN ISSUES MLN MARK BOND FRANKFURT March The West German federal state of Schleswig Holstein is issuing a mln mark domestic bond carrying a coupon of six pct and priced at lead manager Landesbank Schleswig Holstein Girozentrale said The eight year offer yields pct to retail investors at issue and will go on sale on March Some mln will be offered for immediate sale and mln will be retained for market regulation The bond matures on April Listing will be on all eight West German stock exchanges REUTER
