LITTON LIT WINS MLN DLR NAVY CONTRACT BEVERLY HILLS Calif March Litton Industries said its Applied Technology division won a mln dlr contract from the U S Navy to produce AN ALR V and AN ALR F airborne threat warning systems The company said the systems will be used on a variety of Navy attack and reconnaissance aircraft Deliveries are set from August this year to February The Navy also has an option in June to increase the contract value to mln dlrs Litton said Reuter
