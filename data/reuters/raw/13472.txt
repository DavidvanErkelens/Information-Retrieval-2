SHOWA ALUMINIUM ISSUES MLN DLR WARRANT BOND LONDON April Showa Aluminium Corp is issuing a mln dlr equity warrant eurobond due April paying an indicated coupon of pct and priced at par lead manager Nomura International Ltd said Final terms will be fixed on April The selling concession is pct while management and underwriting combined pays pct The non callable bond is available in denominations of dlrs and will be listed in Luxembourg The payment date is April REUTER
