HYPONEX HYPX SELLS DEBENTURES AT PCT NEW YORK March Hyponex Corp is raising mln dlrs through an offering of senior subordinated debentures due yielding pct said sole underwriter Drexel Burnham Lambert Inc The debentures have an pct coupon and were priced at Drexel said The issue is non callable for five years There were no ratings by Moody s Investors Service Inc or Standard and Poor s Corp at the time of the pricing Drexel said Hyponex said proceeds will be used mainly to finance acquisitions of assets and businesses Reuter
