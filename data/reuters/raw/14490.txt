HONDURAS SEEKING PL VESSELS FOR BULK WHEAT WASHINGTON April Honduras will tender April under Public Law for U S and non U S flag vessels to deliver approximately tonnes of various wheats in bulk an agent for the country said The agent said deliveries of northern spring dns wheat will include laydays of July for tonnes August for tonnes and September for tonnes Deliveries of hard red winter wheat will have laydays of June for tonnes July for tonnes and September for tonnes Offers are due by hrs EDT April and will remain valid until the close of business April the agent said Reuter
