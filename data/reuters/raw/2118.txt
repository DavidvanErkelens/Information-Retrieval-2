WORLD BANK ISSUES MLN U S DLR FINANCE PACKAGE FRANKFURT March The World Bank is issuing a multicurrency financing package totalling mln U S Dlrs coordinator DG Bank Deutsche Genossenschaftsbank said The package includes a mln mark five year straight eurobond with a pct coupon priced at par led by DG The bond which is a private placement matures on March It will be listed in Frankfurt beginning in May with no precise date yet specified Denominations are and marks Details of fees were not immediately available The package also includes a five year mln guilder eurobond with a pct coupon priced at par The Dutch guilder issue will be led by Rabobank Nederland The third bond in the package is a seven year five billion yen bond with a pct coupon priced at par led by The Norinchukin Bank The package also includes a mln Swiss franc year credit agreement with interest at pct underwritten by Swiss Volksbank DG said REUTER
