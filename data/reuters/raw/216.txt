JAPAN MARCH BOND COUPON SEEN UNCHANGED AT FIVE PCT TOKYO Feb The Finance Ministry has proposed informally to its bond underwriting syndicate that the coupon on the January year government bond remain unchanged from February at five pct underwriting sources said They said the ministry wants a yen raise in issue price from February to to yield a record low pct The pct February bond issue yield was itself a record low The proposed issue volume is billion yen against the billion in February The underwriting syndicate is likely to accept the proposed terms immediately the sources said REUTER
