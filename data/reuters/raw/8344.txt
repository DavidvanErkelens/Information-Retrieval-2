HAWKEYE BANCORP HWKB EXCHANGE OFFER EXPIRES DES MOINES March Hawkeye Bancorp said its exchange offer to holders of its preferred stock expired and a total of shares of preferred had been tendered Hawekeye said it expects to accept for exchange all shares tendered It said the exchange ratio would be shares of common for each share of preferred stock tendered As a result Hawkeye would have outstanding shares of common stock and shares of preferred which are convertible into additional common shares Upon acceptance of the shares Hawkeye said it would eliminate outstanding preferred shares having dividend arrearages totaling dlrs and a liquidation preference of dlrs It said the exchange offer is a part of Hawkeye s restructuring in accordance with its previously announced debt restructuring agreement Reuter
