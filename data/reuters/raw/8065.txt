MALAWI INTRODUCES NEW TAXES TO BOOST REVENUE ZOMBA March Malawi will impose a pct border tax on some overseas remittances and a five pct profit tax for local branches of foreign firms to boost revenue and meet a projected budget deficit of mln kwacha the Malawi news agency reported Finance Minister Dalton Katopola presenting the new budget said other measures would include raising surtax on goods and services by five points to pct and imposing duty on luxury goods and tax on statutory bodies such as the Water Board Malawi s economy was expected to grow pct this year he said REUTER
