CCC ACCEPTS BIDS ON BONUS WHEAT TO ALGERIA USDA WASHINGTON March The Commodity Credit Corporation CCC has accepted bids for export bonuses on tonnes of durum wheat to Algeria the U S Agriculture Department said The department said the bonuses awarded averaged dlrs per tonne and will be paid to exporters in the form of commodities from CCC inventories The bonuses were made to Cam USA Inc the department said The wheat is for shipment May An additional tonnes of durum wheat are still avaiable to Algeria under the Export Enhancement program initiative announced on March Reuter
