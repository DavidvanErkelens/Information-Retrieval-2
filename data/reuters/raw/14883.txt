DANSK NATURGAS ISSUES BILLION YEN EUROBOND LONDON April Dansk Naturgas A S is issuing a billion yen eurobond due May paying pct and priced at pct lead manager IBJ International Ltd said The non callable bond is available in denominations of one mln yen and will be listed in Luxembourg The selling concession is pct while management and underwriting combined pays pct The issue is guaranteed by Denmark The payment date is May REUTER
