CCC CREDIT GUARANTEES FOR RICE TO ALGERIA USDA WASHINGTON April The Commodity Credit Corporation CCC has authorized mln dlrs in credit guarantees for sales of U S rice to Algeria for fiscal year under the Export Credit Guarantee Program GSM the U S Agriculture Department said The additional guarantees increase the cumulative fiscal year program for sales of U S agricultural products to Algeria to mln dlrs To be eligible for the credit guarantee coverage all sales under the new line must be registered and exported by September the department aid Reuter
