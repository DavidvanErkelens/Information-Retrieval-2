COMMONWEALTH REALTY CRTYZ IN REFINANCING DEAL PRINCETON N J March Commonwealth Realty Trust said it has transferred its ownership of six buildings at the Valley Forge Executive Mall in Valley Forge Pa to an equally owned joint venture with Pennsylvania Public School Employees Retirement System as part of a refinancing The trust said it received five mln dlrs for its half interest in the buildings It said it also received a to mln dlr eight year pct mortgage loan on the buildings with the entire principal payable at the end of the term After associated costs it said it realized net proceeds of about mln dlrs in cash on the transactions mln dlrs of which it will use to repay debt under its credit lines Reuter
