DRESSER INDUSTRIES DI LOSES PATENT APPEAL DALLAS April Dresser Industries Inc said the U S Court of Appeals ruled against it in its appeal of a judgment in favor of Hughes Tool Co HT concerning a patent infringement The case alleged that Dresser infringed on a Hughes patent regarding a drill bit sealing device the company said However Dresser said the Appellate Court set aside the royalty rate of pct on selected bits as being arbitarary and returned the case to the district court level to ascertain a new rate Reuter
