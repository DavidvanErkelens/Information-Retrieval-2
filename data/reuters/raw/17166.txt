USDA ANNOUNCES EXPORT BONUS WHEAT TO ALGERIA WASHINGTON April The U S Agriculture Department announced it accepted three bids from two exporters for export bonuses to cover sales of tonnes of durum wheat to Algeria USDA said the bonuses awarded were dlrs per tonne to be paid in the form of commodities from CCC inventory The bonus awards were made to Cam USA Inc tonnes and Corprostate Inc tonnes Shipment is scheduled for June An additional tonnes of durum wheat are still available to Algeria under the export enhancement program Reuter
