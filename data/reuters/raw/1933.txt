INDIA AND JAPAN TO DISCUSS IRON ORE PRICES NEW DELHI March The state owned Minerals and Metals Trading Corp will send a team to Japan next week to negotiate an iron ore export contract for beginning April trade sources said Japan the biggest buyer of Indian iron ore with imports of around mln tonnes a year has asked India to reduce prices from the current average of dlrs a tonne the sources said Japan has said it may be forced to reduce ore imports from India next year if New Delhi fails to reduce the price one source said but declined to give further details REUTER
