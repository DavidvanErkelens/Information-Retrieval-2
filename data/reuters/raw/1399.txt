ARVIN INDUSTRIES ARV TO SELL MLN DLRS DEBT NEW YORK March Arvin Industries Inc said it is raising mln dlrs through an offering of pct year notes and pct year sinking fund debentures Lead underwriter for the offerings both of mln dlrs is Merrill Lynch Capital Markets The notes were priced at pct to yield pct and the debentures were priced at pct to yield pct The notes are redeemable at par at Arvin s option after March The debentures are redeemable at any time but non refundable before March Both are rated BAA by Moody s Investors Service and BBB by Standard and Poor s Reuter
