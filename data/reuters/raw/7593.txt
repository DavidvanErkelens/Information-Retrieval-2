REBEL AMBUSH KILLS SOLDIERS IN PHILIPPINES MANILA March Communist rebels killed soldiers on Mindanao island in the southern Philippines the army said Military officials said about soldiers on foot patrol were attacked by guerrillas on the border area around Misamis Occidental and Davao del Sur provinces The state run Philippine News Agency said at least rebels died in the clash but the military officials could not confirm the report On Tuesday soldiers were killed in Quezon province in the northern island of Luzon after land mines blew up an armoured vehicle and a troop carrying truck REUTER
