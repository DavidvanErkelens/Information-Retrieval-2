OUTBOARD MARINE OM SELLS PCT DEBENTURES NEW YORK March Outboard Marine Corp is raising mln dlrs via an offering of sinking fund debentures due yielding pct said lead manager Salomon Brothers Inc The debentures have a pct coupon and were priced at to yield basis points over comparable Treasury bonds Morgan Stanley co managed the deal The issue is non refundable for years A sinking fund beginning in to retire annually five pct of the debt may be increased by pct at the company s option giving it an estimated minimum life of years and maximum of years The debt is rated Baa by Moody s and BBB plus by S P Reuter
