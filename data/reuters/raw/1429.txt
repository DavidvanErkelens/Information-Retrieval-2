AUSTIN ROVER GROUP REPORTS SALES ON STERLINGS MIAMI March Great Britain s Austin Rover Group reported the sale of Sterling automobiles during the second half of February the first month in which the European luxury car was on sale The company said the Sterling S sells for dlrs and the Sterling SL sells for dlrs and are aimed at the European luxury sedan market The Sterling was designed in a joint venture with Honda Motor Corp HMC The Rover Group was the former British Leland PLC company according to the company Reuter
