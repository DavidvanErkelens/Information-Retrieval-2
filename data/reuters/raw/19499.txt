JAPAN TO RATIFY INTERNATIONAL COCOA AGREEMENT TOKYO June Japan will ratify the International Cocoa Agreement ICCA with effect from July and will renew its membership in the International Cocoa Organisation ICCO Foreign Affairs Ministry officials said They said Japan would participate in an ICCO meeting on July in London to revise ICCO buffer stock policy The ICCA has been in effect since January Government approval to participate in the ICCO was delayed by parliamentary debate over the sales tax issue the officials said REUTER
