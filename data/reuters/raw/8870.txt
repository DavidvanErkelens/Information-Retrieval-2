FRANCE S CNE ISSUES MLN FRANC DOMESTIC BOND PARIS March Caisse Nationale de l Energie is issuing a mln franc pct bond due October at par lead managers Banque National de Paris and Union de Garantie et de Placement said Societe Marseillaise de Credit is co lead The issue announced earlier today by the French bond issuing committee is of franc nominal bonds The first coupon of francs will be paid on October and thereafter on April each year Each bond is accompanied by a warrant giving the right to subscribe from September to to a new CNE pct bond due October with a par issue price REUTER
