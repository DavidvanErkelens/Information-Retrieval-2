BNL UNIT ISSUES BILLION YEN EUROBOND LONDON April A unit of Banca Nazionale del Lavoro is issuing a billion yen eurobond due May paying pct and priced at pct market sources said The joint bookrunners are Tokai International and Daiwa Europe Ltd The formal name of the borrower is Banca Nazionale del Lavoro London Branch Through the Law Debenture Corporation Plc The non callable bond is available in denominations of one mln yen and will be listed in Luxembourg The selling concession is pct while management and underwriting combined pays pct The payment date is May and there will be a long first coupon period REUTER
