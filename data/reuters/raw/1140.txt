DEUTSCHE BANK UNIT ISSUES MLN STG EUROBOND LONDON March Deutsche Bank Finance NV Curacao is issuing a mln stg eurobond due April paying pct and priced at pct joint lead manager Kleinwort Benson Ltd said Deutsche Bank Capital Markets is the other joint lead manager The issue is guaranteed by Deutsche Bank AG and is callable at pct after seven years declining by point per annum to par thereafter The selling concession is pct while management and underwriting combined pays pct The issue is available in denominations of and stg and will be listed in Luxembourg The payment date is April REUTER
