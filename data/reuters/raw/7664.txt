GUILFORD MILLS GFD SELLS CONVERTIBLE DEBT NEW YORK March Guilford Mills Inc is raising mln dlrs via an offering of convertible subordinated debentures due with a six pct coupon and par pricing said sole manager Bear Stearns and Co The debentures are convertible into the company s common stock at dlrs per share representing a premium of pct over the stock price when terms on the debt were set Non callable for two years the debt is rated Ba by Moody s and BBB minus by Standard and Poor s The issue was increased from an initial offering of mln dlrs because of investor demand Reuter
