VENEZUELA S BIGGEST PRIVATE DEBTOR FACES DEFAULT CARACAS March Venezuela s biggest private sector company La Electricidad de Caracas will meet urgently with bank creditors in New York on monday to discuss the danger of a total default on its mln dlr foreign debt a company spokesman said We are in an impossible situation unless we get government relief the company will be bankrupt in three years he told reuters La Electricidad s problems stem from the government s decision in december to set private debt payments at bolivars per dlr instead of adding pct to the debt in bolivar terms La Electricidad is seeking permission for a pct increase in industrial and commercial tariffs which officials say would bring in an extra billion bolivars this year A pct tariff increase introduced in january will only bring in an extra mln bolivars per year as against additional debt costs of mln Reuter
