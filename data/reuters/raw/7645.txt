QINTEX SAYS PRINCEVILLE PVDC IN CREDIT DEAL NEW YORK March Qintex Ltd of Brisbane said it has arranged for a leading Australian financial institution it did not name to provide Princeville Development Corp with the letter of credit required by the terms of Qintex s proposed acquisition of Princeville The company also said through yesterday when its tender offer for Princeville shares was to have expired it had received over seven mln Princeville shares in the bid which has been extended until March at EST Qintex said the letter of credit is to insure payment of Princeville s contingent subordinated notes that would be distributed to shareholders of record on the date immediately following completion of the tender offer The company said it believes the conditions of the commitment letter will be satisfactory to Princeville It said Princeville had previously held talks with a financial institution on obtaining a letter of credit but was unable to reach mutually acceptable terms Reuter
