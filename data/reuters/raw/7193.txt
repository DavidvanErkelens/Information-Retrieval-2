ALCATEL CIT WINS SWEDISH MILITARY CONTRACT PARIS March Alcatel Cit ALCP PA the newly established French subsidiary of Alcatel NV which now runs the worldwide telecommunications activities of ITT Corp ITT N has won a mln franc contract from the Swedish armed forces for transmission equipment Alcatel said in a communique here The order is for multiplexing equipment and falls within the framework of a mln franc sales contract obtained by Alcatel in it added Alcatel NV is a joint venture between ITT and France s state owned Cie Generale d Electricite CGE REUTER
