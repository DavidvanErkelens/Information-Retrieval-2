JUDGEMENT ENTERED AGAINST ARISTECH ARS PITTSBURGH June Aristech Chemical Corp said a judgment was entered against it in Texas State Court for nine mln dlrs plus prejudgment interest of dlrs and legals fees of three mln dlrs It said the judgment arose from a suit filed by Union Pacific Corp UNP against USX Corp X Aristech s former parent in claiming damages for an alleged breach of a cumene purchasing contract by Aristech s predecessor the USS Chemicals division of USX which was then known as U S Steel Corp The company said it considers the judgment unjustified and it will seek to have it overturned Reuter
