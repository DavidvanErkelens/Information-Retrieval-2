TESCO ISSUES MLN STG CONVERTIBLE BOND LONDON March Tesco Plc TSCO L is issuing a mln stg convertible eurobond due February paying an indicated coupon of four to pct and priced at par lead manager Credit Suisse First Boston Ltd said The issue is callable after days at pct declining by one pct per annum to par thereafter It is not callable until unless the share price exceeds pct of the conversion price Final terms will be set on or before March The deal has an investor put option after five years which will be priced to give the investor an annual yield to the put of to nine pct The selling concession is pct while management and underwriting each pay pct The payment date is March and there will be a short first coupon period The issue is available in denominations of and stg and will be listed in London REUTER
