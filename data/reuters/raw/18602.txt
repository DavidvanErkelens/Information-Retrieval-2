EIB MAKES MLN FRANC LOAN TO PEUGEOT PARIS June The European Investment Bank said it is making a mln franc loan to Peugeot SA PEUP PA to restructure the paint works at its Sochaux car factory in France It said the ten year loan carries interest at pct and follows a mln franc pct ten year loan the bank made to Peugeot last July Work on the paint shop at Sochaux is part of a billion franc operation to modernise the group s factories it added REUTER
