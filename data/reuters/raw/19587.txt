INTERNATIONAL SPOT OIL PRICES FIRM ON OPEC ACCORD By Karen Yates Reuters LONDON June OPEC s weekend decision to cut planned oil output levels for second half has renewed market confidence in the oil price outlook for the rest of the year and boosted international spot prices oil traders said Spot crude prices are now standing cents a barrel higher than at the start of the OPEC meeting last Thursday and could continue their rise in the next few weeks they said The new optimism springs from the agreement reached by OPEC ministers in Vienna on Saturday to fix a July December output ceiling of mln barrels per day bpd At its previous meeting in December OPEC set a provisional mln bpd output limit for the third quarter but proposed a sharp rise to mln in the fourth The ceilings were to back up its newly restored fixed prices of around dlrs a barrel When signs first emerged last Friday that OPEC was contemplating cutting the planned fourth quarter ceiling spot prices of the most widely traded crude oil Britain s Brent Blend surged cents a barrel Traders said the trend continued in a cautious rally this morning in London taking quoted levels up to around dlrs compared with dlrs in the middle of last week Most traders on international oil markets reacted bullishly to news of the final agreement They forecast tight supplies later in the year when demand for oil increases with the onset of the Northern Hemisphere winter and further rises in spot prices There will be a scramble for oil in the third and fourth quarters of this year one trader said In the past three months we have already seen a rise in demand particularly from the U S And I expect to see even higher prices he added Mehdi Varzi analyst with stockbroker Kleinwort Grieveson in London agreed Varzi told Reuters that OPEC was if anything too cautious in setting the new output ceilings There is now a great chance that Brent crude oil will hit dlrs a barrel in the next month he said Laurie Law analyst at E F Hutton and Co Inc in New York said over the weekend that she saw the spot price of West Texas Intermediate WTI rallying to around dlrs by the end of the year from just over dlrs now WTI
