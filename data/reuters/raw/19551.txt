LAND SECURITIES ISSUES MLN STG CONVERTIBLE BOND LONDON June Land Securities Plc LAND L is issuing a mln stg convertible eurobond due December with an indicated coupon of to seven pct and par pricing lead manager J Henry Schroder Wagg Ltd said The issue is callable at pct declining by one pct per annum to par thereafter but is not callable until unless the share price exceeds the conversion price by pct The selling concession is pct while management and underwriting each pays pct Final terms will be set on or before July and the expected conversion premium is to pct The payment date is July The issue is available in denominations of stg and will be listed in London REUTER
