PHILIPS DUPONT UNIT ARRANGES MLN DLR CREDIT LONDON March Philips DuPont Optical Co is arranging a mln dlr three year transferable loan facility Chemical Bank International Ltd said as agent The financing involves a mln dlr loan which is available for drawing in the six months after signing and a mln dlr revolving credit which is available for the life of the credit Drawings under both tranches will be at basis points over the London Interbank Offered Rate There will be a utilisation fee of five basis points on the revolving credit if more than pct is used and a commitment fee of basis points Borrowings will be available in currencies other than dollars and banks have been invited to join as lead managers at mln dlrs for basis points and as managers at mln dlrs for five basis points The borrower is an optical disk joint venture between Philips Gloeilampenfabrieken NV and E I Du Pont de Nemours and Co REUTER
