MORGAN GUARANTY JPM SUES METROMEDIA FOR NOTES NEW YORK March Morgan Guaranty Trust Co said it filed suit against Metromedia Inc seeking a redemption at par of Metromedia subordinated discount debentures The debentures due were issued in and were zero coupon notes for the first five years Beginning they were to receive semi annual interest payments at an annual rate of pct Morgan said the complaint filed in U S District Court for the Southern District of New York stems from the recent voluntary liquidation of Metromedia which has intentionally created an event of default on the notes The company said the default amount is dlrs for each dlrs principal amount of debentures The figure takes into account the deep discount the holders received when they bought the issue Morgan Guaranty said there is about mln dlrs principal outstanding Reuter
