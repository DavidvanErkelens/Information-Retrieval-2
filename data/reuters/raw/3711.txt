ANITEC IMAGE ANTC SELLS CONVERTIBLE DEBENTURES NEW YORK March Anitec Image Technology Corp is raising mln dlrs via an issue of convertible subordinated debentures due with a pct coupon and par pricing said lead manager Smith Barney Harris Upham and Co Inc The debentures are convertible into the company s common stock at dlrs a share representing a pct premium over the stock price when terms on the debt were set Non callable for two years the debentures are rated B by Moody s and B by Standard and Poor s Donaldson Lufkin Salomon Brothers and Mabon Nugent co managed the issue which was increased from an initial offering of mln dlrs Reuter
