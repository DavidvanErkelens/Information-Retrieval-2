BRITAIN GIVES FAMINE RELIEF AID TO MOZAMBIQUE HARARE March Britain has granted Mozambique about eight mln sterling for food aid and emergency relief for famine victims the British High Commission announced A high commission embassy statement said about tonnes of cereals worth six mln sterling would be shipped to Mozambique and the first load of tonnes was due soon Britain will give one mln sterling for other disaster relief programs and mln sterling to relieve the plight of Mozambican refugees it added The statement said Britain would also contribute mln sterling to the U N Refugee agency UNHCR for refugees in neighbouring countries and give sterling to relief agencies for food transport and distribution in Mozambique The British public has also raised more than one mln sterling Reuter
