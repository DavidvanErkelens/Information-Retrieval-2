EC COURT RULES IN FAVOUR OF CHEAP BUTTER PLAN BRUSSELS March The European Court in Luxembourg has ruled a so called Christmas butter scheme under which surplus stocks were sold off cheaply did not infringe European Community EC rules a Commission spokesman said The spokesman said the Court rejected a case brought by three European margarine manufacturers which argued the EC Executive Commission did not have the power to launch the scheme in October without approval from EC ministers The plan was aimed at offloading tonnes of butter from a one mln tonne stockpile at a specially reduced price of European currency units per kilogram Reuter
