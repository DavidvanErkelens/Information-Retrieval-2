PHILIPPINE LONG DISTANCE PHI PLANS SPLIT NEW YORK June Philippine Long Distance Telephone Co said it has called a special shareholder meeting to approve a proposed two for one stock split and a pct stock dividend The company said the meeting will be held July and shareholders of record on April will be eligible to vote The company said it will also ask shareholders to approve an increase in authorized common shares to mln and of serial preferred stock to mln and to authorize the sale of two billion pesos of debentures Reuter
