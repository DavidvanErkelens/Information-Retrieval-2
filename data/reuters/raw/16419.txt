MIDLANTIC MIDL O TO ACQUIRE COUNTY BANCORP EDISON N J April Midlantic Corp said it agreed to acquire County Bancorp for about mln dlrs in an agreement calling for County Trust Co a County Bancorp subsidiary to merge into Midlantic National Bank North Midlantic said it will pay dlrs a share in cash or times County Bancorp s March book value for each of County s about shares outstanding It said it received an option from three principal shareholders for pct of County s outstanding The acquisition is expected in the third quarter of and is subject to regulatory and shareholder approvals Reuter
