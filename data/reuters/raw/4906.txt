UNION PHOENIX STEEL UNABLE TO REACH AGREEMENT CLAYMONT Del March Phoenix Steel Corp said it and the union representing workers at its seamless tubing mill in Phoenixville Penn were unable to reach agreement to extend the union contract that expires March Phoenix said the union rejected its offer to extend a labor agreement through May and a medical insurance agreement for active employees through June The company said no further bargaining sessions are planned between it and United Steelworkers Local Reuter
