TRADERS CONFIRM SOME INDIAN PALM OLEIN PURCHASES LONDON April Traders said the Indian State Trading Corporation STC bought three tonne cargoes of refined bleached deodorised palm olein at its import tender today They also said the STC is still in the market and additional business may be concluded over the weekend The confirmed business comprised two cargoes of rbd olein for June shipment at dlrs per tonne and one July at dlrs cif all on pct counter trade Reuter
