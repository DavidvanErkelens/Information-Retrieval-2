USDA REPORTS CORN SOLD TO USSR TAIWAN WASHINGTON March The U S Agriculture Department said private U S exporters reported new sales of tonnes of corn to the Soviet Union and tonnes of corn switched from previously announced unknown destinations to the USSR The corn is for delivery during the marketing year and under the fourth year of the U S USSR Long Term Grain Supply Agreement the USDA said The department said exporters also reported corn sales of tonnes for delivery to Taiwan with tonnes for shipment in the season and the balance for shipment in the year The marketing year for corn began September Sales of corn to the USSR for delivery during the fourth year of the agreement which ends this September now total mln tonnes Reuter
