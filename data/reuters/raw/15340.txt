USDA DISCUSSING PL AGREEMENT WITH MOROCCO WASHINGTON April The U S Agriculture Department is currently discussing an amendment to a PL agreement signed with Morocco on January but the mix of commodities under the amendment has not been determined a U S Agriculture Department official said The official noted the agreement signed in January provided for the supply of about tonnes of vegetable oil tonnes of corn and tonnes of wheat all for delivery during the current fiscal year ending this September No purchase authorizations for the commodities provided in the January agreement have been announced by the department Reuter
