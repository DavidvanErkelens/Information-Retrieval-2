DEN DANSKE LAUNCHES MLN DLR EURO CD PROGRAMME FRANKFURT March Den Danske Bank of Denmark is raising mln dlrs through a programme of short and medium term euro certificates of deposit arranger London based Salomon Brothers International Ltd said in a statement received here Signing takes place today and the first issuance of maturities from seven days to five years should take place later this month Denominations would be and one mln dlrs and similar amounts in sterling and other currencies Aside from the London branch of Den Danske and the arranger other dealers in the programme would be the Swiss Bank Corp International and S G Warburg and Co Ltd Salomon said the principal paying agent for the CDs would be Bankers Trust Co Den Danske s U S Domestic commercial paper program is rated A by Standard and Poor s and P by Moody s rating agency Salomon said REUTER
