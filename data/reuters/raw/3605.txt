BANCA NAZIONALE DEL LAVORO LAUNCHES BOND WARRANTS LONDON March Banca Nazionale del Lavoro London branch is issuing a mln dlr five year straight bond with equity warrants lead manager Credit Suisse First Boston Ltd said The issue which matures April has an indicated coupon of to six pct and par pricing Fees total pct and listing will be in Luxembourg Payment date is April The bonds will be sold in denominations of dlrs with one warrant attached which will be exercisable between April and April Each warrant will entitle the holder to purchase savings shares of EFI Banca at a premium of about pct above the closing price on the Milan Stock Exchange on or before March EFI is a medium term credit institution about pct owned by Banca Nazionale del Lavoro REUTER
