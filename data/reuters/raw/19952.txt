STAR TECHNOLOGIES STTX O REVISES TH QTR NET STERLING Va June Star Technologies Inc said that a settlement of a shareholder lawsuit has produced a mln dlr charge which the company recorded in its fourth quarter earnings The company said it will pay the settlement with company stock subject to court approval To reflect the settlement therefore the company said it has revised its fiscal fourth quarter ended March earnings by mln dlrs or nine cts per share on a primary basis As such the company said it is recording a net loss of dlrs or four cts a share versus the net income of dlrs previously reported For the year to date ended March net income was reduced to mln dlrs from the mln dlrs previously reported on May The class action suit filed in a federal district court charged the company with various violations of federal securities laws with regards to the company s initial public offering in December and certain subsequent filings Reuter
