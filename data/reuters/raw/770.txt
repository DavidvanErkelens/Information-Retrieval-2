ANTITRUST LAWYER NOMINATED CLAIMS COURT JUDGE WASHINGTON March Roger Andewelt deputy assistant attorney general for litigation in the Justice Department s antitrust division since last June is being nominated by President Reagan as a judge of the United States Claims Court the White House said The nomination which is subject to Senate approval is for a year term Andewelt who has served in a number of antitrust division posts since would succeed Thomas Lydon at the Claims Court Reuter
