NORD EST ISSUES MLN FRENCH FRANC BOND PARIS March Financial holding company Nord Est is issuing a mln franc pct five year domestic bond convertible into shares an announcement in the Official Bulletin BALO said From March to April subscription for mln of the mln bonds of francs nominal each will be reserved for shareholders holding mln shares of francs nominal representing the company s capital of mln francs on the basis of one bond for eight shares held The bonds will be exchangeable from July for shares on a one for one basis REUTER
