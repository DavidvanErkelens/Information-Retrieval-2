U K MONEY MARKET DEFICIT FORECAST AT MLN STG LONDON April The Bank of England said it forecast a shortage of around mln stg in the money market today Among the main factors affecting liquidity bills maturing in official hands and the take up of treasury bills will drain some mln stg while bills for repurchase by the market will remove around mln In addition a rise in note circulation and bankers balances below target will each drain around mln stg Partly offsetting these outflows exchequer transactions will add some mln stg to the system today REUTER
