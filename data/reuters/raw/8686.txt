SCHLESWIG HOLSTEIN LB ISSUES AUSTRALIAN DLR BOND London March Schleswig Holstein Landesbank LB Finance BV is issuing a mln Australian dlr bond due April carrying a coupon of pct and priced at said ANZ Merchant Bank Ltd as lead manager The securities are guaranteed by parent company Schleswig Holstein Landesbank Girozentrale and will be listed on the Luxembourg Stock Exchange They are available in denominations of dlrs Payment date is April There is a one pct selling concession and a pct combined underwriting and management fee REUTER
