BELGIUM LAUNCHES BONDS WITH GOLD WARRANTS ZURICH April The Kingdom of Belgium is launching mln Swiss francs of seven year notes with warrants attached to buy gold lead manager Credit Suisse said The notes themselves have a pct coupon and are priced at par Payment is due April and final maturity April Each franc note carries warrants Two warrants are required to allow the holder to buy grammes of gold at a price of francs during the entire life of the bond The latest gold price in Zurich was francs per grammes Reuter
