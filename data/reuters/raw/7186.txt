D P LOWERS MINNESOTA POWER MPL DEBT RATING CHICAGO March Duff and Phelps said it lowered its ratings assigned to Minnesota Power and Light Co s fixed income securities covering about mln dlrs in debt The first mortgage bonds were lowered to D P low single A from D P low double A and the preferred stock to D P high triple B from D P high single A it said The ratings reflect continuing erosion of a major segment of its sales base as the outlook for the taconite industry continues to be depressed D P said Reuter
