PEGA TO BUY STAKE IN TAMPER PROOF PACKAGING TORONTO June Pega Capital Resources Ltd PGA TO said it agreed in principle to acquire a pct undivided interest in all rights and patents to a tamper resistant and tamper evident packaging process for dlrs The company said the purchase price is payable in common shares priced at one dlr each The shares will be paid under an escrow agreement where Pega may terminate the purchase agreement and cancel the shares up to October It said the process results in readily visible breakage if packaging material is pierced by a sharp object Reuter
