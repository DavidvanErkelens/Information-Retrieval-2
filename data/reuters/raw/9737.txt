INDIAN PLANT SIGNS FIRST ALUMINA EXPORT CONTRACT NEW DELHI March An unnamed Norwegian firm agreed to buy tonnes of alumina a year from a refinery in eastern Orissa state due to start operations in the next months a Commerce Ministry official told Reuters He said the state owned National Aluminium Co which owns the plant and the state owned Mineral and Metals Trading Corp signed its first long term export agreement with the company but gave no further details Of the plant s tonnes annual capacity will be smelted into tonnes of aluminium and the remaining will be exported the official said REUTER
