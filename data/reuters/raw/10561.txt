CABOT CBT CALLS NOTES FOR REDEMPTION WALTHAM Mass March Cabot Corp said it called for redemption its outstanding pct notes due November and pct notes due September aggregating mln dlrs at face amount It said the redemption is part of a restructuring in which it has said it would divest itself of certain discontinued businesses repurchase shares and reduce long term debt The pct notes will be redeemed at pct plus interest from November The pct notes will be redeemed at pct plus accrued interest from March Reuter
