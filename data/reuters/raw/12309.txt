JOHNSTOWN AMERICAN JAC MAKES EXCHANGE OFFER ATLANTA April Johnstown American Cos said it began a tender offer for the outstanding shares of its preferred stock Under the offer the company said holders of the preferred would receive one share of a new issue of convertible redeemable preferred stock for each share of their preferred The company said terms of the new preferred will permit it to redeem and retire the preferred shares at a discount from their current liquidation value of mln dlrs or dlrs a share The company said that if all the preferred shares are tendered it will issue preferred shares disburse dlrs and cancel all preferred shares that are tendered The company said that if it elects to redeem the preferred it must pay holders mln dlrs in cash It said it must also issue warrants for the purchase of three mln common shares at a price of dlrs a share or pct over the market price on the day before the notice of redemption whichever is greater The company said the exchange offer is conditioned on tendering shareholders releasing certain rights to contingent payouts and profit share interests that were given in connection with its acquisition of Consolidated Capital Equities Corp and Johnstown Management Co The offer it said is also conditioned on the tender of at least two thirds of the outstanding preferred shares The company said the exchange offer will expire at EST on May one unless extended Reuter
