NEW EEP DURUM WHEAT OFFER TO ALGERIA USDA WASHINGTON March U S exporters will have the opportunity to sell an additional tonnes of U S durum wheat to Algeria under the Export Enhancement Program EEP the U S Agriculture Department said The department said the sales will be subsidized with commodities from the Commodity Credit Corporation CCC inventory and the subsidy will enable U S exports to compete at commercial prices in the Algerian market Algeria has already purchased tonnes of U S durum wheat under a previous export enhancement initiative announced November it said Details of the latest initiative including an invitation for offers from exporters will be issued in the near future the department said Reuter
