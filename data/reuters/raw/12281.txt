AMERICAN QUASAR COMPLETES REORGANIZATION PLAN FORT WORTH Texas April American Quasar Petroleum Co said it completed its previously announced plan of reorganization The company said it accepted subordinated notes and debentures in the aggregate principal amount of mln dlrs which were tendered in exchange for mln shares of American Quasar common stock and Class A warrants to acquire mln additional shares of American Quasar common stock for five cts per share The principal amount of the subordinated debt remaining outstanding is dlrs most of which is now held subject to indentures which were amended in certain respects in connection with the exchange offers the company said Outstanding bank debt of mln dlrs plus an additional dlrs in borrowings for working capital has been financed with RepublicBank in Dallas Reuter
