JAPAN AUCTIONS BILLION YEN OF FOUR YEAR NOTES TOKYO April The Finance Ministry auctioned billion yen of four year government notes the first auction of fiscal which started on April The coupon was set at pct a record low for any government note and below the pct set on the last two year notes auctioned in February Today s issue was the first for four year notes since last May which produced an average price of average yield of pct and a coupon of pct the Ministry said The auction results will be announced tomorrow afternoon REUTER
