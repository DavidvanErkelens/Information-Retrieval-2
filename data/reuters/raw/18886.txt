JORDAN GETS MLN DLRS IN WORLD BANK LOANS WASHINGTON June Jordan will receive two loans totalling mln dlrs to support projects aimed at increasing that country s electrical production and improving housing in slum and squatter areas the World Bank said The bank said it is making a mln dlr loan to help fund a mln dlr project designed to meet its future power needs It said the other loan is mln dlrs in support of project to provide affordable housing urban infrastructure and social services to low income families in slum and squatter areas Reuter
