STANDARD SRD MOBIL MOB PLAN OFFSHORE PLATFORM HOUSTON April Standard Oil Co said a contract has been awarded to CBS Engineering Inc for a drilling and production platform to be installed in Ewing Bank Block in the Gulf of Mexico where Standard and Mobil Corp each own a pct interest Standard said its Standard Oil Production Co subsidiary will operate the platform which is being designed to produce barrels of oil and mln cubic feet of gas daily The platform is now expected to be installed in the summer of Other owners are Kerr McGee Corp KMG with pct and Prudential Insurance Co of America with pct Reuter
