NASD SETS FILING DEADLINES FOR BROKERS DEALERS WASHINGTON April The National Association of Securities Dealers Inc said it set April as the deadline for government securities brokers and dealers to submit applications for registration under new rules It said the rules apply to unregulated brokers and dealers who must be registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission by July NASD said it could guarantee brokers and dealers would meet the July deadline if it received application materials by April After April it said it will make every effort to meet the July deadline but could not guarantee it Reuter
