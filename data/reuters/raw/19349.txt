TRANSAMERICAN TA GETS APPROVAL ON CHAPTER HOUSTON June TransAmerican Natural Gas said it received approval from a U S District Court in Texas allowing the company to solicit creditor acceptance of its negotiated Chapter plan of reorganization The company said the approval authorized the company to distribute the disclosure statement to its creditors and solicit creditors acceptance of the plan TransAmerican said the confirmation hearing is scheduled for August in U S Southern District of Texas court Reuter
