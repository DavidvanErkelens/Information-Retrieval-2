EXPORT CREDIT BANK FOUNDED BY CARIBBEAN NATIONS CASTRIES St Lucia June Finance ministers from the member Caribbean Community Caricom approved a draft agreement for a new export credit facility in an effort to stem the steady decline in interregional trade The Caribbean Export Credit Bank scheduled to begin operations January will be headquartered in Barbados Its initial capital of mln dlrs will come from Caricom member governments private sector donors and the Caribbean Development Bank Capitalisation is projected at mln dlrs with foreign aid including soft loans from international institutions Caricom leaders are attending their annual meeting and high on the agenda is how to stop the decline in inter regional trade which dropped by a third last year Reuter
