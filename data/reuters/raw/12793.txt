MOSCOW STAGES NUCLEAR TEST MOSCOW April The Soviet Union conducted an underground nuclear test aimed at perfecting military techology the official news agency Tass reported Tass said the test was carried out at gmt at the Semipalatinsk testing ground in Kazakhstan Soviet Central Asia It was the third Soviet underground blast since Moscow halted an month nuclear test moratorium last February saying Washington s refusal to join the ban had forced it to resume testing Moscow began its unilateral nuclear test moratorium in August calling on the United States to agree to a total ban on testing as the first step to nuclear disarmament Last December it announced that it would end the freeze after the first U S Test this year Washington had continued testing saying the blasts were necessary to maintain its nuclear deterrent and that problems remained concerning the monitoring of a test freeze The United States staged one test on February and another on February Moscow responded with a blast on February and a second on March Soviet officials and the state media blamed the resumption of testing on the U S Decision not to join the freeze Reuter
