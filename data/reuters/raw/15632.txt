FOOD N FUEL REGISTERS PARTNERSHIP UNIT OFFERING FORT WORTH April Food N Fuel Partners L P said it filed a registration statement with the Securities and Exchange Commission covering a planned initial offering of Class A Units of limited partnership interest The partnership said the units are expected to be offered at to dlrs each next month by underwriters managed by Donaldson Lufkin and Jenrette Rotan Mosle Inc Lovett Mitchell Webb and Garrison Inc and Southwest Securities Inc Food N Fuel said it will use the proceeds and Class B Units to acquire convenience stores self service gasoline outlets and truck stocks primarily in small communities in the Southwestern and Southern United States which the partnership will manage Reuter
