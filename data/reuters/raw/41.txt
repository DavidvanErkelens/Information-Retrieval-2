ICO PRODUCERS TO PRESENT NEW COFFEE PROPOSAL LONDON Feb International Coffee Organization ICO producing countries will present a proposal for reintroducing export quotas for months from April with a firm undertaking to try to negotiate up to September any future quota distribution on a new basis ICO delegates said Distribution from April would be on an unchanged basis as in an earlier producer proposal which includes shortfall redistributions totalling mln bags they said Resumption of an ICO contact group meeting with consumers scheduled for this evening has been postponed until tomorrow delegates said Reuter
