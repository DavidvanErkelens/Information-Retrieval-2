CORNING GLW HAZLETON HLC COMPLETE MERGER CORNING N Y April Corning Glass Works and Hazleton Laboratories Corp jointly said they completed a merger of the two companies in a deal valued at about mln dlrs A joint statement said Hazleton stockholders will receive shares of Corning common for each Hazleton share Hazleton a supplier of biological and chemical services and products will operate as a Corning subsidiary under its current management team the statement said Reuter
