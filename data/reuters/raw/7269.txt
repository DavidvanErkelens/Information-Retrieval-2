GERMANTOWN SAVINGS BANK IN SUBSCRIPTION OFFER BALA CYNWYD Pa March Germantown Savings Bank said it has started a subscription offering of shares at a maximum price of dlrs each to customers and community residents It said the offer will expire April The company said any shares not sold will be offered to the public through underwriter Alex Brown and Sons Inc ABSB and the stock is expected to trade on the NASDAQ system It said the actual price to be charged for the shares will be determined after the subscription offering Reuter
