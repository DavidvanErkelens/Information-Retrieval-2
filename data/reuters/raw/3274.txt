PITT DES MOINES PDM GETS QUALIFIED AUDIT PITTSBURGH March Pitt Des Moines Inc said it will receive a qualified opinion from auditors on its and financial statements It said the qualification related to its inability to determine the effects if any of the final resolution of a suit filed against it by Washington Public Power Supply System The suit was dismissed in a lower court action but the ultimate outcome of the matter is still uncertain since Washington Public Power has appealed to the U S Court of Appeals Reuter
