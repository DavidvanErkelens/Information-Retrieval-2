SWISS SIGHT DEPOSITS FALL BY MLN FRANCS ZURICH April Sight deposits of commercial banks at the Swiss National Bank fell by mln Swiss francs to billion in the six days ending April the National Bank said Sight deposits are an important measure of money market liquidity in Switzerland Foreign exchange reserves fell by mln francs to billion francs The National Bank attributed this fall to the dismantling of outstanding swap arrangements Bank notes in circulation fell by mln francs to billion while other deposits on call at the National Bank mainly government funds fell by mln francs to mln REUTER
