SWEDISH UNEMPLOYMENT DOWN AS DATA IS REVISED STOCKHOLM March A new method of calculating Sweden s unemployment figures reduced the number of jobless by a sixth a spokesman for the Central Bureau of Statistics SCB said reporting a substantial drop in the past year According to the revised data there were jobless in February representing pct of the workforce against or pct of the workforce in February SCB official Olle Wessberg said the new figures were based on a more extensive survey of the unemployed which brought Sweden into line with the practises recommended by the Geneva based International Labour Organisation Wessberg said the new method cut the number of unemployed by about pct The way we are now collecting data is far more accurate and we are asking many more questions to find out whether the jobless want work whether they are able to work and whether they have actually looked for work he told Reuters The new method was first used for the January figures which showed unemployment dropping to pct of the workforce from pct old style in December but Wessberg said the change had apparently not been noticed by the press Recalculated according to the new method unemployment in February would have stood at pct the SCB said REUTER
