ALLISON S PLACE ALLS SALES INCREASE IN FEBRUARY LOS ANGELES March Allison s Place Inc president Marvin Schenker said company owned stores sales for February increased pct over the same period last year He said comparable store sales in February increased pct The company which owns and franchises a total of clothing outlets where all articles cost six dlrs will increase that figure to seven dlrs starting March Schenker said He said the impact of that boost will start to be felt in the early part of the company s second quarter and continue throughout the year Schenker said costs of the company s merchandise will not increase Reuter
