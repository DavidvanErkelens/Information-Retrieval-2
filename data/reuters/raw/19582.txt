THOUSANDS TRAPPED IN BANGLADESH FLASH FLOODS DHAKA June Flash floods and high winds have trapped some people and injured at least in northeastern Bangladesh s Sunamganj district officials said They said water knee deep in at least villages following heavy rains last Wednesday was receding after extensively damaging crops and property The situation was aggravated by a mile per hour storm which swept across the area yesterday and flattened at least houses the officials said but gave no other details Relief Ministry officials describing the floods as a routine hazard told Reuters relief squads had been dispatched to the area with food and medicine Reuter
