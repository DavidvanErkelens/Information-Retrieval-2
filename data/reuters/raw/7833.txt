ZUERCHER ZIEGELEIEN SETS MLN SFR WARRANT BOND ZURICH March Zuercher Ziegeleien AG plans a mln Swiss franc pct domestic bond with warrants for participation certificates lead manager Swiss Bank Corp said The year par priced bonds of francs face value carry two warrants A and B each of which entitle holders to acquire one certificate of francs nominal value The A warrants may be exercised from June to March at francs and the B warrants from June to March at francs The exercise prices remain in force after the dilution through an announced rights issue to be conducted on May REUTER
