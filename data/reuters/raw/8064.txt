HONG KONG LAND HKLD HK DETAILS MANDARIN SPIN OFF HONG KONG March Hong Kong Land Co Ltd HKLD HK gave details of a previously announced spin off of its pct stake in Mandarin Oriental International Ltd and a pct stake in Jardine Strategic Holdings Ltd JSH An offer document prepared by financial adviser Jardine Fleming Securities Ltd said Hong Kong Land offers shareholders Mandarin shares at H K Dlrs each and JSH shares at dlrs each for every Hong Kong Land shares But price of Mandarin may be lowered to dlrs each while shareholders may instead receive JSH shares if all Hong Kong Land s existing warrants are exercised before April Hong Kong Land warrants are worth mln dlrs Holders of the firm s mln preference shares will receive a similar offer as ordinary shareholders The company also agreed to sell a pct stake in Mandarin to Jardine Strategic for some mln dlrs Hong Kong Land said last month it is expected to receive net proceeds of about mln dlrs from the spin off and the sum will be used either for expansion or to reduce its debts Hong Kong Land shares gained cts to close at eight dlrs each yesterday while JSH was up cts at dlrs Mandarin will commence trading on June REUTER
