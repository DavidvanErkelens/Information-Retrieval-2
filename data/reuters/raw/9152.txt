VIETNAM TO RESETTLE ON STATE FARMS IN BANGKOK March Vietnam will resettle people on state farms known as new economic zones in to create jobs and grow more high value export crops the communist party newspaper Nhan Dan said Yesterday s edition received here today said Vietnam would invest one billion dong including the costs of relocation in new economic zones About one third of that sum would be spent on export crops such as coffee tea rubber and pepper in the Central Highlands it said Since Vietnam has resettled about three million people from cities and crowded river deltas to the zones REUTER
