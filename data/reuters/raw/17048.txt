QANTAS TO BUY EXTENDED RANGE BOEING AIRCRAFT SYDNEY April State owned Qantas Airways Ltd said it has placed a firm order for a single Boeing Co BA N ER extended range aircraft for delivery in August at a cost of mln Australian dlrs including spares A statement said it also has options to buy six more and will decide in mid whether to use engines made by United Technologies Corp UTX unit Pratt and Whitney or General Electric Co GE The ER can carry more cargo and passengers and is more fuel efficient than the six of which Qantas has in service REUTER
