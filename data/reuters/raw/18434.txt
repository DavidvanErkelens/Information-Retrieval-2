DOWTY TO BUY BOEING UNIT FOR MLN DLRS LONDON June Dowty Group Plc DWTY L said it agreed to buy Hydraulic Units Inc from Boeing Co BA N for around mln dlrs cash The final sum payable would be adjusted according to changes in net tangible assets between March and completion due on July Payment would be financed through medium term borrowings Hydraulic reported mln dlrs pretax profit in the year to end December on sales of mln It designs manufactures and sells hydraulic and mechanical systems to airframe manufacturers REUTER
