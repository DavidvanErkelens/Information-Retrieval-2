HOUSE SPEAKER BACKS OIL IMPORT FORECAST PLAN WASHINGTON March House Speaker Jim Wright endorsed a proposal to require the president to take action to reduce oil imports if they threaten to top pct of U S consumption Wright told reporters the plan by Sen Lloyd Bentsen a fellow Texas Democrat was positive and useful Oil imports peaked in at pct were short of pct last year and this year are up over pct Bentsen said The Bentsen proposal supported by other Senators would would require the president to issue annual three year oil import forecasts In any year they threatened to top pct he would be ordered to propose quotas or other solution Reuter
