DAIWA HOUSE EQUITY WARRANT INCREASED FIXED LONDON June The five year equity warrant eurobond for Daiwa House Industry Co Ltd DHOU T has been increased to mln dlrs from the initial mln dlrs lead manager Nomura International Ltd said The coupon has been set at pct compared with the indicated level of pct Bond market sources noted that this equals the lowest ever coupon seen in this sector The exercise price was set at yen per share representing a premium of pct over today s closing price of yen The foreign exchange rate was set at yen to the dollar REUTER
