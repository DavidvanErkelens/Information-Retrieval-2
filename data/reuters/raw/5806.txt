JAPAN OFFICIALS SEE DIFFICULTY HITTING GNP TARGET TOKYO March The government will find it very difficult to achieve its new gross national product GNP forecast of three pct growth in the fiscal year ending March Economic Planning Agency officials said They made the comment to reporters after news that GNP rose pct in the October December quarter after a pct increase the previous three months For Japan to achieve three pct growth in GNP in the January March period would have to grow pct from October December the officials said The government lowered its GNP forecast for from four pct last December REUTER
