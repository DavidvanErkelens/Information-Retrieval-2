AQUINO SWEARS IN FOUR CABINET SECRETARIES MANILA March Philippine President Corazon Aquino swore in four new cabinet secretaries to fill vacancies created by the resignations of secretaries who have said they will run for the May senate elections Carlos Dominguez was made agriculture secretary A former deputy agriculture secretary Dominguez is considered an expert on land reform Sedfrey Ordonez was made justice secretary and Fulgencio Factoran secretary for natural resources Ramon Diaz was named chairman of the Presidential Commission on Good Government All three men are lawyers REUTER
