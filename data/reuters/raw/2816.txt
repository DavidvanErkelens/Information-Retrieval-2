JAMAICA CORRECTS SIZE OF PARIS CLUB RESCHEDULING NEW YORK March The Jamaican Information Service said the rescheduling agreement reached with the Paris Club of western government creditors covers mln dlrs of debt and not mln dlrs as it announced yesterday Under the accord clinched on Thursday in Paris pct of the principal and pct of the interest owed between April and March is being rescheduled The debts due in are being treated as arrears and will be rescheduled over six years with years grace while the rest of the debt will be rescheduled over years with a year grace period Reuter
