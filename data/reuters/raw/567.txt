ZAPATA ZOS WON T PAY INTEREST ON DEBENTURES HOUSTON March Zapata Corp said it does not intend to pay the interest due March on its pct subordinated debentures due or the interest due May One on its pct subordinated debentures due Zapata said the results of continuing discussions with its bank lenders and the company s future financial position will determine Zapata s ability to meet its obligations to holders of the subordinated debentures The company said deferrals of payment obligations and covenant waivers provided by all of its bank lenders which were scheduled to expire February have been extended through April Zapata stopped paying interest on the debentures in April Reuter
