ASIX SYSTEMS GETS MORE FINANCING FREMONT Calif April ASIX Systems a developer of test systems for ASIC devices said it has received dlrs in a second round of equity financing The company said investors include first round participants Paragon Partners Citicorp CCI Vista Ventures Rust Ventures and Orien Ventures plus new investors North Carolina Bank Venture Co LP RepublicBankCorp RPT and ECI Ventures It said it has now raised total capitalization of dlrs Reuter
