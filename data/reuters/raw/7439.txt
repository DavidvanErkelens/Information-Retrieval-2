GM GM TO IDLE WORKERS DETROIT March General Motors Corp said it will lay off workers temporarily at one of its car assembly plants on March the same day that other workers will return to work The number one U S automaker in announcing its plant schedule for the week of March said no further indefinite layoffs are expected for that week The number of GM workers that have been put on indefinite layoff status remains at a spokeswoman said GM said it will idle the workers at its Lansing Mich Buick Oldsmobile Cadillac plant which will be shut down from March until April for inventory adjustment Also on March work will resume at GM s Norwood Ohio Chevrolet Pontiac GM of Canada plant and hourly employees there will return to work The plant had been idled due to a shortage of materials The number of workers on temporary layoff remains at the spokeswoman said Reuter
