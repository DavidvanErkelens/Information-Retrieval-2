CCC ACCEPTS EXPORT BONUS ON SEMOLINA TO EGYPT WASHINGTON March The Commodity Credit Corporation CCC has accepted a bid for an export bonus to cover a sale of tonnes of semolina to Egypt the U S Agriculture Department said The department said the semolina is for shipment April October and the bonus awarded was dlrs per tonne The bonus was made to International Multifoods Corp and will paid to the exporter in the form of commodities from CCC stocks the department said An additional tonnes of semolina are still available to Egypt under the Export Enhancement Program initiative announced August it said Reuter
