OSAKA POSTPONES STOCK FUTURES CONTRACT TOKYO March The Osaka Stock Exchange has postponed its planned April launch of a futures contract tied to stock market movements until after parliament considers a bill to cut tax on futures transactions an exchange official said Opposition parties have disrupted all parliamentary proceedings to protest government plans for a new sales tax The bill would reduce the tax to pct of the transaction value from the current pct the exchange said The Osaka futures contract will be for a package of issues selected from the stocks that make up the Nikkei average on the Tokyo Stock Exchange REUTER
