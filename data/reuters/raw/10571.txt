ALLEGHENY LUDLUM FILES INITIAL PUBLIC OFFERING PITTSBURGH March Allegheny Ludlum Corp said it filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission for an initial public offering of common shares The company said it is offering mln primary shares and secondary shares in the U S and mln primary shares overseas Goldman Sachs and Co will manage the U S offering and Goldman Sachs International Corp will manage the international side of the offering Proceeds from the sale will be used to reduce debt The company makes specialty steel and metels and said it is one of the largest U S makers of stainless steel Reuter
