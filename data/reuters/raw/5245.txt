ALSTHOM PLANS ELECTRICAL EQUIPMENT JOB CUTS PARIS March Engineering group Alsthom ALSF PA said it planned to slash jobs this year from the strong workforce in its electrical equipment division Details of the proposed staff reductions will be put to labour representatives at a meeting on March Company officials said the cuts were needed to adjust to a drop in orders from French state utility Electricite de France and the cancellation or postponement of unspecified export contracts REUTER
