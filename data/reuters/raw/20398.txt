DAHLBERG DAHL O COMPLETES SEARS S AGREEMENT MINNEAPOLIS Oct Dahlberg Inc said it completed a previously announced agreement with Sears Roebuck and Co to operate hearing aid centers in sears stores on a national basis Dahlberg currently operates some hearing aid centers on a concession basis in Sears stores In connection with the program the company said it granted Sears an option to buy up to shares of Dahlberg stock at dlrs a share Reuter
