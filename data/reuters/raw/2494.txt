MEATPACKERS REJECT OCCIDENTAL OXY UNIT OFFER CHICAGO March United Food and Commercial Workers Union Local rejected a new contract proposal from Iowa Beef Processors Inc and remain out of work union spokesman Allen Zack said In mid December Iowa Beef a subsidiary of Occidental Petroleum Corp closed its beef processing plant at Dakota City Nebraska because it said it had no alternative to threats by meatpackers to disrupt operations About UFCWU members are affected by what the union terms as a lockout A year labor contract at the plant expired December Zack said IBP s proposal included elimination of a two tier wage structure a cent an hour wage cut for slaughterers and a cent an hour wage reduction for processors The new proposal also included a bonus system of dlrs for workers who had been at the plant for two years Zack said The annual turnover rate at the facility is pct he said Reuter
