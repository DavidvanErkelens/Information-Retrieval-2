USAIR U CUTS ATLANTA COACH FARES ATLANTA April USAir Group Inc said it is reducing coach fares up to pct between Atlanta and destinations for travel April through June The fares are non refundable and applicable to roundtrip flights Reservations must be made at least two days in advance and tickets purchased within hours after reservations are made the company said Roundtrip fares between Atlanta and Pittsburgh for example would be dlrs instead of dlrs USAir said Reuter
