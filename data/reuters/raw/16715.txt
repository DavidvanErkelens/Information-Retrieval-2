PACIFICORP PPW REDEEMS PREFERRED STOCK PORTLAND Ore April PacifiCorp said on May it is redeeming mln shares of its preferred stock The company said it is fully redeeming its dlr no par serial preferred and its dlr no par serial preferred and partially redeeming its pct serial preferred The shares of dlr will be redeemed at dlrs per share plus accumulated dividend of dlrs The shares of dlr preferred will be redeemed at dlrs per share plus dividend of dlrs Pacificorp will redeem pct of its shares of pct preferred at dlrs plus dlrs dividend Reuter
