CONNECTICUT ENERGY CNE UNIT SEEKS HIGHER RATES BRIDGEPORT Conn March Connecticut Energy Corp said its Southern Connecticut Gas Co unit filed with state regulators a letter of intent to raise its rates by pct or about mln dlrs beginning in late The company said it expects to file a formal application with the Connecticut Department of Public Utility Control within days The company said a rate increase is needed to cover higher operating and maintenance expenses that have occurred since it last received a rate hike of three pct or mln dlrs in January Reuter
