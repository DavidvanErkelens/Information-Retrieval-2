MORE INTERMEDIATE U S WHEAT CREDITS FOR MOROCCO WASHINGTON March The Commodity Credit Corporation has approved an additional mln dlrs under its interemdiate export credit guarantee program GSM for sales of U S wheat to Morocco the U S Agriculture Department said The action increases Morocco s cumulative fiscal year program for wheat under GSM to mln dlrs The credit terms extended for exports under the program must be in excess of three years but no more than seven years to eligible for credit coverage All sales under this line must be registered and exported by September The department also said the guarantee line for sales of U S wheat under the Export Credit Guarantee Program GSM has been decreased mln dlrs to mln Reuter
