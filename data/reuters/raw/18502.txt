DAIRY FARM GETS BILLION H K DLR LOAN HONG KONG June Dairy Farm International Holdings Ltd obtained a billion H K Dlr loan from the Hongkong and Shanghai Banking Corp to finance its tender offer for a pct stake in Kwik Save Discount Group Plc KWIK Dairy Farm chairman Simon Keswick told reporters He did not disclose the terms of the loan but said they were fairly fair Dairy Farm said earlier in a statement it would offer to buy up to mln Kwik Save shares at stg each raising its stake in the retail firm to pct from pct now Keswick said Dairy Farm wanted to obtain at least pct of Kwik Save which would make it Kwik Save s biggest shareholder otherwise it would dispose of its entire stake Kwik Save opened at stg up from yesterday s stg close REUTER
