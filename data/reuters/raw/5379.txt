KAWASAKI STEEL ISSUES NOVEL MLN DLR EUROBOND LONDON March Kawasaki Steel International Finance Plc is issuing a stepped coupon mln dlr eurobond due March priced at pct lead manager Wako International Europe Ltd said The deal guaranteed by Mitsui Trust and Banking Co Ltd will carry a seven pct coupon in year one rising to pct in the final year Payment date is March The bond is available in denominations of dlrs and will be listed in Luxembourg Fees were not disclosed but a Wako official confirmed the deal was a targetted issue REUTER
