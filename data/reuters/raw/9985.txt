FORD F CREDIT UNIT SELLS NEW ZEALAND DLR FRNS NEW YORK March Ford Motor Credit Co a unit of Ford Motor Co is offering in the domestic debt market mln New Zealand dlrs of floating rate notes due said sole manager Bear Stearns and Co The initial rate for the notes will be set on April at basis points below the day New Zealand bank bill rate After that the rate will be reset quarterly Non callable to maturity the issue pays quarterly and is rated A by Moody s and AA minus by S and P Underwriters said floaters denominated in a foreign currency are a new wrinkle on Wall Street Reuter
