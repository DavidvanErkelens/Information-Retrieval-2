SANKEI BUILDING ISSUES EQUITY WARRANT EUROBOND LONDON April Sankei Building Co Ltd is issuing a mln dlr equity warrant eurobond due May paying an indicated coupon of pct and priced at par lead manager Nomura International Ltd said The issue is guaranteed by Sumitomo Bank Ltd and is available in denominations of dlrs The selling concession is pct while management and underwriting combined pays pct The payment date is May and listing will be in Luxembourg Final terms will be fixed on April The warrants are exercisable from May until April REUTER
