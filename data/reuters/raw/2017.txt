GM LAYING OFF AT TWO MICHIGAN PLANTS DETROIT March General Motors Corp said it ordered temporary layoffs of hourly workers to cut production and thereby reduce inventories of cars built at two plants later this month A spokesman said workers would be laid off one week beginning March at GM s Detroit Hamtramck luxury car plant Another will be laid off a week effective March at GM s Lansing Mich plant which builds the company s N body compact cars Reuter
