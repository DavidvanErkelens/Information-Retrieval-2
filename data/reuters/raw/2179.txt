INTERNATIONAL LEASE FINANCE ILFC LEASES JETS LOS ANGELES Calif March International Lease Finance Corp said it has agreed to lease a new Boeing Co BA jet and two new McDonnell Douglas MD MD jets The company said the value of the combined transactions totals mln dlrs The Boing jet will be leased to Monarch Airlines Ltd in five year mln dlr transaction and will be delivered in May The two McDonnell Douglas jets will be leased to British Island Airways PLC in an eight year mln dlr transaction They will be delivered in April the company said Reuter
