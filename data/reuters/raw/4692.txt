DUTCH CHEMICAL GROUP LAUNCHES DUAL CURRENCY BOND AMSTERDAM March Dutch state owned chemical group N V DSM is launching a mln guilder dual currency bond with a pct coupon due and priced at par lead manager Algemene Bank Nederland N V Said Interest will be paid in guilders and each guilder bond will be redeemed in sterling on April Subscription closes Friday March payment date is April listing will be on the Amsterdam stock exchange Co leads are Bank Mees en Hope AMRO Bank and Citicorp Investment Bank Netherlands The management group consists of RABO NMB CLN Oyens en van Eeghen and F van Lanschot Bankiers REUTER
