FRANCE S CRH CANCELS TWO BILLION FRANC TAP ISSUE PARIS June French mortgage refinancing agency Caisse de Refinancement Hypothecaire CRH has cancelled today s auction of between one and two billion francs worth of tap stock the Bank of France said In a statement the agency cited exceptional circumstances on the bond market which resulted in a low level of applications Today s issue would have been a new tranche of the agency s March pct year issue At its last auction in May the CRH sold billion francs worth of the stock at a top accepted price of pct giving an average pct yield REUTER
