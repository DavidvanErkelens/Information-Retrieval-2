MAI SEEKS MLN DLR MULTIPLE FACILITY LONDON March MAI Plc the financial services and advertising group has asked S G Warburg and Co Ltd to arrange a mln dlr multiple option facility which will restructure a part of the company s exisiting banking arrangements banking sources said The financing will be for five years and involves a committed backstop facility and an uncommitted tender panel for cash advances in dollars or other available currencies The committed part of the transaction will carry a facility fee of basis points and drawings will be at a margin of basis points There also will be a utilization fee of five basis points if the underwriting banks are called upon to provide more than pct of the facility Reuter
