IRAQ CCC CREDIT GUARANTEES SWITCHED USDA WASHINGTON April The Commodity Credit Corporation CCC has transferred mln dlrs in credit guarantees previously earmarked for sales of U S corn and mln dlrs for sales of oilseeds to increase available coverage on sales of U S poultry meat to Iraq the U S Agriculture Department said The department said the action was taken at the request of Iraq s State Trade Organization for Grains and Foodstuffs The guarantee line for sales of corn has been reduced from mln dlrs to mln and the line for oilseeds from mln dlrs to zero The guarantee line for sales of frozen poultry has been increased from mln dlrs to mln dlrs USDA said Reuter
