UNION CARBIDE UK SELLS NOTES YIELDING PCT NEW YORK March Union Carbide Corp is raising mln dlrs through an offering of senior subordinated notes due yielding pct said sole manager First Boston Corp The notes have a pct coupon and were priced at to yield basis points more than comparable Treasury paper Non callable for five years the debt is rated B by Moody s and BB minus by Standard and Poor s The issue was increased from an initial offering of mln dlrs Proceeds from the note sale and a mln dlr issue of convertible subordinated debentures will be used to repay indebtedness incurred by Carbide s recapitalization plan interest expense strengthen the company s financial condition and increase operating flexibility it said in a release The company s convertible subordinated debentures were priced late Tuesday by lead manager First Boston The debentures were given a pct coupon and par pricing They are convertible into Carbide s common stock at dlrs per share representing a premium of pct over the stock price when terms on the debt were set The convertibles are rated B by Moody s and BB minus by S and P Reuter
