ARMY TO RENEGOTIATE ITT ITT RADIO CONTRACT WASHINGTON Feb The Army said it will renegotiate a mln dlr contract with ITT Corp for jam proof field radios after recent tests indicated the radios will work properly Full production of of the Single Channel Ground and Airborne Radio System SINCGARS sets has been delayed since the contract was let in The radios did not meet specifications of operating for hours before failing The Army said recent tests have indicated better reliability and that the contract will be renegotiated REUTERS
