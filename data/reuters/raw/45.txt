U S SUGAR IMPORTS DOWN IN WEEK USDA WASHINGTON Feb Sugar imports subject to the U S sugar import quota during the week ended January the initial week of the sugar quota year totaled short tons versus tons the previous week the Agriculture Department said The sugar import quota for the quota year January December has been set at short tons compared with tons in the quota year which was extended three months to December The department said the Customs Service has reported that weekly and cumulative imports are reported on an actual weight basis and when final polarizations are received cumulative import data are adjusted accordingly Reuter
