NOMURA INTERNATIONAL FINANCE ISSUES EUROBOND LONDON March Nomura International Finance Plc is issuing a mln dlr eurobond due April with a pct coupon and priced at pct Nomura International Ltd said as lead manager The transaction carries the guarantee of Nomura Securities Bonds will be issued in denominations of dlrs and will be listed in London Payment date is April Fees comprise pct for management and underwriting including a pct praecipuum and pct for selling Co lead is Pru Bache Securities The issue is targeted at Europe with no Japanese co managers REUTER
