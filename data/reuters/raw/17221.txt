MALAYSIA MAY CUT BASE LENDING RATE IN JUNE KUALA LUMPUR April Malaysia may cut its base lending rate by to one percentage point from a current pct in June to stimulate economic growth Finance Minister Daim Zainuddin said The last cut of pct was effected by banks and finance companies on April following a Central Bank directive The lending rate has been declining in recent months because the Central Bank has injected more funds into the economy Daim told an assembly of the dominant Malay based United Malays National Organisation party Daim who is also the party s Treasurer General said Malaysia s gross domestic product growth in is likely to exceed the one pct forecast in his budget presented last year He gave no details of the expected rise GDP growth in was pct REUTER
