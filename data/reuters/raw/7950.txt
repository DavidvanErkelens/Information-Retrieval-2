CCC GUARANTEES TO TURKEY SWITCHED TO MEALS USDA WASHINGTON March The Commodity Credit Corporation CCC has reallocated mln dlrs in credit guarantees to Turkey previously earmarked for sales of U S oilseeds to provide coverage for sales of U S protein meals in fiscal year the U S Agriculture Department said The department said the action reduces the guarantee line authorized for sales of oilseeds to mln dlrs from mln dlrs and creates the new line for protein meals All sales under the credit lines must be registered and exports completed by September it said Reuter
