JAPAN TO SELL BILLION YEN IN BILLS TRADERS TOKYO March The Bank of Japan will sell tomorrow billion yen of financing bills under a day repurchase agreement maturing on May to help absorb a projected money market surplus due largely to distribution of local allocation tax ahead of the March fiscal year end money traders said The yield on the bills for sales to banks and securities houses from money houses will be pct against the pct discount rate for two month commercial bills and the pct yield today on two month certificates of deposit The operation will put outstanding bill supply at about billion yen REUTER
