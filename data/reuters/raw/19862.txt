MOODY S UPGRADES SCOTT PAPER SPP DEBT TO A NEW YORK JUNE Moody s Investors Service Inc said it upgraded Scott Paper Co s long term debt rating to A from Baa The company s Prime commercial paper rating remains unchanged Approximately one billion dlrs of long term debt is affected Moody s said the upgrade reflects Scott s continuing improvement in operating results as well as Moody s expectation of substantial funds flowing to the company from an asset sale Reuter
