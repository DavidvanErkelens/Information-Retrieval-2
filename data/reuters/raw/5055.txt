SUDAN RECEIVES MLN DLRS IN PL AUTHORITY WASHINGTON March Authorizations to purchase mln dlrs worth of U S wheat and wheat flour under Public Law were issued to Sudan today the Agriculture Department said The authorization provides for mln dlrs about tonnes worth of wheat grade U S number two or better except durum which shall be number three or better It also provides for mln dlrs about tonnes worth of wheat flour The contracting period for both commodities is March through August The delivery period for wheat is March through September and for wheat flour is April through SEptember USDA said Reuter
