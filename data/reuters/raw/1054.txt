SOCIETE GENERALE DUTCH UNIT LAUNCHES CD PROGRAMME AMSTERDAM March The Amsterdam unit of French bank Societe Generale said it is launching a mln guilder five month certificate of deposit programme starting March and ending September Denominations will be in one mln guilders The price is to be set on March issue date is March and redemption at par is on September Subscriptions are to be placed with Societe Generale Oolders en de Jong C V or Haighton en Ruth B V A global note for the issue will be deposited with the Dutch central bank REUTER
