JIM WALTER JWC APROVES STOCK SPLIT TAMPA Fla April Jim Walter Corp said its board approved a for stock split in the form of a pct stock dividend to be distributed July to stockholders of record June It also said its board approved a regular quarterly cash dividend of cts a share on its pre split common payable July one to holders of record June The current dividend is equal to cts on the common outstanding after the split The company said it plans to increase the quarterly cash dividend on the post split shares by seven pct to cts beginning with the October one payment Reuter
