PARALAX VIDEO VIDO TO BUY AMERICAN VIDEO NEW YORK March Paralax Video Enterprises Inc said it signed a definitive agreement to buy American Video Group Inc for stock According to the merger proposal American Video shareholders will receive about restricted Paralax shares and warrants to buy another restricted shares at six dlrs a share The warrants run for three years In addition it said holders of American Video convertible debentures agreed to exchange their holdings for about dlrs worth of Paralax stock American Video operates stores in U S military bases Reuter
