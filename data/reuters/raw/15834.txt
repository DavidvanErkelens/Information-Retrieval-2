IRT PROPERTY LAUNCHES DEBENTURE IN EUROMARKET LONDON April IRT Property Co a real estate investment trust based in Atlanta Georgia has launched a mln dlr convertible subordinated debenture in the Euromarket a partner of Chandler Partnership Ltd said The issue is unique in several respects and is only one of a handful of such deals to be brought to this market he said Unlike most eurobonds the issue will not be underwritten Instead IRT mandated Chandler Partnership to find potential investors with whom the issue can be preplaced and to then place on a best efforts basis what ever amount may not be subscribed Chandler itself was set up in the Isle of Jersey in the middle of last year as a partnership whose members have been involved in financial markets The deal has two sponsors who assist in the placement Interallianz Bank Zurich AG and Nivison Cantrade Ltd a small U K Trading firm that formerly was broker R Nivison and Co The debentures are due June and pay a semi annual coupon of two pct and are priced at par They can be converted into shares of the company s stock at dlrs a share IRT s shares closed last night on the New York Stock Exchange at The debentures can be redeemed at par at maturity and are callable at the company s option after the first two years On or after August the company can call half the debentures if the stock trades for days at pct of the conversion price and half if it reaches pct of the conversion price Nivison said In addition holders will be able to surrender half the bonds during May at pct for a semi annual yield of pct and the other half in May at pct for a yield of pct The payment date is June The debentures will be listed in London and the fees for the deal total pct Nivison said that the deal contains a convenent which states that if the company s debt to equity ratio exceeds two to one the debentures cannot be converted unless the company obtains a bank guarantee or a letter of credit Nivison said IRT s debt to equity ratio currently is less than one to one The Chandler partner said that this type of deal gives the borrower funds that are less costly than if it had done an issue in the U S Domestic market
