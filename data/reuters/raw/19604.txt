FIRST CHICAGO FNB TO BUY WOOD GUNDY STAKE CHICAGO June First Chicago Corp said its First National Bank of Chicago subsidiary agreed in principle to acquire a pct interest in Wood Gundy Corp an international investment banking firm headquartered in Toronto Under the agreement the bank through its Canadian bank subsidiary First National Bank of Chicago Canada will invest mln canadian dlrs in a combination of newly issued common equity and convertible debentures of Wood Gundy First Chicago also agreed to invest additional funds jointly with Wood Gundy in venture capital and merchant banking businesses Wood Gundy shareholders will retain pct ownership Reuter
