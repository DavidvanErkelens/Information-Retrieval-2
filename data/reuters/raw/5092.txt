HEALTHCARE INTERNATIONAL HII GETS FUNDING AUSTIN Tex March Healthcare International Inc said it completed two financial transactions with HealthVest HVT a health care real estate investment trust The company said the first entailed receiveing approximately mln dlrs through a sale and leaseback arrangement on the property of Austin Diagnostic Clinic which it acquired in December The company also said it received mln dlrs in financing on its Healthcare Medical Center of Tustin in Orange County Calif The funds will be used to repay floating indebtedness the company said Reuter
