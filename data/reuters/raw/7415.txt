OPEC FUND APPROVES DLR AID FOR ECUADOR VIENNA March The OPEC Fund for International Development approved dlrs in aid for Ecuador which was struck recently by an earthquake The Fund which was set up by the OPEC Organization of Petroleum Exporting Countries member states to help Third World development said in a statement the money would be used to provide medical supplies for the earthquake victims Ecuador itself a member of OPEC suffered a major blow to its economy when the earthquake damaged a pipeline carrying crude oil its chief export earner Reuter
