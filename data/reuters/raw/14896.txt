HOKURIKU ELECTRIC ISSUES MLN DLR WARRANT BOND LONDON April Hokuriku Electric Industry is issuing a mln dlr equity warrant bond due May paying an indicated coupon of pct and priced at par lead manager Nomura International Ltd said The issue is guaranteed by Hokuriku Bank Ltd The selling concession is pct while management and underwriting combined pays pct Final terms are due April The payment date is May and the issue is available in denominations of dlrs Listing will be in Luxembourg REUTER
