MOODY S DOWNGRADES BANKAMERICA BAC NEW YORK March Moody s Investors Service Inc said it downgraded BankAmerica Corp and the unit BankAmerica Overseas Finance Corp but affirmed ratings of the units Bank of America N T and S A Seafirst Corp and Seattle First National Bank The actions affect billion dlrs of debt Moody s said the downgrade reflected its concern over prospects for material improvement in the holding company s operating profitability in the medium term Cut were BankAmerica s senior debt to Ba from Baa subordinated debt to Ba from Ba preferred stock to B from Ba and commercial paper to Not Prime from Prime BankAmerica is the second largest bank holding company in the U S Moody s reduced the subsidiary BankAmerica Overseas Finance s senior debt to Ba from Baa and subordinated debt to Ba from Ba That debt is guaranteed by the parent The rating agency also cited the holding company s continuing high level of problem assets including significant exposure to Latin American debtor countries that will continue to pressure profitability However Moody s noted that BankAmerica Corp remains liquid and owns considerable marketable assets Moody s said it affirmed the ratings of the Bank of America unit because of that bank s franchise value significant core deposit base and an expectation of regulatory support if further stresses develop The agency confirmed Bank of America s Baa long term rating and Prime short term rating as well as Seafirst s Ba senior debt and Seattle First s short and long term ratings of Baa and Prime respectively Reuter
