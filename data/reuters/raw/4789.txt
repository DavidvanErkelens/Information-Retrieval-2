SALLIE MAE SLM OFFERS FIXED RATE NOTES WASHINGTON March The Student Loan Marketing Association Sallie Mae announced an offering of mln dlrs of three year fixed rate notes redeemable at maturity in the U S dollar equivalent of mln Australian dlrs It said the pct obligation will be priced at par and sold in the United States in a public offering managed by Morgan Stanley Interest payments on the notes will be made semiannually in U S dlrs starting on Sept Sallie Mae said It added that the notes are due march Sallie Mae said it will enter into a separate currency exchange agreement to convert the principal payment at maturity from Australian dollars to U S dollars It said the notes will be issued in registered form and be available in denominations of U S dlrs and in dlr increments above that amount Sallie Mae said proceeds will be used for general corporate purchases Reuter
