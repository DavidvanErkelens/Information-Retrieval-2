HIGH POINT SELLS DEBT AND STOCK CONTRACTS NEW YORK March High Point Financial Corp is raising six mln dlrs through an offering of redeemable subordinated debentures due yielding pct said sole underwriter Ryan Beck and Co The debentures have an pct coupon and were priced at to yield basis points more than comparable Treasuries High Point also sold mln dlrs of cancellable mandatory stock purchase contracts that require the purchase on March of the company s common stock at dlrs per share High Point is trading at dlrs a share in the over the counter market according to Ryan Beck Reuter
