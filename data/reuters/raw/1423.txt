HOKKAIDO BANK ISSUES MLN DLR CONVERTIBLE BOND LONDON March The Hokkaido Bank Ltd is issuing a mln dlr convertible eurobond due March with an indicated pct coupon and priced at par lead manager Nomura International Ltd said Terms will be fixed on March involving a premium of about five pct above a six day average The conversion period will run from April to March The bonds will be sold in denominations of dlrs and will be listed in Luxembourg Total fees of pct include one pct for management and underwriting and pct for selling including an basis point praecipuum Nomura said the bonds are callable from March at declining by pct per annum There will be no calls before March unless the share price reaches at least pct of the conversion price REUTER
