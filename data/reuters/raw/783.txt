HOLIDAY CORP HIA TO REDEEM TWO BOND ISSUES NEW YORK March Holiday Corp said it would redeem on March all outstanding pct first mortgage bonds due of its Holiday Inns Inc unit and all pct first mortgage bonds Series A due of its Harrah s subsidiary The Holiday bonds will be bought back at pct of the bonds principal amount plus accrued interest or dlrs per dlr face amount Holiday will redeem the Harrah s bonds at pct of the principal amount plus accrued interest or dlrs per dlr face amount Reuter
