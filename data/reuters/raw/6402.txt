BOYCOTT FORCES NAKASONE TO ADOPT STOP GAP BUDGET TOKYO March An on off parliamentary boycott has forced Japanese Prime Minister Yasuhiro Nakasone to adopt a provisional budget government officials said Economic analysts saw this as another setback in Nakasone s plans to overhaul the Japanese tax system The parliamentary boycott by opposition parties over Nakasone s plans for a five per cent sales tax has made it impossible for the full budget to be passed by the start of the fiscal year on April government officials said Reuter
