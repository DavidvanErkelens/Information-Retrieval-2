MITSUI AND CO EUROBONDS FORMALLY LAUNCHED LONDON March The two mln dlr equity warrant eurobonds for Mitsui and Co Ltd reported earlier today from Tokyo have now been formally launched lead manager Nomura International Ltd said The first tranche matures on March and has an indicated coupon of pct while the second tranche matures on March and has a fixed coupon of three pct Both deals have an indicated pricing of par The selling concession for both deals is pct while management and underwriting combined pays pct Final terms on the deals will be fixed on March Reuter
