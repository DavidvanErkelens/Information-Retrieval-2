LSI LOGIC LLSI SETS MLN DLR DEBT OFFER MILPITAS Calif March LSI Logic Corp said it intends to offer mln dlrs of convertible subordinated debentures due outside the U S through Morgan Stanley International and Prudential Bache Capital Funding The company said it is issuing the debentures with an indicated coupon range of pct to pct and an indicated conversion premium range of pct The debentures will be convertible into common stock through the life of the bonds The company said the debt is expected to be listed on the Luxembourg Stock Exchange Reuter
