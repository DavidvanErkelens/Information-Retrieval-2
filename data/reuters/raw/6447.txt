FRENCH FINANCE GROUP SDR ISSUES DOMESTIC BOND PARIS March French regional financing group Societes de Developpement Regional is issuing a billion franc domestic bond Banque Nationale de Paris and Banque Indosuez said The issue will be in three tranches with BNP being the book keeper for a mln franc year pct fixed rate tranche issued at par Redemption will be in seven equal tranches after the eighth year and payment date is April The banks said Indosuez will be the book keeper for two non syndicated variable rate eight years and day tranches of mln francs each issued at par Interest will be based on the annual money market rate TAM with a TAM reference rate of pct and with a margin of minus pct It will be payable on April every year from and onwards Redemption is to be in six equal tranches after two years REUTER
