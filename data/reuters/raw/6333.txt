MALAYSIA SAYS IT DEPOSITED FUNDS IN AILING BANK KUALA LUMPUR March The Malaysian government has deposited mln ringgit with the financially troubled Cooperative Central Bank Bhd CCB since last October Deputy Finance Minister Sabaruddin Chik said He told Parliament the government has always supported CCB The statement is the government s first acknowledgment that it has put money into the bank CCB s management board has been changed twice since last June It reported pretax profit of mln ringgit in the half year to June down from mln ringgit a year earlier The bank did not release its net results New bank chairman Nik Hussein Abdul Rahman has said he asked police to investigate possible wrongdoing and malpractices related to loans made mainly to property developers and politicians The bank had a paid up capital of mln ringgit and deposits totalling billion at June last year Malaysia s Central Bank has rescued two large commercial banks United Asian Bank Bhd and Perwira Habib Bank Malaysia Bhd since late REUTER
