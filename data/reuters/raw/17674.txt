PACIFIC GAS PCG TO BUY BACK BONDS NEW YORK June Pacific Gas and Electric Co said it will buy back on July one mln dlrs of two mortgage bond issues Series A and B The redemption will save the company about mln dlrs in annual financing costs for the remaining lives of the two mortgage bond issues Pacific Gas said It will buy back the Series A bonds at dlrs per dlr principal amount plus one month s accrued interest of dlrs It will redeem the Series B at dlrs per dlr face amount plus one month s accrued interest of dlrs Reuter
