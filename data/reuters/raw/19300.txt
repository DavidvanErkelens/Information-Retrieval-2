MANUFACTURERS MHC SELLS YEAR EXTENDABLE NOTES NEW YORK June Manufacturers Hanover Corp is offering mln dlrs of two year senior extendable notes with a final maturity of yielding pct said lead manager Shearson Lehman Brothers Inc The notes have an pct coupon and were priced at to yield basis points more than the when issued two year Treasury note After two years holders can sell the notes back to Manufacturers at par In and every year until the issuer will conduct an open reset on the yields of those notes not sold back to it Shearson said Moody s Investors Service Inc rates the Manufacturers issue A compared to an A grade by Standard and Poor s Corp The gross spread is dlrs the selling concession is two dlrs and the reallowance is dlrs First Boston Prudential Bache and Salomon Brothers co managed the deal Reuter
