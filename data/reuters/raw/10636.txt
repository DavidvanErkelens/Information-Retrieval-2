FRENCH PUBLIC SECTOR TO GET FIVE BILLION FRANCS PARIS March The French public sector will get a total of five billion francs of investment from the billion francs which the government expects to raise through its privatisation program the Finance Ministry said in a statement Prime Minister Jacques Chirac said in a televised interview on Wednesday the successful program would net the government billion francs more than the billion orignally estimated Around pct of the additional revenue will be used to cut state debt and pct for public sector investment Of this investment envelope a total of francs will go towards industrial modernisation especially of the chemical sector and and a further francs for high tech industries such as the aeronautical and electronic sectors the Finance Ministry said France s state railways the SNCF will receive billion francs to speed up financing of the high speed TGV rail link to the Atlantic coast A total of two billion francs will go towards improving motorways around the country the ministry said REUTER
