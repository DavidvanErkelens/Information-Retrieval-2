EC INDUSTRIAL PRODUCTION GROWTH HALTS IN JANUARY LUXEMBOURG April Industrial production in the European Community EC in January showed no growth compared with January the EC statistics office Eurostat said in a report It said the EC industrial production index base stood at in both months EC officials said while the lack of growth may have been due in part to the icy weather which gripped large parts of the EC in January it also reflects a worrying trend Eurostat said for the last three months for which figures are known industrial output rose by only pct compared with the year earlier period Annual growth in the months to January averaged about pct well below the pct figure recorded in the months ending last June the office said REUTER
