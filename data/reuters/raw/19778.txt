GREYHOUND CORP G DEBT AFFIRMED BY MOODY S NEW YORK June Moody s Investors Service said it affirmed Greyhound Corp s Baa subordinated debt rating and Prime commercial paper rating Some mln dlrs of long term debt is affected Moody s said Greyhound s debt had been under review for possible downgrade since May when it agreed to acquire the in flight catering and airport terminal concession businesses of Carson Pirie Scott and Co for about mln dlrs Moody s said it expects Greyhound to experience generally improved results over the next several years due to recent strategic acquisitions It has reduced debt via asset sales Reuter
