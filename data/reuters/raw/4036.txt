rest of the year Finance Minister Domingo Cordovez said He said in a statement The idea with the foreign banks is to obtain from them the best terms to give the Ecuadorean economy a complete relief in the period of deferral of payments on the foreign debt during the present year The statement referred only to payments due to private foreign banks a senior government finance official told Reuters
