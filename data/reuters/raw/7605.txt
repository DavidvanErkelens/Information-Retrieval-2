ITALIAN JANUARY INDUSTRIAL OUTPUT FALLS PCT ROME March Italian industrial production fell pct in January compared with the same month last year the national statistics institute Istat said The rise follows a year on year increase in December of pct Istat s industrial production index base not seasonally adjusted registered compared with in January Istat said there were working days in January the same as December but one fewer than January last year Istat said the year on year fall reflected poorer performances in the footwear clothing textiles chemicals and metals industries It said office machinery and data sectors wood and furniture precision mechanics oil and electricity showed improved activity Calculations based on Istat figures showed industrial production rose pct in January on a month on month basis after falling pct in December over November REUTER
