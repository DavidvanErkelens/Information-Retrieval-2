COLLINS FOODS CF MOVES UP WARRANT CONVERSION LOS ANGELES March Collins Foods International Inc said it moved up the conversion date for its warrants to purchase common stock to April There are currently warrants outstanding the company said It said each warrant entitles the holder to buy one Collins common share at dlrs per share Collins also said the warrants were originally scheduled to expire on December But the agreement under which the warrants were issued permits the company to accelerate the date if the closing price of its common stock equals or exceeds dlrs per share for consecutive trading days the company said It also pointed out that the stock price closed at dlrs per share on March Reuter
