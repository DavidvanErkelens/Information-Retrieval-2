PROXMIRE SAYS RATE OF U S BANK CLOSINGS AT WASHINGTON March Senate Banking Committee chairman William Proxmire says the U S banking system is sound despite a rate of failures that could reach this year In a statement in the Congressional Record the Wisconsin Democrat said that as of March banks had failed At that pace some banks will go bankrupt before the year is out he said compared to last year worst since the depression Despite the statistics he said American banking today remains overall remarkably safe and prudent Reuter
