BNP PLANS MLN H K DLR CD ISSUE HONG KONG March Banque Nationale de Paris Hong Kong branch is planning a mln H K Dlr certificate of deposit issue with BT Asia Ltd as lead manager banking sources said They said this is the first capped FRCD issue in Hong Kong It is in two equal tranches Tranche A which matures January is payable April this year Tranche B which matures September is payable April Interest on both tranches is percentage point over three months Hong Kong interbank offered rate subject to a pct ceiling Management fee is basis points The CDs will be issued at par in denominations of dlrs each REUTER
