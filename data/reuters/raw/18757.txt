PHELPS DODGE PD MAY BE UPGRADED BY MOODY S NEW YORK June Moody s Investors Service Inc said it may upgrade the ratings on Phelps Dodge Corp s mln dlrs of debt securities The agency cited Phelps Dodge s progress in restoring profitability in a difficult environment for the copper industry Moody s said it will study the company s prospects for continued improvements in debt protection measurements It noted that copper prices recently increased Under review for possible upgrade are Phelps Dodge s Ba sinking fund debentures and industrial revenue bonds and Ba convertible exchangeable preference shares Reuter
