SOUTHDOWN SDW DEBT DOWNGRADED BY MOODY S NEW YORK March Moody s Investors Service Inc said it downgraded to Ba from Baa Southdown Inc s mln dlrs of senior debt The agency also assigned an initial rating of B to the company s mln dlrs of senior subordinated notes due Moody s said the action reflected increased leverage and reduced interest resulting from lower earnings It also cited Southdown s repurchase of pct of its common stock and the financing of that transaction by debt Moody s said the lower ratings anticipate certain debt reduction in the coming year from the sale of assets Reuter
