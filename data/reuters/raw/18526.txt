FRENCH OFFICIAL RESERVES RISE IN MAY PARIS June French official reserves rose mln francs in May to billion francs from billion at the end of April the Finance Ministry said The slight rise partly reflected the repayment last month of France s outstanding billion francs of debt owed to the European Monetary Cooperation Fund FECOM It also reflected a negative mln franc position with France s Exchange Stabilisation Fund after intervention purchases of billion francs during May Foreign currency holdings fell billion francs to billion while ECU holdings fell by billion francs to billion due to the repayment of part of France s debt owed to the FECOM and Stabilisation Fund operations the ministry said Claims on the IMF rose billion francs to billion including a mln franc increase in France s reserve position with the IMF and a mln franc rise in holdings of Special Drawing Rights Gold holdings were unchanged at billion francs REUTER
