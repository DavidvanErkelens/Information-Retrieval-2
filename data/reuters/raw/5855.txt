TORONTO DOMINION ISSUES BILLION YEN EUROBOND LONDON March Toronto Dominion Bank is issuing a billion yen eurobond due April with a pct coupon and priced at lead manager Nomura International Ltd said The bonds will be listed in London and will be issued in denominations of one mln yen Fees consist of pct for management and underwriting including a basis point praecipuum and pct for selling Pay date is April REUTER
