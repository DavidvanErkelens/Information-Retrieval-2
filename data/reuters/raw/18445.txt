PASCO CORP ISSUES MLN DLR EQUITY WARRANT BOND LONDON June Pasco Corp is issuing an mln dlr equity warrant eurobond due July with an indicated coupon of pct and par pricing lead manager Nomura International Ltd said The issue is guaranteed by Mitsubishi Bank Ltd and final terms will be set on June The selling concession is pct while management and underwriting combined pay pct The issue is available in denominations of dlrs and will be listed in Luxembourg Payment date is July and the warrants are exercisable from July until June REUTER
