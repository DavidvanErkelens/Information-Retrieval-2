BONUS WHEAT FLOUR FOR NORTH YEMEN USDA WASHINGTON Feb The Commodity Credit Corporation CCC has accepted an export bonus offer to cover the sale of long tons of wheat flour to North Yemen the U S Agriculture Department said The wheat four is for shipment March May and the bonus awarded was dlrs per tonnes and will be paid in the form of commodities from the CCC inventory The bonus was awarded to the Pillsbury Company The wheat flour purchases complete the Export Enhancement Program initiative announced in April it said Reuter
