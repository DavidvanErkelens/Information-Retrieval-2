CHICAGO PACIFIC CPAC SELLS CONVERTIBLE DEBT NEW YORK April Chicago Pacific Corp is raising mln dlrs through an offering of convertible subordinated debentures due with a pct coupon and par pricing said lead manager Goldman Sachs and Co The debentures are convertible into the company s common stock at dlrs per share representing a premium of pct over the stock price when terms on the debt were set Non callable for two years the issue is rated B by Moody s Investors Service Inc and B by Standard and Poor s Corp First Boston Corp and Lazard Freres and Co co managed the deal Reuter
