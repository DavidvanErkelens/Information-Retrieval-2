U S FARM CREDIT BANKS DEFER APRIL OFFERING NEW YORK March The Farm Credit Funding Corp said it will not issue bonds as scheduled on April because of a decline in borrower needs for term credit A total of billion dlrs of bonds maturing on April will be retired with excess cash and internally generated funds The next term debt offering is scheduled for June with an announcement covering the terms due on May The farm credit system s reduced need for medium to long term financing also enabled it to cancel bond issues scheduled for January and last October Reuter
