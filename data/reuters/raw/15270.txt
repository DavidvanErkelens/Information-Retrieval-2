CCC ACCEPTS EXPORT BID FOR WHEAT FLOUR TO IRAQ WASHINGTON April The Commodity Credit Corporation has accepted a bid for an export bonus to cover a sale of tonnes of wheat flour to Iraq the U S AGriculture Department said The bonus awarded was dlrs per tonne and will be paid to Peavey Company in the form of commodities from CCC stocks The wheat flour is for delivery May June the department said An additional tonnes of wheat flour are still available to Iraq under the Export Enhancement Program initiative announced January USDA said Reuter
