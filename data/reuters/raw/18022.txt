CANON SALES ISSUES MLN DLR WARRANT BOND LONDON June Canon Sales Co Inc is issuing a mln dlr equity warrant bond due June with an indicated coupon of pct and par pricing lead manager Yamaichi International Europe Ltd said The issue is guaranteed by Fuji Bank Ltd and final terms will be set on June The selling concession is pct while management and underwriting combined pays pct The deal is available in denominations of dlrs and will be listed in Luxembourg The warrants are exercisable from July until June The payment date is June REUTER
