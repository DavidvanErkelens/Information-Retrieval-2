INDIA TOOK THREE CARGOES OF RBD OLEIN AT TENDER LONDON March The Indian State Trading Corporation STC bought three cargoes of rbd palm olein at its vegetable oil import tender yesterday traders said Late yesterday the market was reporting it had bought one to two cargoes The business comprised two tonne cargoes for March April shipments at dlrs per tonne cif and tonnes for May at dlrs The first two cargoes were on a cash basis and the May position was transacted on a pct counter trade basis traders said REUTER
