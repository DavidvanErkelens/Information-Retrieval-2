CANADA DEVELOPMENT UNIT CDC TO REFINANCES SARNIA Ontario Oct Canada Development Corp said its Polysar Ltd unit completed a refinancing package worth about mln Canadian dlrs The company said the financing which involves banks and four syndicated loans consists of a mln Canadian dlr revolver a mln Canadian dlr European medium term loan a mln Canadian dlr revolver and a mln Canadian dlr operating loan The company said the refinancing will reduce borrowing costs in addition to having other benefits Reuter
