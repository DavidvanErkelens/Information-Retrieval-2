BALLY BLY FILES SHELF FOR PREFERRED STOCK CHICAGO June Bally Manufacturing Corp said it filed a shelf registration with the Securities and Exchange Commission for a secondary public offering of two mln shares of Series D convertible exchangeable preferred stock The stock is exchangeable at Bally s option into the company s convertible debentures due February one and is convertible at the holder s option into shares of Bally common The preferred stock carry a liquidation value of dlrs a share All of the shares are being offered by holders of Bally who bought the preferred stock in a private placement in February Bally will not receive any proceeds of the offering it said Reuter
