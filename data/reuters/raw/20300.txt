SHAMROCK CAPITAL COMPLETES CENTRAL SOYA SALE BURBANK Calif Oct Shamrock Capital L P a limited partnership led by Shamrock Holdings Inc said it completed its sale of Central Soya Co Inc to Ferruzzi Agricola Finanziaria of Italy Under terms of the sale agreement which was announced on September Ferruzzi acquired all the equity in Central Soya and assumed subordinated term debt of about mln dlrs in a transaction valued at about mln dlrs Reuter
