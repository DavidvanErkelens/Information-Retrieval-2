K O P EQUITY WARRANT BOND EXPECTED TO BE SWAPPED LONDON March The proceeds from yesterday s mln dlr equity warrant for Kansallis Osake Pankki K O P will probably be swapped into floating rate dollars once the final terms have been set for the deal executive vice president Erkki Karmila told a press conference The seven year issue has an indicated coupon of to pct and is priced at par Final terms will be set on April Karmila said that in the short term K O P did not need additional capital funds but the issue gives us access to attractive funds REUTER
