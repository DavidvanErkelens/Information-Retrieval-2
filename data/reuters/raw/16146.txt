CHINA RAISES GRAIN PURCHASE PRICES PEKING April China has raised the state purchase prices of corn rice cottonseed and shelled peanuts from April to encourage farmers to grow them the official China Commercial Daily said The paper said the price paid for corn from northern provinces cities and regions has increased by one yuan per kg A foreign agricultural expert said the rise will take the price to fen per jin kg from fen The paper said the price for long grained rice from southern provinces and cities was raised by yuan per kg The paper said the price for round grained rice from provinces regions and cities in central east and northwest China has been increased by yuan per kg It gave no more price details It said local authorities must inform farmers of the price increases before farmers begin planting to encourage production of grains and oilseeds Chinese officials have said farmers are unwilling to grow grain because they can earn more from other crops REUTER
