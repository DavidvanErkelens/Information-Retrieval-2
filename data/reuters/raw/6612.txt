AMERICAN EXPRESS AXP SIGNS DEBT EQUITY DEAL NEW YORK March American Express Bank Ltd a unit of American Express Co said it has agreed with the Mexican government and local business groups to convert mln dlrs of existing debt into equity to fund the construction of hotel rooms around Mexico American Express said in a statement that the swap will be made via its International Capital Corp unit over two years The Mexican government said in a statement that the deal should result in about extra tourists per year the creation directly and indirectly of more than new jobs and annual foreign exchange gains of over mln dlrs Reuter
