U S FEB DURABLE GOODS ROSE PCT WASHINGTON March New orders for durable goods received by U S manufacturers rose billion dlrs or pct in February to billion dlrs the Commerce Department said Excluding defense orders rose pct compared with a revised January decline of pct In January durable goods fell a revised pct instead of the previously reported pct Durables excluding defense were reported originally as having fallen pct in January The Commerce Department on Monday revised orders statistics for to to reflect more current inventory valuation methods and the February orders statistics are consistent with the revisions officials said The February order increase was led by transportation equipment up pct after an pct decline in January Orders for defense capital goods rose pct to billion dlrs following a pct decline in January Non defense capital goods orders fell pct in February to billion dlrs after falling pct in January the department said Electrical machinery orders rose in February by pct to billion dlrs after falling pct in January Primary metals orders were up pct to billion dlrs after a pct decline in January the department said New orders for non electrical machinery were up in February by pct to billion dlrs after a three pct orders decline in January Reuter
