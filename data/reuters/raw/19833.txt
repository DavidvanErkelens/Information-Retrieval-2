WORLD BANK APPROVES LOAN FOR BRAZIL RAILWAY WASHINGTON June The World Bank said it approved a mln dlr loan to help Brazil finance the rehabilitation of the railway in Sao Paulo state and establish an institutional reform program as a base for future commercial operation The mln dlr project is designed to financially rehabilitate the railway the primarily state owned Ferrovia Paulista S A and improve transport on the lines leading to the city of Sao Paulo and the port of Santos The loan is for years including years of grace and carries a variable interest rate currently pct linked to the bank s cost of borrowing Reuter
