FRANCE S FSGT ISSUES BILLION FRANC BOND PARIS March The Fonds Special de Grands Travaux FSGT is issuing a billion franc pct year domestic bond at pct lead manager Societe Generale said The bond guaranteed by the state will carry warrants exchangeable for variable rate bonds with interest based on the average monthly yield of long term state bonds TME Denominations are of francs nominal and the first coupon of francs will be paid on March Both the bonds and the warrants will be quoted on the Paris Bourse Reuter
