DELTA DENTAL GETS MLN DLR PENTAGON CONTRACT WASHINGTON April Delta Dental Plan of San Francisco has been awarded a mln dlr contract to provide dental services to families of military personnel in the United States Puerto Rico and the Virgin Islands the Defense Department said The plan is a private non profit health organization which operates in states The contract is the first let by the Office of Civilian Health and Medical Program of the Uniformed Services CHAMPUS in a move to provide more cost effective and efficient health care for dependents of U S military personnel REUTER
