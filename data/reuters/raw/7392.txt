YAMAKAWA MANUFACTURING TO BUILD U S PLANT PORTLAND Tenn March Yamakawa Manufacturing Corp of America said it began construction of a mln dlrs manufacturing plant in Portland Tenn The plant will produce metal parts for automobile manufacturers including Nissan Motor Manufacturing Corp USA Long term plans call for the addition of stamping capabilities and pressed plastic parts to its product line Assembly operations are expected to begin in October The plant is being built to employ people Yamakawa is a wholly owned subsidiary of Yamakawa Industrial Co Ltd of Japan Reuter
