AMERICAN EXPLORATION AXCO RAISES MLN DLRS HOUSTON April American Exploration Co said it raised mln dlrs for its property acquisition program and has spent all but mln dlrs of that total on acquiring property American Exploration said the mln dlrs raised for its APPL IV program represented the largest amount raised for any of its APPL programs It said it will attempt to raise more capital in its upcoming APPL V program American said it raises money from outside sources and then uses the funds to buy on and off shore oil and gas producing properties mostly in Texas and Louisiana Reuter
