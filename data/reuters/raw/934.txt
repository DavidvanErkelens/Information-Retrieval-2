FRENCH BOND COMMITTEE APPROVES THREE ISSUES PARIS March The French domestic bond issuing committee said it has approved three new issues totalling billion francs Credit Lyonnais will lead manage a billion franc issue for the Caisse Centrale de Credit Cooperatif Banque Francaise de l Agriculture will lead with Union de Garantie et de Placement a mln franc issue for its own account Caisse Centrale des Banques Populaires will lead a mln issue for the Groupement des Industries pour le Batiment et Travaux Publics No further details were immediately available REUTER
