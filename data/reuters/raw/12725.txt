BRAZIL BUYS FRENCH WHEAT AT TENDER RIO DE JANEIRO April Brazil bought tonnes of French wheat at tonight s tender a Brazilian Wheat Board spokesman said For May shipment it accepted offers from J Souflet for tonnes of wheat at dlrs Fob per tonne For June shipment Brazil bought tonnes from J Souflet also at dlrs Fob per tonne The next tender is set for April for May June and July shipment the spokesman said Reuter
