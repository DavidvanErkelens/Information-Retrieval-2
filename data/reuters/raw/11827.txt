BANK OF TOKYO ISSUES CONVERTIBLE EUROBOND LONDON April The Bank of Tokyo Ltd is issuing a mln dlr convertible eurobond due March paying an indicated coupon of two pct and priced at par lead manager Bank of Tokyo International Ltd said The coupon will be fixed on April while the conversion price and foreign exchange rate will be set on April The selling concession is pct while management and underwriting each pay pct The issue is available in denominations of dlrs and is convertible from May until March The payment date is April and there will be a short first coupon period REUTER
