WORLD BANK LOANS PORTUGAL MLN DLRS WASHINGTON June World Bank said it has loaned Portugal mln dlrs to help improve its highway system The bank loan is part of a mln dlr three year government financed project to rehabilitate or improve about miles of highway the bank said It also said the project hopes to increase road safety by improving signs aiding enforcement of axle load legislation and adding patrol cars and communications equipment The loan is for years including three years of grace at the standard variable interest rate currently pct the bank said Reuter
