ASEA GROUP AWARDED MLN DLR POWER CONTRACT NEW YORK June Asea Group AB ASEAY said it has been awarded a mln dlr contract from the Swedish State Power Board and the Finnish utility Imitran Oma Oy The company said the contract is for a megawatt kv fenno skan high voltage DC transmission to be built between Sweden and Finland The company also said three other Asea companies Asea Transmission Ludvika Sweden and Strondberg Vaasa in Finland received a related order for converter equipment Reuter
