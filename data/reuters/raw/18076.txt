AMERICAN MICRO SIGNS THREE YEAR SERVICE PACT TUSTIN Calif June American Micro Technology Co said it signed a three year installation and service agreement with TRW Inc TRW Under the agreement TRW initially will provide on site installation and service of AMT s full line of PC compatible products in a mile radius of major cities which represent more than two thirds of the PC market American Micro said The company said the pact allows it to pursue government bids which require installation and service support Reuter
