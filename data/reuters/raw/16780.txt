VAN OMMEREN PLANS MLN GUILDER BOND ISSUE AMSTERDAM April The Dutch transport trading and storage firm PHS Van Ommeren NV OMMN AS said it plans a mln guilder bond issue with warrants attached The bond will have a maturity of years with repayment in the second five year period in equal yearly instalments Each bond of guilders nominal has five A warrants and B warrants attached Each warrant entitles the holder to acquire one non cancellable share certificate The exercise period of the A warrants will be three years and of the B warrants five years Coupon and price of the bond issue as well as the exercise price of the warrants will be announced April after the close of the Amsterdam Stock Exchange REUTER
