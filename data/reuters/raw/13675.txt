LME LEAD MIDSESSION FIRMER ON CASH METAL DEMAND LONDON April Trade house pricing purchases of cash metal at the end of the official ring gave the LME lead market a firmer tone this morning dealers said The cash metal price rose from up to stg before the pricing demand was satisfied The backwardation widened to around six stg a tonne from three stg yesterday Some buying interest was stimulated for three months delivery up to stg and the dlrs equivalent but dealers generally reckoned this was range trading and expected resistance to develop around stg or dlrs REUTER
