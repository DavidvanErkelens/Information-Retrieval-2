DOW DOW TO ISSUE MLN DLRS IN BONDS MIDLAND MICH March Dow Chemical Co said it will issue mln New Zealand dollar denominated bonds worth approximately mln U S dlrs at current exchange rates It said the two year bonds will offer a floating interest rate which will not be less than pct in New Zealand dollars Proceeds from the bonds issued under the company s shelf registration will be swapped for U S funds resulting in an all in cost to Dow fixed at basis points below the two year U S Treasury rate Reuter
