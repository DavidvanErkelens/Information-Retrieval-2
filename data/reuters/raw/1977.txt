GUNMA BANK ISSUES MLN DLR CONVERTIBLE EUROBOND LONDON March The Gunma Bank Ltd is issuing a mln dlr convertible eurobond due March paying an indicated pct and priced at par lead manager Nomura International Ltd said The issue is callable from March at pct declining by pct per annum to par thereafter but is not callable until unless the share price exceeds pct of the conversion price The selling concession is pct while management and underwriting combined pays one pct Final terms will be fixed on March The issue is available in denominations of dlrs and will be listed in Luxembourg The payment date is March The conversion period is from April until March REUTER
