BURLINGTON INDUSTRIES BUR SELLS CONVERTIBLES NEW YORK April Burlington Industries Inc is raising mln dlrs through an offering of convertible subordinated debentures due with a pct coupon and par pricing said lead manager Kidder Peabody and Co Inc The debentures are convertible into the company s common stock at dlrs per share representing a premium of pct over the stock price when terms on the debt were set Non callable for three years the issue is rated Ba by Moody s Investors Service Inc and BBB by Standard and Poor s Corp Merrill Lynch Capital Markets and Salomon Brothers Inc co managed the deal Reuter
