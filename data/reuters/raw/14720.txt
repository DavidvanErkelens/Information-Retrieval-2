JORDAN BOOSTS BIDS IN EXPORT BONUS WHEAT TENDER KANSAS CITY April Jordan has boosted its bids for U S soft and hard wheat under Export Bonus private export sources said Jordan today bid dlrs per tonne for soft wheat and dlrs per tonne for hard red winter wheat in its tender for tonnes of hard and soft wheat for April through November shipment Earlier today USDA rejected its bids of dlrs for both varieties they said Reuter
