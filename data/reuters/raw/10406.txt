ELDERS RESOURCES ISSUE TO RAISE MLN DLRS MELBOURNE March Elders Resources Ltd said its two for seven convertible unsecured notes issue will raise mln dlrs for group expansion and development The pct owned Elders IXL Ltd ELXA S offshoot announced the issue at dlrs each with its first half results statement The group lifted net earnings to mln dlrs in the half ended December from mln a year earlier when it was still in its formative stages The pct notes are convertible to one share on May and November of each year from next November Reuter
