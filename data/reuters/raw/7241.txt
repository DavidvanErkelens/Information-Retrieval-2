MCORP M TO CONVERT MEMBER BANKS TO BRANCHES DALLAS March MCorp said it plans to convert of its member banks to branches during the next nine months It said it expects the conversions pending regulatory approval of branching applications will provide substantial long term benefits for its customers and shareholders In connection with the conversions MCorp said it also has realigned its operating structure and named eight group chairmen It said the group chairmen with its chairman and president will comprise its management committee Reuter
