EC FREES MORE U K INTERVENTION WHEAT FOR SALE LONDON April The EC Cereals Management Committee agreed in Brussels today that tonnes of U K Intervention wheat would be made available for sale on the U K Home market over the next three months the Ministry of Agriculture said The committee agreed that tonnes of wheat would be made available on April followed by a further tonnes later in the month It also gave an assurance that a further tonnes would be allocated for both May and June which would complete the tonnes originally requested by the U K The ministry said sales out of intervention for July and August would be discussed later Earlier Paris trade sources said the EC Commission made available a further tonnes of British intervention feed wheat for sale to the domestic market Reuter
