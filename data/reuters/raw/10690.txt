SSANGYONG CONSTRUCTION RAISES MLN U S DOLLARS HONG KONG March Ssangyong Construction Co Ltd of South Korea has signed a mln U S Dlr financing package to fund the construction of a hotel and commercial complex in Jakarta the agent Wardley Ltd said The package guaranteed by the parent firm Ssangyong Cement Industrial Co Ltd includes a mln dlr medium term loan a five mln dlr revolving letter of credit facility and an mln dlr bonding facility The three year loan carries interest at point over three or six month Singapore interbank offered rate Sibor The LC facility costs basis points over Sibor and the bonding facility basis points The facility is being lead managed by Hongkong and Shanghai Banking Corp Managers are Arab Banking Corp Bank of Nova Scotia Gulf International Bank Istituto Bancario San Paolo di Torino National Bank of Kuwait Standard Chartered Asia Ltd and Sumitomo Finance Asia Ltd Co managers are Australian European Finance Corp Ltd and Cho Heung Bank A Wardley spokesman said the loan was well received by international banks which have a keen appetite for increasingly scarce Korean assets The loan was approved by the Korean Finance Ministry because it is for use outside Korea and will not raise the country s indebtedness he said REUTER
