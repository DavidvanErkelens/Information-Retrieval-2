JAPAN APPROVES AID FOR INDONESIA BRAZIL ALUMINIUM TOKYO June Japan s cabinet approved a plan to help financially troubled aluminium ventures in Indonesia and Brazil an official at the Ministry of International and Trade Industry MITI said Japan will invest billion yen in PT Indonesia Asahan Aluminium in addition to the billion yen already invested in the company The government and private interests will equally share the additional investment he said They will also provide equal shares in billion yen in new investment in the Albras Amazon aluminium project in Brazil in addition to the billion yen already invested The Japan Export Import Bank will cut its rates on loans to Asahan and Albras to about five pct from about seven pct the official said Interest rates on loans by Japan s private banks to the two projects are expected to be reduced to around five pct from the current seven to eight pct but an agreement has yet to be reached industry sources said Under the rescue scheme for Asahan in which billion yen has been invested Indonesia will also extend another billion yen to the company This will raise Indonesia s investment ratio to about pct from the current pct The Brazilian government has already agreed to invest an additional billion yen in Albras in which investment now totals billion yen but its stake will not change from pct the official said The sources said the rescue programs for the two projects were larger than earlier expected reflecting Japan s desire to help develop the economies of Indonesia and Brazil and to stabilise sources of aluminium Japan depends on imports for more than pct of its aluminium demand which totals some mln tonnes a year they said REUTER
