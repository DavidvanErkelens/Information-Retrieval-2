BUDAPEST BANK ISSUES BONDS TO EXPAND RESOURCES BUDAPEST March Budapest Bank Rt has become the first of the commercial banks operating since January to issue bonds to expand its resources the official Hungarian news agency MTI said The seven year bonds totalling mln forints may be taken up by companies cooperatives and enterprises MTI said The interest rate will always be points above that charged to commercial banks by the National Bank of Hungary for loans exceeding one year This year s rate will thus be pct The funds raised will be used to increase the bank s investment loan offer for small and medium sized companies REUTER
