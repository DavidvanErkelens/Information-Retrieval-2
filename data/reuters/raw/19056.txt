TAIWAN BUYS TONNES OF U S SOYBEANS TAIPEI June The joint committee of Taiwan s soybean importers awarded a contract to Cargill Inc of Minneapolis Minnesota for supply of one tonne cargo of U S Soybeans a committee spokesman told Reuters The cargo priced at U S Dlrs per tonne c and f Taiwan is set for delivery before July he said The committee cancelled a tender for another cargo because the prices offered by U S Suppliers were too high he added REUTER
