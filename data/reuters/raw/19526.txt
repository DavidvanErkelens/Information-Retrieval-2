BHP STEEL SEES BENEFITS FROM INVESTMENT IN MELBOURNE June The major benefits of BHP Steel International Group s big capital expenditure program should begin to be seen in its financial year ending next May Broken Hill Pty Co Ltd BRKN S officials said A decline in BHP Steel s net profit to mln dlrs in the year ended May from mln the year before contributed to the drop in attributable group earnings to mln dlrs from mln We look forward to a year of consolidation while continuing our strong business development initiatives BHP Steel chief executive officer David Rice told reporters Rice said BHP Steel s capital spending in totalled mln dlrs and that total expenditure would be about billion dlrs by the time the government s five year Steel Industry Plan expires at the end of calendar He said the investment program aimed at making BHP Steel both technically and commercially competitive on a world scale was now over its peak and would begin to scale down Operational and commissioning problems flowing from the investment program combined with the highest levels of industrial action since the steel plan started had contributed to the decline in earnings he said Rice said the industrial and production problems forced BHP Steel to import tonnes of steel in its financial year to keep faith with customers The new items of plant are now past their initial teething problems and talks have intensified with the unions on the industrial troubles he said Despite the profit drop in the year BHP Steel International group was one of the most profitable steel producers in the world on an after tax basis he said Its capacity utilisation was far higher at pct than the current western world average of pct he added REUTER
