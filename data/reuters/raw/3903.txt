USDA ACCEPTS BONUS WHEAT OFFERS FOR CHINA WASHINGTON March The Commodity Credit Corp CCC has accepted bids for export bonuses to cover sales of tonnes of hard red winter wheat and tonnes of soft red winter wheat to China the U S Agriculture Department said The bonuses awarded averaged dlrs per tonne and the wheat is scheduled for delivery during May Oct The bonus awards were made to Cargill Inc tonnes Continental Grain Co tonnes Mitsubishi International Corp tonnes and Richco Grain Co tonnes Bonuses will be paid to the exporters in the form of commodities from CCC stocks The purchases complete the Export Enhancement Program initiative for China announced Jan Reuter
