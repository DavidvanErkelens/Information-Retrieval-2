ITALIAN CERTIFICATE OFFER HAS HIGHER YIELDS ROME April The Italian Treasury said it would offer billion lire of indexed government discount certificates at rates slightly higher than on the preceding issue The seven year certificates carry a first year coupon payable April of pct identical to that on the preceding issue in March They will be offered at a base price of lire down from previously for an effective net annual yield on the first coupon of pct against in March Yields after the first year will be linked to rates on month Treasury bills Subscriptions to the issue close April REUTER
