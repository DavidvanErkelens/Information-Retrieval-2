MITSUBISHI TO ISSUE TWO CONVERTIBLE BONDS TOKYO April Mitsubishi Corp MITT T said it will issue a billion yen unsecured seven year convertible bond and a billion yen unsecured nine year convertible bond on the domestic capital market through public placement with Nikko Securities Co Ltd as lead manager Coupon and conversion price for both par priced bonds will be set at a board meeting to be held later this month and payment is due on May Mitsubishi said in a statement The seven year bond matures on September and the nine year bond on September Mitsubishi s share price fell yen to on the Tokyo Stock Exchange today REUTER
