FEDERAL PAPER FBO OFFERS PREFERENTIAL SHARES MONTVALE N J March Federal Paper Board Co Inc said its is offering shares or mln dlrs of its cumulative convertible preferred stock at dlrs per share The company said it is applying to list the preferred stock on the New York Stock Exchange The First Boston Corp Morgan Stanley and Co Inc and PaineWebber Inc are underwriting the issues the company said The preferred stock has a liquidation preference of dlrs per share and is convertible at any time into common stock at dlrs per share subject to adjustment The preferred stock is redeemable starting at dlrs per share on March and declining on each March to dlrs on and after March The company said it intends to used the proceeds from the sale to redeem its entire mln dlrs of its outstanding pct subordinated debentures due with the balance to be added to the company s general funds Reuter
