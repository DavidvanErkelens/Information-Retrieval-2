PORTUGAL ISSUES MLN FRENCH FRANC BOND PARIS June The Republic of Portugal is issuing a mln French franc variable rate bond due July at pct lead manager Credit Commercial de France said The issue of unsecured unsubordinated debt will have a coupon based on three month LIBOR plus basis points If on any interest date this is pct or more above three month Paris Interbank Offered Rates then the interest rate will be three month PIBOR plus basis points Payment date is July There is a call at par at the end of the first year and thereafter on any interest payment date Listing is Paris and denominations are of and francs Fees total basis points with points for selling and for management and underwriting combined including a six basis points praecipuum The issue is rated A by Moody s REUTER
