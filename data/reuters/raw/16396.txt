DUFF PHELPS DOWNGRADES MELLON BANK CORP MEL CHICAGO April Duff and Phelps today lowered the senior debt rating of Mellon Bank Corp to D P High BBB from D P middle A and lowered the preferred stock rating to D P middle BBB from D P low A These rating changes affect approximately mln dlrs of securities In addition the certificate of deposit rating of Mellon Bank has been lowered to Duff one minus from Duff one The rating affects approximately bln dlrs on CDs A sharp deterioration in asset quality resulting in a significant first quarter loss were cited in the change Reuter
