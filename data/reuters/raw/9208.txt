CHAD SAYS TROOPS KILLED LIBYANS IN BATTLE N DJAMENA March Chad s military high command said today troops killed Libyan soldiers in routing a strong force to capture Tripoli s major air base in northern Chad on Sunday In its first published casualty list from the fierce battle for Ouadi Doum air base the high command said Libyans were taken prisoner while Chadian soldiers were killed and wounded It said the prisoners included the regional commander Colonel Khalifa Abul Gassim Hastar while his deputy Colonel Gassim Ali Abu Nawar was among the dead The Chadian army also captured substantial amounts of weaponry including Czechoslovak made L bombers three Soviet MI fighter helicopters and a large number of tanks as well as hundreds of other vehicles armed with guns or anti aircraft missiles Following Sunday s battle French officers said the fall of Ouadi Doum deprived Libya of its only hard runway air base in Chad Its main strongpoint Faya Largeau some km miles north of the so called red line along the th parallel was left increasingly exposed The th parallel divides the central African country into government held zones in the south and mainly Libyan controlled areas in the north Reuter
