ROWNTREE ISSUES CONVERTIBLE EUROSTERLING BOND LONDON March Rowntree Mackintosh Plc RWNT L is issuing a mln stg eurobond due March paying an indicated coupon of pct to five pct and priced at par lead manager J Henry Schroder Wagg and Co Ltd said Final terms on the issue will be fixed by March Denominations are stg and listing will be London There is a put option on March to yield to nine pct and the bond is also callable by the borrower until March at pct declining by one pct per annum thereafter to par However the call will not be before REUTER
