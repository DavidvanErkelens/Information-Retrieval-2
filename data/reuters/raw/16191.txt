GERMAN METALWORKERS WAGE TALKS BEGIN WEDNESDAY FRANKFURT April Negotiations scheduled between the metalworkers IG Metall union and employers to resolve an impasse over a new contract will start on Wednesday the union said in a statement IG Metall is demanding a hour week with a parallel increase in wages of five pct Employers Gesamtmetall have offered a half hour shortening in the current working week to hours and an initial rise of pct IG Metall under newly elected leader Franz Steinkuehler proposed the talks on Thursday after regional negotiations in the northern part of the state of Baden Wuerttemberg collapsed REUTER
