MCDONNELL DOUGLAS MD GETS BIG THAI AIR ORDER NEW YORK June McDonnell Douglas Corp said it has received an order from Thai Airways International for one McDonnell Douglas DC Series ER aircraft and four MD long range trijets plus options for four more MD s The company said the nine aircraft together with two other DC ER s ordered earlier this year by Thai Air are worth about one billion dlrs It said the DC is scheduled for April delivery and the first two newly ordered MD s in September and November with the others two in October and November McDonnell Douglas said the DC will be powered by General Electric Co GE CF C engines and the MD s by GE CF C engines Reuter
