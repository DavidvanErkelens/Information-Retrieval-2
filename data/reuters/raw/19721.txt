U S CAR OUTPUT SEEN DROPPING IN RD QTR DETROIT June U S car production is scheduled to drop by pct to about mln in the third quarter from last year s mln as domestic automakers react to a drop in sales from last year s record levels trade paper Automotive News said Automotive News said General Motors Corp GM has slashed its planned car production by pct from last year s third quarter totals while Ford Motor Co F is scheduling its plants to build pct fewer cars and Chrysler Corp C will be off pct But the paper quoted analysts as saying the production should be cut by at least more units to avoid excessive inventory build up in view of a relatively soft market for new cars Through June sales of new domestic built cars are off pct from last year s levels Only Ford among the Detroit Big Three is enjoying a sales gain GM and Ford may be working to stockpile cars despite the market conditions because of the prospect of a strike in September by the United Automobile Workers union analysts said Reuter
