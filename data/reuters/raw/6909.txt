BOEING BA GETS MLN DLRS IN DEFENSE CONTRACTS WASHINGTON March Boeing Military Airplane Co a subsidiary of Boeing Co is being awarded two Air Force contracts totalling mln dlrs the Defense Department said It said the company was being awarded a mln dlr contract for B aircraft components engineering and data for a reengining program adding that the work is expected to be completed in January The department also said it awarded the company a mln dlr increase to a contract for work modifying two G B aircraft Reuter
