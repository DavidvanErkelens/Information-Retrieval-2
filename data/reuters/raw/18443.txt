MITSUBISHI HEAVY BUILDS ENERGY SAVING TANKER TOKYO June Mitsubishi Heavy Industries Ltd MITH T said it began building the world s most advanced energy saving tanker which consumes only tonnes of fuel oil a day Construction of the dwt VLCC very large crude carrier Nisseki Maru for Tokyo Tanker Co Ltd a shipping arm of Nippon Oil Co Ltd NPOL T is expected to be completed in April It would run on the Japan Gulf route a company statement said The statement gave no other details Mitsubishi Heavy last year finished building a vlcc of similar size but which consumed tonnes of fuel a day REUTER
