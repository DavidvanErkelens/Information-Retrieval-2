VANCOUVER EXCHANGE FEBRUARY VOLUME RISES VANCOUVER March The Vancouver Stock Exchange said February volume rose to mln shares up from mln a year ago and up pct from the previous volume record for the month set in The exchange said market value for the month was mln dlrs up from mln dlrs a year before and pct ahead of the previous record for the month set in It said year to date volume was mln shares up pct from a year before and value was up pct to mln dlrs Reuter
