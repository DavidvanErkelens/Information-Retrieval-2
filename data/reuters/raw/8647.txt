COPENHAGEN TELEPHONE MLN FRENCH FRANC ISSUE PARIS March Copenhagen Telephone Co Ltd is issuing a mln French franc year retractable adjustable bond lead manager Credit Commercial de France CCF said The bond will have an issue price of pct and for the first five years it will carry a nine pct coupon The final due date will be April and the issue will be both puttable and callable on April and at par It will be denominated in franc units and be listed in Luxembourg Co lead will be Privatbanken REUTER
