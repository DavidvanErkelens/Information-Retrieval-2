CREDIT NATIONAL ISSUES MLN DLR EUROBOND LONDON March Credit National is issuing a mln dlr eurobond due April with a pct coupon and priced at pct LTCB International said as lead manager The bonds guaranteed by the French government will be issued in denominations of dlrs and will be listed in Luxembourg Gross fees of pct comprise pct for management and underwriting combined and pct for selling REUTER
