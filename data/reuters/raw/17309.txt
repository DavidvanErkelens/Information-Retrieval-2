INVESTCORP SIGNS FOR MLN DLR CREDIT FACILITY BAHRAIN April Arabian Investment Banking Corp EC Investcorp signed for a mln dlr revolving credit facility one of the lead managers Arab Banking Corp ABC said The facility was first mandated for mln dlrs but was increased before syndication to mln when a lead group of six banks was formed Strong demand during syndication led to another increase in the final amount to mln dlrs ABC said the facility with a final lending syndicate of banks from countries is part of Investcorp s strategy to develop and diversify its source of funds and reduce its borrowing costs ABC as overall arranger said the facility will be used to finance Bahrain based Investcorp s general corporate funding requirements It can be exercised in three different ways These are through the issue of euronotes or advances by tender the extension of committed advances by underwriting banks or the issue of contingent obligations from a panel of selected banks The facility has a maturity of three years with interest on committed advances based on utilisation from basis points over London Interbank Offered Rates for one third to basis points for two thirds and for an amount above that level REUTER
