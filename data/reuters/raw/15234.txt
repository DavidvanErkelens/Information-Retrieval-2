SIEMENS AG UNIT AND NEC NIPNY UNIT IN PACT BOCA RATON Fla April Siemens Information Systems Inc a unit of Siemens AG of West Germany has reached an agreement in principle with NEC America Inc a unit of NEC Corp of Japan allowing Siemens Information s Tel Plus Communications Inc subsidiary to continue distributing NEC s NEAX Information Management System Siemens Information said the three year agreement will allow TPC to market all current and future NEAX models over the contract s term Reuter
