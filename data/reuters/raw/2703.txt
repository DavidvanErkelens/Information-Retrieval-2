REFINANCING SAID CLOSE FOR VENEZUELAN BANK Caracas March The Venezuelan government is close to agreement with foreign banks to refinance mln dlrs in foreign debt owed by the Banco de los Trabajadores de Venezuela BTV finance minister Manuel Azpurua said He told reporters Bankers Trust which heads the BTV s bank committee is consulting other creditors on an eight year refinancing plan with a margin of pct over libor He added there is a relending option for which the state owned Banco Industrial de Venezuela will act as agent BTV pct owned by the government and pct by unions was taken over by trustees in November with severe liquidity problems REUTER
