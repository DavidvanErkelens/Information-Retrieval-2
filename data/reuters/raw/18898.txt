EC INCREASES SPECIAL FEED WHEAT TENDERS TRADE LONDON June The European Community EC has increased the size of two special export tenders for British and West German feed wheat held in intervention stores and included South Korea as an acceptable destination traders said The tender was originally for tonnes of British and tonnes of West German feed wheat for shipment only to Poland But now both tranches have been increased by tonnes to tonnes with South Korea added as a possible destination Both tenders are open from June Reuter
