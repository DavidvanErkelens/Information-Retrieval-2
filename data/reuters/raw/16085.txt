MOBIL AUSTRALIA REPORTS MLN DLR LOSS MELBOURNE April Wholly owned Mobil Corp MOB unit Mobil Oil Australia Ltd reported a mln dlr net loss in a turnaround from its mln profit in The loss reflected a combination of strikes plus scheduled and unscheduled refinery shutdowns for maintenance and inventory losses caused by government controls on both crude and product prices Mobil said in a statement However equity accounting of associates profits reduced the loss to mln dlrs against a mln profit in Mobil said it was confident would see a return to profit as it built further on its company restructuring REUTER
