GOODMAN FIELDER UNIT ISSUES MLN STG CONVERTIBLE LONDON June Goodman Fielder U K Plc is issuing a mln stg convertible eurobond due July with an indicated coupon of five to pct and par pricing lead manager Credit Suisse First Boston Ltd said The issue is guaranteed by Goodman Fielder Ltd and final terms will be set on or before June The issue is callable at pct declining by one pct per annum to par thereafter but is not callable until unless the share price exceeds pct of the conversion price The selling concession is pct while management and underwriting each pay pct The issue has an investor put option in which will be priced to give a yield of approximately pct The expected conversion premium is to pct The issue is available in denominations of stg and will be listed in London The payment date is June and there will be a long first coupon period The conversion period is from July until June Over the weekend Goodman Fielder announced in Sydney that it had signed for a mln U S Dollar composite revolving euronote issuance facility to be launched in Hong Kong REUTER
