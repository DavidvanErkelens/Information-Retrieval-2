U S TREASURY SELLING BILLION DLRS OF NOTES WASHINGTON June The U S Treasury said it will auction billion dlrs of two four and seven year notes next week to raise a total of billion dlrs of new cash The financing will begin with the monthly auction on Tuesday June of billion dlrs of two year notes That will be followed on Wednesday and Thursday by respective quarterly sales of billion dlrs of four year notes and billion dlrs of seven year notes to refund billion dlrs of two year and four year notes maturing June The two year notes mature June The four years mature June with the seven years due July Reuter
