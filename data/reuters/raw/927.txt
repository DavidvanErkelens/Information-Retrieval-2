COUNCIL OF EUROPE ISSUES LUXEMBOURG FRANC BOND LUXEMBOURG March The Council of Europe is issuing a mln luxembourg franc private placement bond carrying a pct coupon and priced at par lead manager Banque Internationale a Luxembourg SA BIL said The non callable bullet issue maturing in August follows an earlier private placement bond announced last month for mln francs but with the same conditions The new issue is for payment on March and coupon payments annually on August with the first coupon a long coupon REUTER
