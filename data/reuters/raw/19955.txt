BELMORAL BME TO SETS MLN DLR UNIT ISSUE TORONTO June Belmoral Mines Ltd said it agreed in principle for the mln dlr sale of four mln units priced at dlrs each to investment dealer McLeod Young Weir Ltd subject to board and regulatory approvals Holders will be allowed to exchange the units after September for one common share and one common share purchase warrant of Belmoral the company said Each warrant holder will have the right to purchase one Belmoral common share for dlrs before January McLeod Young Weir plans to resell the units to institutional and private investors mainly in Europe Belmoral said Reuter
