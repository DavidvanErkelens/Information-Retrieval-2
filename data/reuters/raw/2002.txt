CCC ACCEPTS BONUS BID ON WHEAT FLOUR TO IRAQ WASHINGTON March The Commodity Credit Corporation CCC has accepted bids for export bonuses to cover sales of tonnes of wheat flour to Iraq the U S Agriculture Department said The department said the bonuses awarded averaged dlrs per tonne The shipment periods are March April tonnes and April May tonnes The bonus awards were made to Peavey Company and will be paid in the form of commodities from CCC stocks it said An additional tonnes of wheat flour are still available to Iraq under the Export Enhancement Program initative announced January the department said Reuter
