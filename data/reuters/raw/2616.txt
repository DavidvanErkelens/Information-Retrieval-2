CANADA UPHOLDS COUNTERVAIL DUTY ON U S CORN OTTAWA March The Canadian Import Tribunal ruled today subsidized U S corn imports were injurious to Canadian growers and upheld a countervailing duty of U S cts a bushel set earlier this year The ruling is the result of trade action launched in by the Ontario Corn Producers Association that contended U S subsidies were driving American corn prices below Canadian production costs A spokesman for the Department of Revenue said a study of the level of the duty will be undertaken in the next six months to determine if it should be adjusted Reuter
