SMITH AND NEPHEW ISSUES CONVERTIBLE EUROBOND LONDON March Smith and Nephew Associated Companies Plc is issuing an mln stg convertible eurobond due May paying a fixed coupon of four pct and priced at par lead manager Credit Suisse First Boston Ltd said The issue is callable at pct declining by one pct per annum to par thereafter but is not callable until May unless the share price exceeds the conversion price by pct or the issue is more than pct converted There is a put option on May which will give the investor an indicated annual yield to the put of to pct Final terms will be fixed on or before April The selling concession is pct while management and underwriting each pay pct The payment date is May The expected conversion premium is eight to pct and the issue is convertible into Smith and Nephew ordinary shares REUTER
