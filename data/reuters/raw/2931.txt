PUGET SOUND POWER PSD REDEEMS MORTGAGE BONDS NEW YORK March Puget Sound Power and Light Co said its board authorized the redemption on April of the outstanding principal amount of mln dlrs of first mortgage bonds The issues included are mln dlrs of pct series due at the regular redemption price of pct mln dlrs of the pct series due at the redemption price of pct and mln dlrs of the pct series due at pct Accrued interest will be added in each case Reuter
