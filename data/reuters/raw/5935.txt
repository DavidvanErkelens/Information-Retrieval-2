NUMAC OIL NMC HAS MLN DLR FINANCING Edmonton Alberta March Numac Oil and Gas Ltd said it arranged a mln dlr financing with two investment dealers The financing includes mln dlrs of convertible subordinated debentures and mln common shares at a price of dlrs per share for a total of mln dlrs Wood Gundy Inc and Gordon Capital Corp agreed to buy the securities The debentures will be for a term of years with a seven pct coupon and convertible into common shares for years at dlrs per share The debentures are not redeemable for three years Numac said Reuter
