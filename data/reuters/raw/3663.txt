PENNSYLVANIA POWER PPL OFFERS PREFERRED STOCK ALLENTOWN Pa March Pennsylvania Power and Light Co said it will offer shares of pct series preferred stock at dlrs per share and shares of pct series preferred stock at the same price plus accumulated dividends from the date of original issuance The First Boston Corp Drexel Burnham Lambert Inc Merrill Lynch Capital Markets and Prudential Bache Capital Funding are the underwriters offering the stock said Pennsylvania Power The pct series preferred stock is redeemable at any time at the option of the company at pct per share on or before March and declining to pct on and after April the company said The company added that no optional redemption may be made prior to April with or in anticipation of borrowed funds or senior or parity stock having an effective cost of less than pct per annum The pct series preferred stock will have a mandatory sinking fund in the amount of shares in each year commencing on April calculated to retire the issue no later than April The company has the non cumulative option to retire an additional shares on any sinking fund payment date it said Pennsylvania Power said the pct series preferred stock is redeemable at any time also at the option of the company at dlrs per share on or before March and declining to pct on and after April The company said that no optional redemption may be made prior to April with or in anticipation of borrowed funds or senior or parity stock having an effective cost of less than pct per annum Pennsylvania Power said the pct series preferred stock will have a mandatory sinking fund of shares in each year commencing April calculated to retire the issue no later than April It said it has the non cumulative option to retire an additional shares on any sinking fund payment date Reuter
