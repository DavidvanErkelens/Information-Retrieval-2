PECHINEY TO SELL PART OF STAKE IN CANADA PLANT PARIS April French state owned aluminium and special metals group Pechiney PUKG PA is planning to sell pct of a Canadian aluminium plant at Becancour to the U S s Reynolds Metals Co RLM N over the coming weeks the company said Pechiney currently holds a majority pct stake in the plant The company also said it planned an agreement soon with Italy s La Metalli Industriale a pct owned subsidiary of Societa Metallurgica Italiana related to cupreous products No further details were available REUTER
