PAPERBOARD TO SELL MLN DLRS OF NOTES TORONTO April Paperboard Industries Corp said it agreed to sell mln dlrs of convertible notes with half to be purchased by brokers Wood Gundy Inc and McLeod Young Weir Ltd and the other half by controlling shareholder Kinburn Industrial Corp The notes will be convertible into six mln common shares at dlrs a share by September Paperboard now has shares outstanding Proceeds will be used partly to finance the previously announced acquisition of Belkin Inc Purchase of notes will be conditional on Paperboard acquiring pct of Belkin s shares from Balaclava Enterprises Ltd Reuter
