GERMAN COMPANY INSOLVENCIES DOWN IN JANUARY WIESBADEN West Germany March West German corporate insolvencies totalled in January pct less than in the same month of the Federal Statistics Office said The number of insolvencies fell by pct in the building industry and by four pct in manufacturing industry but rose by pct in the services sector Overall insolvencies including those of private individuals totalled in January pct down on the same month of last year REUTER
