SALOMON OFFERS UNITS OF VAN DUSEN AIRPORT L P NEW YORK March Salomon Brothers Inc acting as sole underwriter said it is publicly offering units of Van Dusen Airport Services Co L P The offering consists of mln dlrs of pct senior subordinated notes due and contingent payment obligations The notes are priced at par or dlrs each A unit has one par value note and one contingent payment obligation The notes are redeemable by Van Dusen after March Salomon said A payment obligation entitles the holder to receive a cash payment under certain events or to certain repurchase rights Reuter
