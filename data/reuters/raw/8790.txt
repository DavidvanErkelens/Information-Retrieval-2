HYDRAULIC THC SPLITS FOR HIKES DIVIDEND BRIDGEPORT Conn March The Hydraulic Co said its board approved a three for two stock split of its common stock and increased its quarterly cash dividend It said the stock split will occur through a pct stock distribution on Hydraulic s common stock payable April to stockholders of record on April The quarterly cash dividend payable April to stockholders of record on April is to be paid on Hydraulic s pre split shares that are currently outstanding the company said The dividend will be cts per share up from cts per share It will represent a quarterly common stock cash dividend of cts per share on the share that will be outstanding after the stock split the company said Reuter
