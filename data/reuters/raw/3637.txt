MICROPOLIS MLIS SELLS CONVERTIBLE DEBENTURES NEW YORK March Micropolis Corp is raising mln dlrs via an offering of convertible subordinated debentures due with a six pct coupon and par pricing said lead manager Kidder Peabody and Co Inc The debentures are convertible into the company s common stock at dlrs per share representing a premium of pct over the stock price when terms on the debt were set Non callable for two years the issue is rated B minus by Standard and Poor s Robertson Colman and Stephens co managed the deal Reuter
