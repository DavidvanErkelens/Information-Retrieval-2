USDA REJECTS SRI LANKA S U S DLR WHEAT PRICE COLOMBO March Sri Lankan Food Department officials said the U S Department of Agriculture rejected a U S Firm s offer of U S Dlrs per tonne CAF to supply tonnes of soft wheat to Colombo from the Pacific Northwest They said Sri Lanka s Food Department subsequently made a counter offer to five U S Firms to buy wheat at U S Dlrs CAF for April delivery The company which obtains USDA approval for the proposed price must inform the Department before gmt they said REUTER
