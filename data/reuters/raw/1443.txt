JAPAN FIRM DEVELOPS ULTRA HEAT RESISTING RUBBER TOKYO March Shin Etsu Chemical Co Ltd said it had developed an ultra thermal resistance rubber which can be used at centigrade continuously without losing its properties Shin Etsu put potential demand for the product at two tonnes a month mainly for microwave ovens and as a sealant But increasing use of the product in parts attached to car engines will raise demand and the company will boost output to tonnes by March from two now he said Reuter
