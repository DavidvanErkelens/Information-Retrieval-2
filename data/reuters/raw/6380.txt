DUNHILL HOLDINGS ARRANGES MLN DLR STG PROGRAM LONDON March Dunhill Holdings Plc a subsidiary of Rothmans International PLC ROT L has appointed Chemical Bank International Ltd as sole dealer for a mln U S Dlr and sterling commercial paper program Chemical said The paper will be sold in denominations of dlrs and have maturities of seven to days Chemical Bank London branch will act as issuing and paying agent The financing is the first of its kind for the company which makes luxury consumer products REUTER
