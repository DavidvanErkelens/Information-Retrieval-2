TOYO TRUST ISSUES MLN DLR CONVERTIBLE BOND LONDON April Toyo Trust and Banking Co Ltd is issuing a mln dlr convertible eurobond due March paying an indicated two pct and priced at par lead manager Toyo Trust International said The issue is callable after three years at pct declining by pct per annum to par thereafter It is not callable unless the share price exceeds the conversion price by pct The selling concession is pct while management and underwriting each pay pct Final terms will be fixed on April The payment date is April and there will be a short first coupon period The issue will be listed in Luxembourg REUTER
