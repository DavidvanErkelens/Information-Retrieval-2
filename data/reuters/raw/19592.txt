METROLOGIE ISSUES MLN FRENCH FRANC BOND PARIS June French financial group Metrologie International said it is issuing mln francs worth of stock option bonds with Banque Paribas as lead manager The eight year issue at par will carry interest of six pct payable on August Existing shareholders will have priority subscription to the franc nominal bonds on the basis of one bond for shares held Each bond will be accompanied by two warrants giving the right to subscribe to one franc nominal share at a price of francs REUTER
