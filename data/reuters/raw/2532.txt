ROYAL TRUST PLANS MLN DLR SHARE ISSUE TORONTO March Royal Trustco Ltd said it planned a mln dlr issue in Canada in late March of mln class A common shares at dlrs a share The company added that it would also double the amount of class A and class B common shares on June through a stock dividend of one class A or one class B share for each class A or class B share held on June record date Each series A and B cumulative redeemable convertible preferred share will be convertible after June to common shares Royal Trust said The current conversion rate is common shares for each preferred Royal Trust said it would use proceeds to acquire income producing investments Underwriters are Gordon Capital Corp and Dominion Securities Inc as co lead managers and Merrill Lynch Canada Inc McLeod Young Weir Ltd Nesbitt Thomson Deacon Inc and Wood Gundy Inc as co managers Royal Trust said Reuter
