AMERICAN ELECTRIC AEP UNIT TO BUY BACK BONDS NEW YORK March Ohio Power Co a unit of American Electric Power Co said it will redeem mln dlrs of its first mortgage bonds on June one It will buy back at par mln dlrs of its outstanding pct bonds of and mln dlrs of its outstanding pct bonds due Also the utility will pay accrued interest for the pct series Transfer books for both series will be closed at the close of business on April to allow for selection by lot of the bonds to be redeemed Ohio Power said The books will be reopened around April when redemtion notices are mailed Reuter
