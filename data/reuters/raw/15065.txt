CMS ACMS UNVEILS FIRST IBM SYSTEM ADD ONS TUSTIN Calif April CMS Enhancements Inc said it has introduced the first add on mass storage products for the new International Business Machines Corp IBM IBM Personal System Model personal computers CMS said the products are available immediately and include hard disk drives tape backup subsystems and a hard disk drive on an expansion card It added it plans to introduce more products for higher end models of the IBM personal computers within the next two months Prices for the new products range from dlrs for a tape backup subsystem to dlrs for a mass storage subsystem Reuter
