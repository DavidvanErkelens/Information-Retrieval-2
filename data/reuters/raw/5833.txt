SPAIN S FECSA SUSPENDS PRINCIPAL REPAYMENTS MADRID March Fuerzas Electricas de Catalunya FECSA suspended repayments of principal on its debts in order to start renegotiating its obligations with banks next month a company spokesman said He said the decision did not affect payment of interest on loans and debentures FECSA faces repayments of billion pesetas in interest and billion pesetas in principal this year The Madrid stock exchange suspended trading in FECSA shares on February saying the company was unable to meet payments on its billion peseta debt Foreign banks hold almost half the debt The spokesman said FECSA was sending telexes to its creditors to set dates for the renegotiation It would seek longer repayment deadlines and lower interest rates He said banks had insisted on a formal declaration that FECSA was suspending principal repayments before discussing a renegotiation of its debts REUTER
