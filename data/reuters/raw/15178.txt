COCOA TERMINAL DEPOSITS TO BE CUT PCT ICCH LONDON April The basic deposit on cocoa futures contracts will be reduced by pct as from Friday April taking the required deposit for one tonne lot to stg from previously a spokesman for the International Commodity Clearing House ICCH said The deposit for spread contracts will be similarly cut to stg for a one tonne lot from previously he said The ICCH had been looking at cocoa market fluctuations over a period of weeks he said adding the market s basic stability had warranted a cut in deposit rates The decision to cut deposits was taken by the ICCH after consultation with the London Cocoa Terminal Market the spokesman said The cuts were likely to attract more business to the market he said Reuter
