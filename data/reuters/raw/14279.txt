AEGON ISSUES MLN GUILDER BONDS WITH WARRANTS AMSTERDAM April Dutch insurer Aegon NV AEGN AS plans to issue mln guilder pct six year bonds at an issue price of pct with warrants lead manager Amsterdam Rotterdam Bank NV Amro said From July Aegon may at its option redeem early the bonds in whole or in part The issue has denominations of guilders The warrants give the right to subscribe to a second series of bonds a mln guilder pct six year bullet loan The issue price of the warrants has been fixed at guilders each From May this year up to and including July each warrant will entitle the holder to the bullet by surrender of the other bond and a warrant After July each warrant will entitle the holder to acquire one bullet at a price of pct of the nominal value plus accrued interest from the preceding coupon date Aegon will pay the holders of unexercised warrants outstanding on July guilders each Subscriptions close April and pay date is May Coupon date is July Application will be made for listing on the Amsterdam Stock Exchange Amro said it was the second time it arranged a bond with debt warrants for Aegon This type of issue gives the holder the opportunity in times of falling interest rates to ensure himself of a fixed maturity loan while with unchanged or rising rates he has the option not to exercise his warrant Amro said In the underwriting syndicate are ABN bank Morgan Bank Nederland NMB bank Rabo bank Pierson Heldring en Pierson Mees en Hope Van Haften en Co Citicorp Investment Bank The Netherlands and Swiss Bank Corp Int Holland REUTER
