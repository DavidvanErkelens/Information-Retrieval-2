HIGH VOLTAGE HVE ADOPTS STOCK OWNERSHIP PLAN BURLINGTON MASS March High Voltage Engineering Corp said it adopted an employee stock ownership plan All domestic non union employees who have completed one thousand hours of service in the months ended December are eligible to participate in the plan On March the plan purchased common shares at dlrs per share In connection with the purchase the plan borrowed mln dlrs under a loan that is guaranteed by High Voltage In addition High Voltage contributed shares to the plan Reuter
