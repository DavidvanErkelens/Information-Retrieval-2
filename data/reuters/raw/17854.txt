GENERAL MOTORS GM CANADA TO CLOSE TRUCK CENTRE MONTREAL June General Motors of Canada Ltd said it will close its Montreal truck centre August due to low sales volume putting most of the centre s employees out of work The sales volume at the centre has been insufficient to support the operation the company said in a statement With the recently announced joint venture with Volvo to supply the heavy duty truck market customer requirements for the GM line of light and medium duty trucks will be better satisfied by GM dealers located throughout Quebec the company said General Motors Canada said it will provide weeks of layoff benefits for most employees and up to weeks for some senior employees Reuter
