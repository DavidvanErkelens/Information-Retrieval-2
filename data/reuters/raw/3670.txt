EDF ISSUING FIVE BILLION FRENCH FRANC BOND PARIS March The French electricity utility Electricite de France is issuing a five billion French franc bond in three tranches co lead manager Banque Paribas said here The issue is also being lead managed by Credit Lyonnais The first two billion franc tranche carries a fixed interest rate of pct with a life of years days and redeemable en bloc at par at the end of its life The issue price will be pct and the payment date is March The second tranche of billion francs will be denominated in franc units and carry an pct coupon payable December each year The issue price will be pct and it will have a life of years days Redemption will be en bloc at par at the end of its life and the payment date will be March Each bond will carry a warrant permitting subscription on December to a fixed rate bond which will be integrated with the previous issue at an issue price of pct and with a nominal coupon of pct The life of the issue will be years A third tranche of billion francs will be issued at a variable interest rate and the whole issue will be quoted on the Paris Bourse Paribas and Credit Lyonnais will be market makers for the first two tranches the communique added REUTER
