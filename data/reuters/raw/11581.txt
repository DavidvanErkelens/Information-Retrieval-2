CALIFORNIA ENERGY CECI NEGOTIATES FINANCING SANTA ROSA Calif March California Energy Co Inc said it negotiated a mln dlr private financing with Drexel Burnham Lambert Inc As part of the financing it received a commitment letter subject to standard conditions for mln dlrs of project financing from Credit Suisse Proceeds from the financing will be used for equipment procurement and construction of an megawatt power plant by its China Lake Joint Venture the company said It said the plant will be built on a geothermal site at China Lake Calif on property leased from the federal government Reuter
