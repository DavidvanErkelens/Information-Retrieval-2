U S WHEAT CREDITS FOR JORDAN SWITCHED WASHINGTON March The Commodity Credit Corporation CCC has switched mln dlrs in wheat credit guarantees to Jordan under the Export Credit Guarantee Program to the Intermediate Export Credit Guarantee Program the U S Agriculture Department said The switch reduces the total value of GSM guarantees for the current fiscal year to mln dlrs The credit terms extended for export sales under the Intermediate Export Credit Guarantee Program GSM must be in excess of three years but not more than seven years All sales must be registered and exports completed by September the department said Reuter
