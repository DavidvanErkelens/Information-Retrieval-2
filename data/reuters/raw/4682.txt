METAL BOX ISSUES MLN STG CONVERTIBLE BONDS LONDON March Metal Box Plc MBXL L is issuing mln stg of convertible eurobonds due April with an indicated coupon of pct to six pct and priced at par Swiss Bank Corp International Ltd said as lead manager The conversion premium will be five to pct and the price will be fixed on or before March The bonds will be callable at after five months and until April and thereafter at prices declining by one pct per annum until but only if the share price is pct of the conversion price The bonds will be listed in London and will be issued in denominations of stg Fees for the issue total pct comprising pct for selling and pct each for management and underwriting There is a pct praecipuum Pay date is April REUTER
