EIB ISSUES BILLION YEN EUROBOND LONDON April The European Investment Bank EIB is issuing a billion yen eurobond due May paying pct and priced at lead Nomura International Ltd said The non callable bond is available in denominations of one mln yen and will be listed in Luxembourg The selling concession is pct while management and underwriting combined pays pct The payment date is May There is a mandatory purchase fund operating in years one and two except for the first month after the payment date whereby the EIB can purchase up to five pct of the issue each year if it is trading below par REUTER
