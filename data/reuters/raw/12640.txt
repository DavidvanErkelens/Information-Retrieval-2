NORANDA S MURDOCHVILLE MINE SHUT DOWN BY FIRE Toronto April Noranda Inc said production at its Murdochville Quebec copper mine was shut down by the fire that trapped miners and killed at least one Noranda spokesman Denis Morin said it was impossible to judge right now how long the shutdown might last but said he thought it could be at least one week The mine produced metric tons of ore in Morin said It has a mineral inventory of tons In the mine s three zones last year copper grade was pct at Needle Mountain pct at Copper Mountain Oxide and pct at Murdochville Morin said Another Noranda spokesman said the smelter at Murdochville which has not been shut down has the capacity to treat tons of mineral concentrate per year The smelter treats ore from other mines as well as product from the Murdochville facility he said Reuter
