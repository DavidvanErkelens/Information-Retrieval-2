VOLVO UNIT RECEIVES GENERAL ELECTRIC ORDER STOCKHOLM March Sweden s AB Volvo VOLV ST said its subsidiary Volvo Flygmotor had won a contract worth mln crowns over four years from General Electric Co GE N to supply flight engine components Volvo said it received the order after General Electric had won a contract for CF C aircraft engines from American Airlines The CF C is a joint venture between Volvo Flygmotor and General Electric the Swedish firm said The contract runs from next year until Volvo added Reuter
