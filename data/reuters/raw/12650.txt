NORANDA COPPER MINE SHUT DOWN AFTER FIRE TORONTO April Noranda Inc said production at its Murdochville Quebec copper mine was shut down by the fire that broke out last night trapping miners and killing one Noranda spokesman Denis Morin said it was impossible to judge how long the shutdown might last but said he thought it could be at least one week The mine produced tonnes of ore in Morin said It has a mineral inventory of tonnes In the mine s three zones last year copper grade was pct at Needle Mountain pct at Copper Mountain Oxide and pct at Murdochville Morin said Another Noranda spokesman said the smelter at Murdochville which has not been shut down has the capacity to treat tonnes of mineral concentrate per year The smelter treats ore from other mines as well as product from the Murdochville facility he said The fire broke out last night in the mine which is situated in the Gaspe district of eastern Quebec Reuter
