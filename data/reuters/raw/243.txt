BANGLADESH MOVES AGAINST LOAN DEFAULTERS DHAKA March Bangladesh police mounted a cross country hunt for defaulters on bank loans arresting four industrialists and issuing arrest warrants against others for failure to repay overdue obligations No names were given Metropolitan police told reporters the four arrested put under six month pre trial detention owed nearly mln taka the equivalent of about mln dlrs to Bangladesh Shilpa Industrial Bank President Hossain Mohammad Ershad has said non payers are crippling the industrial sector But the Chamber of Commerce and industry said the crackdown would scare away entrepreneurs REUTER
