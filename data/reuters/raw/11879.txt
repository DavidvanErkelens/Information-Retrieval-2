SWEDEN RAISES FUEL TAXES TO FUND DEFENCE SPENDING STOCKHOLM April Sweden announced tax increases on petrol and heating oil from July to help finance a pct rise in defence spending over the next five years A Finance Ministry statement said the increase should boost the price of petrol by crowns to an average of crowns per litre while light grade heating oil would go up by crowns per cubic metre to an average of crowns It said oil companies should absorb part of the tax rise internally and not pass it on to consumers as compensation for a reduction in the stocks that the industry is required to keep for Sweden s national petroleum reserve REUTER
