FRANCE PLANS TIGHTER SHAREHOLDING DISCLOSURE LEVELS PARIS March The French government plans new legislation this year which will lower the thresholds at which investors in companies must declare their shareholdings Bourse officials said The new rules part of a scheme to provide more openness in corporate ownership structure will lower the initial declaration threshold to five pct of a company s capital Under current rules an investor has to notify the Bourse supervisory commission when a holding exceeds pct and again at pct and pct The officials said a pct level has also been added to the scale Reuter
