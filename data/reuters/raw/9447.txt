MOUNTLEIGH GROUP SEEKS MLN STG FACILITY LONDON March Mountleigh Group Plc a U K Property company is seeking a mln stg multicurrency credit facility Union Bank of Switzerland London branch said The revolving evergreen facility will be completely underwritten and the borrower will be able to issue cash advances sterling bankers acceptances and sterling commercial paper through a tender panel There will be a cap of basis points over the London Interbank Offered Rate Libor on any drawings for advances and a basis points commission on acceptances There will be a facility fee of pct Banks are being invited to join at to mln stg for basis points at to mln stg for basis points and at five to mln stg for basis points The facility which can only be cancelled after underwriters provide months notice to the borrower will refinance some of Mountleigh s existing debt Reuter
