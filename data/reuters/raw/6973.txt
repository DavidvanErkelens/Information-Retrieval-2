SWISSAIR ORDERS SIX MCDONNELL DOUGLAS MD JETS ZURICH March Swissair SWSZ Z said it was confirming six orders for the new MD long haul jet of McDonnell Douglas Corp MD and had taken options for a further planes The order which involved rejecting the A plane being built by the European Airbus consortium is to replace the airline s fleet of DC aircraft in the early s Swissair said in December it was giving the MD primary consideration and had reserved six places for delivery pending a final order REUTER
