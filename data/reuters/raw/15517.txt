SEKISUI CHEMICAL ISSUES EQUITY WARRANT EUROBOND LONDON April Sekisui Chemical Co Ltd is issuing a mln dlr equity warrant bond due May paying an indicated coupon of two pct and priced at par lead manager Yamaichi International Europe Ltd said The issue is guaranteed by Sanwa Bank Ltd and is available in denominations of dlrs The selling concession is pct while management and underwriting combined pays pct The payment date is May while listing will be in Luxembourg Final terms will be fixed on April The warrants are exercisable from May until April REUTER
