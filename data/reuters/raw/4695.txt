U S LENDS SUDAN MLN DLRS FOR WHEAT PURCHASES KHARTOUM March The U S Is to lend Sudan mln dlrs to buy tonnes of wheat and tonnes of wheat flour according to an agreement signed here Under the agreement Sudan will receive mln dlrs for the commodities and the rest for sea transportation The loan is repayable in years including a year grace period Interest will be two pct for the grace period and three pct over the repayment period of years REUTER
