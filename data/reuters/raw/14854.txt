SAITAMA BANK ISSUES MLN DLR CONVERTIBLE BOND LONDON April The Saitama Bank Ltd is issuing a mln dlr convertible eurobond due March paying an indicated two pct and priced at par the bank s London office said The issue is being lead managed by Saitama Bank Europe SA The deal is available in denominations of dlrs and will be listed in Luxembourg Final terms will be set on April The selling concession is pct while management and underwriting each pay pct The payment date is May and there will be a short first coupon period REUTER
