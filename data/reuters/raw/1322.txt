REIDEL RETI UNIT SELECTED FOR EPA CONTRACT PORTLAND Ore March Reidel Environmental Technologies Inc said its Reidel Envirnomental Services Inc unit won a contract with the Environmental Protection Agency that could total over mln dlrs over the next four years Under the agreement which began March Reidel will act as the prime contractor for the cleanup and removal of hazardous substances from contaminated sites in the EPA s Zone IV which encompasses states west of the Mississippi Reuter
