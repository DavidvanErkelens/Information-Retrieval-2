SPAIN S FOREIGN RESERVES RISE IN FEBRUARY MADRID April Spain s foreign reserves rose by mln dlrs in February to total billion dlrs compared with billion dlrs in February Bank of Spain figures show Under a new accounting system introduced this month Spain s foreign reserves now exclude foreign currency held by financial institutions Under the previous system Spain s foreign reserves would have risen by mln dlrs in February taking into account a fall of mln dlrs in financial institutions foreign currency position In January this year the Central Bank s foreign reserves rose by mln dlrs while financial institutions foreign currency position fell by mln dlrs REUTER
