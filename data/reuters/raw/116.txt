SCHULT HOMES CORP MAKES INITIAL STOCK OFFER NEW YORK Feb Schult Homes Corp announced an initial public offering of units at five dlrs per unit said Janney Montgomery Scott Inc and Woolcott and Co managing underwriters of the offering They said each unit consists of one common share and one warrant to buy one half share of common The warrant will entitle holders to buy one half common share at dlrs per full share from March one to September one and thereafter at dlrs per full share until March they said Reuter
