COLOMBIA TO MAINTAIN FOREIGN BORROWING LEVELS MIAMI March Colombia plans to maintain foreign borrowing at levels similar to the period during which it received a net billion dlrs net of repayments Finance Minister Cesar Gaviria said He told the Inter American Development Bank IADB annual meeting here that despite Latin America s debt crisis Colombia continues to achieve positive credit flows reflecting the strength of its economy that grew five pct last year Public Credit Director Mauricio Cabrera said Colombia plans to launch a foreign bond issue in the second half of possibly for mln dlrs Cabrera said a mln dlr floating rate note FRN issue by Colombia is expected to close next month It carries a pct margin over Libor with a seven year maturity and four years grace He also told Reuters he did not expect any increase in Colombia s financing needs as a result of current falling coffee prices after a surge last year doubled export income from this source to around three billion dlrs The drop will simply bring us back to levels with a loss of no more than about mln dlrs he said Colombia with a total foreign debt of around billion dlrs has a foreign borrowing programme of around three billion dlrs this year of which about one billion is new credits Its foreign reserves ended at billion dlrs equivalent to more than nine months of imports and non financial services and GDP growth is again expected to reach five pct this year Gaviria said Reuter
