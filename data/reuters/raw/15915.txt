EXPORT BONUS WHEAT FOR JORDAN USDA WASHINGTON April The Commodity Credit Corporation CCC accepted eight bonus offers from two exporters on sales of tonnes of hard red winter and tonnes of soft red winter wheat to Jordan the U S Agriculture Department said The department said the bonuses awarded averaged dlrs per tonne and the wheat is for delivery May November The bonus awards were made to Louis Dreyfus Corp tonnes and Continental Grain Co tonnes and will be paid in the form of commodities from CCC stocks The purchases of U S wheat completes the Export Enhancement Program initiative announced on December Reuter
