CHRYSLER C OVERSEAS UNIT REDEEMING DEBENTURES DETROIT March Chrysler Overseas Capital Corp said it authorized redemption on April of its pct and five pct convertible debentures due The Chrysler Corp unit said the move is prompted by the parent company s three for two stock split which requires a recalculation of the debentures conversion prices that will adversely affect those prices by at least five pct Chrysler said holders converting debentures before the stock split becomes effective March will receive a greater number of shares on a post split basis than if they convert afterwards Reuter
