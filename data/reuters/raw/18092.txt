ALCAN REDUCES COST OF PLANNED SMELTER MONTREAL June Alcan Aluminium Ltd said it has cut the expected cost of its planned Laterriere Quebec smelter by using enhanced Grande Baie reduction technology The company said the decision to use prebaked anode technolgy used at its Grande Baie Quebec and Sebree Ky smelters was taken primarily for cost reasons As a result it said the estimated total cost of the planned smelter will be held to mln U S dlrs the low end of the range estimated in mid May while the cost of the first phase was cut to mln dlrs from the projected mln dlrs Reuter
