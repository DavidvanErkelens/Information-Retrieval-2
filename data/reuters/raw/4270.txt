NORSK HYDRO ISSUES MLN DLR BOND London March Norsk Hydro is issuing a mln dlr bond due April carrying a coupon of pct and priced at said Swiss Bank Corp International as lead manager The issue is non callable and will be listed on the London Stock Exchange Payment date is April Fees for the issue include a pct selling concession and a pct combined management and underwriting fee The securities are available in denominations of dlrs each They are priced to yield basis points over year U S Treasury notes REUTER
