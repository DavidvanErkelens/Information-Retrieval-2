MORGAN STANLEY MS ISSUES MORTGAGE BONDS NEW YORK March Morgan Stanley Mortgage Trust a unit of the Morgan Stanley Group is offering mln dlrs of collateralized mortgage obligations CMOs in seven classes These consist of mln dlrs of adjustable rate class J bonds to be fully paid by November priced at pct to yield pct The notes will bear an interest rate of seven pct for one year from April rising to pct until April and an eight pct rate thereafter Morgan also will offer mln dlrs of floating rate class J bonds to be fully paid by priced at par to yield three month Libor plus pct There is an pct cap Also among the issues are mln dlrs of class J three bonds to be fully paid by May and priced at par to yield pct and mln dlrs of floating rate class J four bonds to be fully paid by the same date and priced at par to yield three month Libor plus pct The cap is pct The class J five bonds totalling mln dlrs will be fully paid by August and priced at par to yield pct The mln dlrs of floating rate class J six bonds of the same maturity are priced at par at a rate of three month Libor plus pct with a pct cap The mln dlrs of pct class J seven bonds due August are priced at pct to yield pct Interest on all classes of bonds will be payable quarterly Morgan Stanley is sole manager of the offering which will be rated AAA by Standard and Poor s Corp Reuter
