PEPCO POM TO REDEEM PREFERRED STOCK WASHINGTON March Potomac Electric and Power Co said it will redeem on May all of the shares of its outstanding dlrs series of serial preferred stock at dlrs per share plus accrued dividends PEPCO also said it will redeem all of the shares of its outstanding dlr series of serial preferred stock at dlrs per share plus accrued dividends In addition the company said it was selling one mln shares of a new series of the company s dlr par value serial preferred stock The annual dividend rate for the new stock will be dlrs per share the company said Reuter
