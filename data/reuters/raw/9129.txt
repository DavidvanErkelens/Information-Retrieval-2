BELL GROUP ISSUING MLN IN CONVERTIBLE BONDS PERTH March The Bell Group Ltd BLLA S said it will make two convertible bond issues totalling mln dlrs An issue of about mln in convertible bonds to be quoted on the Luxembourg Stock Exchange would be offered in Europe through a syndicate led by Banque Paribas Capital Markets Ltd Swiss Bank Corp International Ltd and S G Warburg Securities Bell Group said in a statement Subject to shareholder approval at a meeting on April a further mln dlrs in convertible bonds would be subscribed by Bell Group chairman Robert Holmes a Court it said The funds would provide additional working capital The bonds maturing years from the date of issue would carry a pct per annum interest rate payable annually in arrears Bell Group said The bonds are convertible into ordinary shares of The Bell Group Ltd at any time with the issue price a premium in the range of to pct above the market price of the ordinary shares at the date of issue of the bonds it said The bonds also carry the right for the issuer to redeem them after two years if the share price is above pct of the conversion price for more than days Bell Group made a similar mln dlr bond issue in December REUTER
