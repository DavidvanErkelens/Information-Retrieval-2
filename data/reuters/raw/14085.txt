GENERAL ELECTRIC GE POWERED HELICOPTER UNVEILED LYNN Mass April General Electric Co said that EH Industries a joint effort between Westland Helicopter of England and the Augusta Group of Italy rolled out the first prototype EH helicopter in Yeovil England GE said the helicopter is powered by three GE T engines The company added that the multi mission EH has naval utility and civil models The latter two will be powered by shaft horsepower CT engines being co developed by GE Fiat Aviazione and Alfa Romeo Avio GE said Reuter
