FIAT UNIT ISSUES MLN DLR BOND AND WARRANTS LONDON March Fiat Finance and Trade Ltd is issuing a mln dlr eurobond due April at pct and pct lead manager Morgan Stanley International said The issue is guaranteed by Internazionale Fiat Holding SA and is accompanied by a currency warrant package The bond is available in denominations of dlrs and will be listed in Luxembourg Payment date for bond and warrants is April The selling concession is pct while management and underwriting combined pays pct The warrants indicated at dlrs entitle the holder to buy a minimum of dlrs at a rate of marks per dlr The warrants are exercisable from the April payment date until March A minimum of warrants must be exercised They will also be listed in Luxembourg REUTER
