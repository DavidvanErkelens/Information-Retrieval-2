ALCOA AUSTRALIA PLANS MLN AUS DLR FACILITY HONG KONG April The Alcoa of Australia Ltd is planning a mln Australian dlr note issuance facility lead manager Westpac Finance Asia Ltd said The three year facility is half underwritten and half non underwritten The underwriting margin is basis points over Australian dlr bank bill rate and the underwriting fee is basis points There is an issuance fee of basis points and an utilisation fee of basis points if the underwriters are required to pick up pct or more of the notes issued The notes in denominations of dlrs will have maturities ranging from one to six months REUTER
