TOKYO OPTICAL ISSUES EQUITY WARRANT EUROBOND LONDON April Tokyo Optical Co Ltd is issuing a mln dlr equity warrant eurobond due April paying an indicated coupon of pct and priced at par lead manager Daiwa Europe Ltd said The issue is guaranteed by Mitsui Bank Ltd and final terms will be set on April The issue is available in denominations of dlrs and will be listed in Luxembourg The selling concession is pct while management and underwriting combined pays pct The warrants are exercisable from May until April The payment date is April REUTER
