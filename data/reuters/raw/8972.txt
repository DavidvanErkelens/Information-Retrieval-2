LEECO DIAGNOSTICS LECO AWARDED FEDERAL GRANT SOUTHFIELD Mich March Leeco Diagnostics Inc said the National Institutes of Health have awarded it a two year dlr federal grant to develop a new medical test kit to diagnose and monitor diabetes mellitus Leeco said the Institutes made the grant under their small business innovation research program The company said the research beginning April will focus on developing a more effective technique for detecting hemoglobin A C in the blood High levels of hemoglobin A C can indicate uncontrolled diabetes Reuter
