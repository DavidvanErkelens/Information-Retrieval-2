CHINA WANTS TO BUY TONNES PAKISTANI COTTON KARACHI April China is negotiating with Pakistan to buy tonnes of cotton this year after importing the same amount last year under a barter agreement Chinese consulate sources said Chinese ambassador Tian Ding told a meeting of Pakistani industrialists on Thursday that China intended to increase imports from Pakistan to reduce a trade imbalance Pakistani officials estimate the country s cotton output from the current crop at a record mln bales pounds each Last year s output totalled mln bales and domestic consumption was just below three mln bales they said REUTER
