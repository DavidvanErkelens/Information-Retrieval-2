HECHINGER HECHA SELLS CONVERTIBLE DEBENTURES NEW YORK March Hechinger Co is raising mln dlrs through an offering of convertible subordinated debentures due with a pct coupon and par pricing said sole underwriter Morgan Stanley and Co Inc The debentures are convertible into the company s common stock at dlrs per share representing a premium of pct over the stock price when terms on the debt were set Non callable for two years the debt is rated Baa by Moody s and BBB plus by Standard and Poor s The issue was increased from an initial offering of mln dlrs Earlier this week Moody s and S and P upgraded the company s debt Reuter
