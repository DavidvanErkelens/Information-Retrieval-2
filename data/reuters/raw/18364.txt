MATSUSHITA IN JOINT VENTURE WITH FORD MAZDA TOKYO June Matsushita Electric Industrial Co Ltd MC T Mazda Motor Corp MAZT T and Ford Motor Co F have signed an agreement to set up a joint venture to make car air conditioners in Japan Matsushita said in a statement The new venture Japan Climate Systems Corp will be capitalised at billion yen and the three partners will hold equal shares The partners will invest billion yen in constructing a plant in Hiroshima due for completion in late The plant will initially make air conditioners a year for Mazda it said REUTER
