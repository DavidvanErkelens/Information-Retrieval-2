BANK OF JAPAN TO SELL BILLION YEN IN BILLS TOKYO March The Bank of Japan will sell billion yen of financing bills tomorrow under a day repurchase accord maturing on May money traders said The bills are meant to soak up a projected money market surplus due largely to coupon and principle payments of a previously issued Japanese National Railways bond The yield on the bills for sales to banks and securities houses from money houses will be pct against the pct discount rate for two month commercial bills and the pct yield on two month certificates of deposit today The sale will raise the bill supply to billion yen REUTER
