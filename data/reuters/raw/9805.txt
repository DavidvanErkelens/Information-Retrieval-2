CONRAIL INITIAL OFFER PRICED AT DLRS SHARE NEW YORK March Lead underwriter Goldman Sachs and Co said an initial public offering of shares of Consolidated Rail Corp common stock has been priced at dlrs per share The sale is the largest initial public offering ever All of the shares are being sold by the U S government They represent an pct interest in Conrail the large northeastern freight carrying railroad Employees of Conrail retain the other pct The shares will be traded on the New York Stock Exchange under the ticker symbol CRR starting this morning Initially underwriters had said in proxy materials that the shares were expected to be priced at to dlrs per share But last week they raised the expected range to to dlrs per share Conrail was formed during the s from Penn Central Railroad and other financially distressed northeastern rail carriers The offering will raise about billion dlrs before underwriting fees Previously the largest initial public offering in dollar terms had been Henley Group Inc s HENG billion dlr spinoff from Allied Corp ALD last year Other lead underwriters are First Boston Inc FBC Merrill Lynch and Co Inc MER Salomon Inc SB and American Express Co s AXP Shearson Lehman Brothers Inc Fifty two mln shares are being sold in the U S through a syndicate of underwriters and overseas through a member syndicate Reuter
