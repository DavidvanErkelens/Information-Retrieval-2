DEFENSE CONTRACTOR BOOSTS BIRD BIRD STAKE WASHINGTON April Entwistle Co a Hudson Mass based defense contractor said it raised its stake in Bird Inc to the equivalent of shares or pct of the total outstanding common stock from shares or pct Entwistle a privately held company said it bought Bird common shares on April at dlrs a share It said it may buy up to pct of the total outstanding for investment purposes Entwistle also said it has met with Bird representatives on its request for board representation but the talks were inconclusive Its stake includes common shares with the rest in convertible preferred stock Reuter
