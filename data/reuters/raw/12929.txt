HADSON HADS TO OFFER FOUR MLN SHARES OKLAHOMA CITY April Hadson Corp said it is offering four mln new shares of its common par value cts a share at dlrs a shares It had previously announced it would offer shares in a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission Hadson said proceeds will be used for expansion of its existing businesses and energy related acquisitions and for general corporate purposes Shearson Lehman Brothers Inc and Painwebber Inc are the managing underwriters Reuter
