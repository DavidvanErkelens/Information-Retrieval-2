QUEBEC S CAISSE DES JARDINS ISSUES YEN EUROBOND LONDON March Caisse Centrale Desjardins du Quebec is issuing a billion yen step up eurobond maturing on March and priced at pct joint bookrunner Warburg Securities said The issues pays a coupon of one pct in years one and two and then pays a coupon of pct in years three four and five The selling concession is pct while management and underwriting combined pays pct The payment date is March while the issue will be listed in Luxembourg The borrower is the wholesale financing arm of a major group of credit unions in the Province of Quebec Reuter
