NORDIC INVESTMENT BANK ISSUES DANISH CROWN BOND LONDON March Nordic Investment Bank is issuing a mln Danish crown bond due April priced at par with an pct coupon lead manager Enskilda Securities said The bonds are callable on April at par and at any time for tax reasons They will be sold in denominations of crowns and listed in Luxembourg Fees involve pct for selling and pct for management and underwriting combined minus pct for the co leads Payment date is April The co leads are Privatbanken A S and Generale Bank Brussels REUTER
