AMERICAN CAN AC SELLS CONVERTIBLE EUROBONDS NEW YORK March American Can Co said it is issuing in the Euromarkets mln dlrs of convertible Eurodollar bonds due with a pct coupon and par pricing The Eurobonds are convertible into the company s common stock at dlrs per share representing a premium of pct over today s closing stock price American Can said Proceeds will be used for general corporate purposes which could include refinancing outstanding debt business acquisitions or investments The debt is being offered via a syndicate managed by Morgan Stanley International Credit Suisse First Boston and Salomon Brothers International Reuter
