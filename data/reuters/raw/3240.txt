CHRYSLER C VALUES AMC AMO AT MLN DLRS DETROIT March Chrysler Corp said it valued its proposed buyout of American Motors Corp at mln dlrs not counting the effect of a contingent payment that could reach mln dlrs based on AMC s future profits A Chrysler spokesman told Reuters that the letter of intent signed with Renault AMC s controlling shareholder with a pct direct stake includes a provision for Renault to be paid mln dlrs in cash for AMC s finance subsidiary and mln dlrs in the form of an eight pct note He said he did not know the maturity of the note Reuter
