SUN SUN RAISES HEATING OIL BARGE PRICE NEW YORK March Sun Co s Sun Refining and Marketing subsidiary said it increased the price it charges contract barge customers for heating oil in New York harbor cent a gallon effective today They said the cent increase brings Sun s contract barge price to cts The recent price hike represents the fifth this week totalling cts The increases are following sharp spot and futures price rises the company said Reuter
