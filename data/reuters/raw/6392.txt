ASSOCIATES CORP ISSUES BILLION YEN BOND LONDON March Associates Corp of North America is issuing a billion yen eurobond due November with a five pct coupon and priced at IBJ International Ltd said as lead manager The non callable bonds will be issued in denominations of one mln yen and will be listed in Luxembourg Fees are pct for selling and pct for management and underwriting combined with a pct praecipuum Pay date is April Co lead managers are New Japan Securities Wako Securities and Yasuda Trust REUTER
