MEXICO GETS MLN DLRS IN WORLD BANK LOANS WASHINGTON June The World Bank said it approved two loans to Mexico totalling mln dlrs to provide financial assistance to industrial companies and finance a project dealing with agricultural extension services Mexico s Nacional Financiera NF the recipient of both loans will receive mln dlrs to be used to provide long term credit and equity funds to small and medium sized industrial enterprises the bank said It said a mln dlr loan is being made to help finance a project designed to test strategies to improve the quality and cost effectiveness of agricultural extension services The industrial enterprise project estimated to cost mln dlrs aims to support companies threatened by high inflation and adversely affected by increased competition brought about by bank supported trade liberalization measures The bank said the mln dlr extension services project will examine strategies in of Mexico s rural development districts and finance training for personnel and farmers office construction and the purchasing of equipment Both loans are for years including three years of grace with a variable interest rate currently per cent which is linked to the bank s cost of borrowing funds Reuter
