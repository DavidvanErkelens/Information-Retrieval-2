minister Edouard Balladur said Balladur told an Economist conference that on the product side a series of new contracts including share options and forward contracts in European Currency Units ECUs weakening factor for the market Balladur said He said under the planned new rules stock brokers currently very specialised and undercapitalised would be free to link up with powerful financial
