GEORGIAN AVALANCHES AND FLOODS BRING DEATHS MOSCOW March Avalanches landslides and floods have killed almost people since the start of the year in the Soviet Transcaucasian republic of Georgia the official daily Sotsialisticheskaya Industriya said Some people have been left homeless and hectares of arable land and plantations rendered useless the newspaper said The figures are still preliminary but it estimated total losses at over mln roubles It said a large number of livestock and birds were killed in the natural disaster and more than km of roads needed repairing REUTER
