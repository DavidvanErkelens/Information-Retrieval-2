BHOPAL CASE JUDGE SEEKS INTERIM COMPENSATION BHOPAL India April A judge hearing the Bhopal gas disaster case asked Union Carbide Corp UK and the Indian government to agree on interim compensation for the victims Judge M W Deo said he had made the appeal because the court case was likely to be a long struggle for the disaster victims The Indian government is demanding more than three billion dlrs from Union Carbide on behalf of some claimants following the world s worst industrial disaster in December According to official Indian figures people were killed and more than injured when a cloud of methyl isocyanate gas from a Union Carbide pesticide plant enveloped Bhopal Reuter
