PHARMACONTROL PHAR O CLOSES PUBLIC OFFERING ENGLEWOOD CLIFFS N J June Pharmacontrol Corp said it has closed its public offering of units The company said each unit consists of dlrs principal amount of subordinated convertible debentures due which convert at six dlrs a share into shares of common stock Also each unit included shares of common stock which were immediately detachable the company said Pharmacontrol added that it has also converted its seven mln dlr credit facility with its institutional lender into a mln dlr credit facility Reuter
