BANCO DI SICILIA INTERNATIONAL ISSUES EUROBOND LONDON March Banco di Sicilia International SA is issuing a mln U S Dlr zero coupon eurobond due April and priced at Nomura International Ltd said as joint bookrunner The bonds which yield an effective pct per annum will be listed in Luxembourg and will be issued in denominations of dlrs They are guaranteed by Banco di Sicilia Gross fees of pct comprise pct for management and underwriting combined and pct for selling Pay date is April The other joint bookrunner is Yasuda Trust and Banco di Sicilia is co lead REUTER
