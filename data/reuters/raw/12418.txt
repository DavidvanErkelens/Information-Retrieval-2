DANISH PLAN TO HELP CRISIS HIT BANK DEPOSITORS COPENHAGEN April The Danish Banks Association announced a plan to help depositors in the small Sixth of July Bank Juli Banken A S which ceased payments on March after the Government Bank Inspectorate said provisions were too low The Association said in a statement that its members would give full cover for private deposits up to Danish crowns with pct cover for deposits between and crowns and for special pension savings accounts up to crowns Non pension deposits over crowns and business customers are not covered by the plan the statement added REUTER
