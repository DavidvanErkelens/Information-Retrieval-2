CFTC APPROVES MGE CORN SYRUP FUTURES CONTRACT WASHINGTON March The Commodity Futures Trading Commission has approved the Minneapolis Grain Exchange s application to trade high fructose corn syrup HFCS futures contracts the commission said The contract provides for the delivery of lbs plus or minus two pct of bulk HFCS meeting specified standards regarding its physical and chemical properties CFTC said the exchange plans to begin trading a July HFCS contract on April CFTC said the soft drink industry currently buys at least pct of all U S produced HFCS a liquid food and beverage sweetener produced through the processing of corn starch by corn refiners Reuter
