STELCO STEA TO USWA REACH CONTRACT SETTLEMENT TORONTO April Stelco Inc said contract negotiations with United Steelworkers of America concluded in a memorandum of settlement for a new three year collective agreement covering about workers at of Stelco s plants Further terms of the new agreement were not immediately disclosed The company said early negotiations began March in order to reach a new contract well before the July expiry of the existing contract It said the new contract was endorsed by negotiating committees of all union locals representing Stelco workers Reuter
