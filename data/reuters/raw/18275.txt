CCC ACCEPTS BONUS ON POULTRY TO IRAQ USDA WASHINGTON June The Commodity Credit Corporation CCC has accepted a bid for an export bonus to cover the sale of tonnes of frozen poultry to Iraq the U S Agriculture Department said The poultry is for shipment June September and the bonus was dlrs per tone it said The bonus award was made to ConAgra Poultry Co and will be paid in the form of commodities from the inventory of CCC stocks The purchase completes the Export Enhancement initiative for Frozen Poultry to Iraq announced December Reuter
