ECUADOREAN UNIONS TO CALL GENERAL STRIKE Quito march ecuador s union federations called a general strike for next wednesday to press the governent to suspend austerity measures adopted recently to grapple with losses from a recent earthquake The strong unitary workers front fut and the member general union of workers ugt have protested the measures which include a rise in petrol prices of up to per cent and budget cuts of as much as per cent President leon febres cordero imposed the measures after a march earthquake cost an estimated one billion dollars in losses About people are dead or missing The leftist led fut said it was also backing a call by the maoist popular movement for democracy mpd party to have congress impeach and oust febres cordero a conservative for having adopted the austerity measures Congress needs to muster two thirds of the votes in the seat congress to censure and oust the president whose four year presidential term ends in august The congress last january voted to approve a non binding resolution asking the president to resign for having allegedly violated laws and provoking his hour abduction by air force paratroopers on january Reuter
