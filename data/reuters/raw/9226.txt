SEVERE DROUGHT AFFECTS EAST NORTHEAST THAILAND BANGKOK March A severe drought has affected hectares of farmlands in six eastern and northeastern provinces Interior Permanent Secretary Pisarn Mulasartsathorn said He told reporters some farming families in districts in Chaiyaphum Korat Phrae Chainat Rayong and Trat provinces were seeking relief water from the government The official did not say what crops have been damaged Senior Interior Ministry officials said this week the worse than average drought this year has affected provinces and may last until early May REUTER
