ALLEGHENY INT L AG BANKS EXTEND WAIVER PITTSBURGH March Allegheny International Inc said a consortium of banks which entered into long term and short term secured revolving credit agreements with the company last year has agreed to extend a previously granted waiver of certain financial net worth covenants to April from March The company said last week it was seeking such a waiver AI is currently engaged in discussions with the consortium to extend the short term agreement beyond April and expects such an extension will be obtained Reuter
