BELMORAL MINES TO ISSUE MLN DLRS STOCK TORONTO April Belmoral Mines Ltd said it agreed to sell common shares and purchase warrants for mln dlrs to McCarthy Securities Ltd for private placements to European and Canadian investors The agreement provides for the sale of preferred and common shares of Belmoral held by Continental Illinois Bank Canada according to a prior agreement which gave Belmoral the right to designate a purchaser for the bank s stock Belmoral said it will redeem the first preferred shares The transactions are subject to regulatoral approvals Reuter
