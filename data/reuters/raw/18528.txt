YUGOSLAVIA SIGNS FOR MLN ECU LOAN BELGRADE June Yugoslavia has signed up for a mln ECU loan from the European Community EC the official Tanjug news agency said It said the agreement which has yet to be ratified by the EC countries and the European Parliament was signed last night in Brussels The year pct interest loan will be used to complete the trans Yugoslavia motorway and modernise the country s railway network the agency said Yugoslavia s request for a mln ECU grant was refused by the EC but will be discussed again Tanjug added REUTER
