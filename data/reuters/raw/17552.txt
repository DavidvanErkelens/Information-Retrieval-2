GERMAN APRIL WHOLESALE TURNOVER FALLS FOUR PCT WIESBADEN June West German wholesale turnover provisionally totalled about billion marks in April a real decline of four pct compared with the same month last year the Federal Statistics Office said In March wholesale turnover had risen by a real seven pct on the year ago period The Statistics Office said that in the first four months of this year wholesale turnover of about billion marks was a real one pct down from last year REUTER
