SENATE SEEKS U S PROBE OF CANADIAN CORN LEVY WASHINGTON March The Senate voted unanimously to seek an expedited U S probe of Canadian tariffs on corn imports to determine if the United States should retaliate By to the Senate went on record against the cents per bushel tariff approved by the Canadian Import Tribunal The non binding measure asked for a probe by the U S Trade Representative to determine within days whether the tariff violates the General Agreement on Tariffs and Trade and if so recommend within days to President Reagan retaliatory action against Canada Reuter
