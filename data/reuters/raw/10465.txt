SOCIETE GENERALE ISSUES BILLION YEN BOND LONDON March Societe Generale is issuing a billion yen bond due April carrying a coupon of pct for the first four years and priced at Mitsui Trust International Ltd said as joint book runner In the fifth year the coupon rises to pct but the bond is callable after four years Japan s Ministry of Finance does not permit the issuance of euroyen bonds with maturities shorter than five years Daiwa International Europe Ltd is the other joint book runner Payment is due April The securities are available in denoninations of mln yen each and will be listed on the Luxembourg Stock Exchange There is a pct selling concession and a pct combined management and underwriting fee REUTER
