INDUSTRIAL EQUITY HAS PCT OF TRITON OIL WASHINGTON Oct A group of firms led by Hong Kong based Industrial Equity Pacific Ltd said it acquired shares of Triton Oil Corp common stock or pct of the company s common stock outstanding Industrial Equity Pacific which is controlled by Brierley Investments Ltd BRYW WE and which has applied with U S antitrust regulators to buy up to pct of Triton s common stock said the current pct stake was acquired for investment purposes The company said it informed Triton of its present good faith intention to buy more than mln dlrs worth of Triton stock It said it also told Triton it may depending on market conditions acquire pct or more and possibly pct of the voting securities of Triton It said it received clearance to buy up to pct of the stock on September Industrial Equity Pacific reported it bought shares of Triton common stock on October at dlrs a share Reuter
