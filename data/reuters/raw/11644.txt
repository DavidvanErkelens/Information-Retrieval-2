USDA ACCEPTS BID FOR BONUS WHEAT TO ALGERIA WASHINGTON March The U S Agriculture Department said it had accepted a bid for an export bonus to cover a sale of durum wheat to Algeria USDA General Sales Manager Melvin Sims said the Commodity Credit Corp accepted one bid from Cam USA Inc on a sale of tonnes of durum wheat Sims said the bonus was dlrs per tonne and shipment was scheduled for June An additional tonnes of durum wheat are still available to Algeria under the export enhancement program Reuter
