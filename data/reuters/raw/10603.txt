U S REAL EARNINGS ROSE PCT IN FEBRUARY WASHINGTON March The average weekly earnings of U S workers after adjustment for inflation and seasonal changes rose pct in February after being unchanged in January the Labor department said The department had earlier reported that real earnings fell pct in January but revised the figure to show earnings unchanged Between February this year and February real earnings rose pct the department said The rise in February real earnings resulted from a pct increase in average hourly earnings and a pct increase in average weekly hours That was partly offset by a pct rise in the consumer price index which measures inflation the department said Before seasonal adjustment weekly earnings last month averaged dlrs up from dlrs in January and up from dlrs in February Reuter
