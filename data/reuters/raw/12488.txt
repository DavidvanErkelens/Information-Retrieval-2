MINERS TRAPPED BY FIRE IN GASPE COPPER MINE MURDOCHVILLE Que April Some miners were trapped underground today by a fire in a copper mine in the Gaspe area of eastern Quebec officials said There were no reports of any deaths A mine official said that the fire broke out last night and about of the miners made it to safety He said telephone contact had been established with the trapped miners but they could not be brought to the surface until the fire was extinguished The cause of the fire was not known Reuter
