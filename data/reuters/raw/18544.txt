AMRO LINES UP FIRST GUILDER MULTI OPTION FACILITY AMSTERDAM June Amro Bank said it is arranging the first guilder multi option facility for Greenland NV in a loan package of up to mln guilders The package consists of a mln guilder five year multi option facility with a tender panel facility for short term advances and a back stop by the syndicate at pct above the London Inter Bank Offered Rate LIBOR There is also a mln guilder seven year multi currency term facility at LIBOR plus pct for the first three years at LIBOR plus pct for the fourth and fifth year and at LIBOR plus pct for the remaining two years Greenland has an option to fix the second facility Both facilities have a commitment fee of pct Greenland NV a producer of agricultural machines is a subsidiary of the Thyssen Bornemisza Group The firm will be floated on the Amsterdam and Toronto stock exchanges in the second half of July REUTER
