ALGOMA STEEL HAS NO REASON FOR SHARE PRICE RISE SAULT STE MARIE Ontario April Algoma Steel Corp Ltd said there were no specific developments to explain the recent increase in its share price and there were no announcements planned or anticipated The company said it made the statement at the request of the Toronto Stock Exchange Algoma said it continues to achieve cost reductions and there is a limited improvement in market demand for the company s steel products Algoma common closed yesterday in Toronto at up and was trading today at down Reuter
