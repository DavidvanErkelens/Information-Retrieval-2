CCC ACCEPTS BONUS BID ON BARLEY TO CYRPUS WASHINGTON March The Commodity Credit Corporation CCC has accepted a bid on an export bonus to cover the sale of tonnes of barley to Cyprus the U S Agriculture Department said The barley is for shipment May and the bonus of dlrs per tonne was made to Harvest States Cooperatives the department said The bonus will be paid in the form of commodities from the inventory of CCC stocks The department said an additional tonnes of U S barley are still available to Cyprus under the Export Enhancement Program initiative announced on August Reuter
