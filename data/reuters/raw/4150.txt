CALIFORNIA WORKERS END LONG CANNERY STRIKE WATSONVILLE Calif March An month strike against Watsonville Canning and Frozen Foods one of California s largest food processors ended with workers overwhelmingly ratifying a new contract More than workers walked off their jobs in September after Watsonville Canning cut their wages from dlrs an hour to dlrs Watsonville declared bankruptcy last week and the cannery was taken over by Norcal Frozen Foods Inc a consortium of growers A spokeswoman for Teamsters Local said the workers voted in favor of a new contract The Norcal offer provides a dlrs per hour wage with no additional benefits for most workers for up to three years Reuter
