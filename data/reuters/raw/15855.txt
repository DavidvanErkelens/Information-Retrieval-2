USSR ADDS U S CORN TO COMMITMENTS USDA WASHINGTON April The Soviet Union has added tonnes of U S corn to its previous commitments for delivery in the fourth year of the U S USSR Grain Supply Agreement which began October the U S Agriculture Department said According to the department s Export Sales Report covering transactions in the week ended April corn transactions consisted of tonnes of new sales and changes in destinations for tonnes Total corn commitments to the USSR for delivery in the fourth agreement year amount to tonnes The Soviets have not purchased wheat or soybeans in the fourth year of the agreement Reuter
