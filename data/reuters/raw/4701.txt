GENERALE OCCIDENTALE SEEKS MLN DLR FACILITY LONDON March Generale Occidentale Paris has mandated Orion Royal Bank Ltd to arrange a mln dlr revolving credit facility Orion said Borrowings under the seven year facility can be in dollars or other currencies where available Drawings will be at pct over the London interbank offered rate for one three or six months or at pct over Royal Bank of Canada s U S Prime rate for periods of one to days There will be a commitment fee of pct per annum on the unutilised available amount and pct on the amount designated as unavailable Proceeds from the facility will be used to refinance an existing loan facility and provide a flexible source of finance for general corporate purposes Generale Occidentale a rare borrower in the international capital markets is engaged in food distribution forestry oil production and media activities with interests in Europe and North America REUTER
