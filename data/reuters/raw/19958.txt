CDC LIFE SCIENCES LSI TO STAKE SOLD TORONTO June Canada Development Corp CDC TO said it agreed to sell its pct interest in CDC Life Sciences Inc to Caisse de depot et placement du Quebec the provincial pension fund manager and Institut Merieux a French biological laboratory company for mln dlrs It said the caisse and Institut Merieux will each buy mln common shares of the company for dlrs a share It said following the transaction the caisse will hold about pct of CDC Life Sciences Canada Development said the purchasers do not plan to acquire the remaining publicly held shares Reuter
