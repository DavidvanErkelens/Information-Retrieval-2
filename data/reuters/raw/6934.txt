CZARNIKOW EXPECTS LOWER EUROPEAN SUGAR OUTPUT LONDON March European sugar output on the basis of three year average yields will be over half a mln tonnes white value down on last year although yields do vary widely from year to year broker C Czarnikow said in its market review European Community sowings are likely to be down compared with last year There have been suggestions these sowings might respond to the recent upsurge in world prices but Czarnikow said it is not the sort of fact that easily becomes known The broker bases its forecasts on Licht planting estimates which put W Germany the Netherlands and USSR lower but Hungary Romania Poland Turkey and Yugoslavia higher Czarnikow s projections in mlns tonnes white value and three differing yields include max aver min France W Germany EC W Europe Poland USSR E Europe All Europe REUTER
