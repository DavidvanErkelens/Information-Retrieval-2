KANSAS LEGISLATOR TO OFFER U S BILL TODAY WASHINGTON March U S Rep Dan Glickman D Kan chairman of the House Agriculture subcommittee on wheat soybeans and feedgrains said he would today introduce a bill to apply the so called concept to wheat and feedgrains producers Glickman told Reuters the measure would allow winter wheat producers and feedgrains producers the possibility of receiving no less than pct of their income support payments regardless of how much acreage they planted He also said his bill would protect program participants from reduced income payments in the event market prices rose above the loan rate Reuter
