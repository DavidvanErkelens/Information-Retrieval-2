GREYCOAT GROUP SEEKS TWO FINANCINGS LONDON April U K Property company Greycoat Group Plc is seeking two financings one for mln stg and the other for mln stg in connection with its Embankment Place development above and around Charing Cross station in London banking sources said The first transaction a mln stg credit will be arranged by N M Rothschild and Sons Ltd and Bank of Tokyo International Ltd on behalf of a special purpose Greycoat subsidiary yet to be incorporated It matures in June but will be extendible by up to two years to June at the option of the borrower The first credit a project financing will carry a basic margin of point over London Interbank Offered Rates LIBOR with an additional pct per annum payable on outstanding amounts to the extent that the property is not substantially pre let In addition if the property is pre sold while amounts are still outstanding the basic margin will be reduced to point over LIBOR the banking sources said For a one year extension of the maturity an extra point margin will be paid with a further point if the maturity is lengthened for the optional second year There will be a commitment fee of pct per annum on available amounts and of pct on unavailable amounts the sources said Rothschild is sole agent for this transaction The second transaction a mln stg credit for the purpose of working capital is being arranged by Rothschild alone on behalf of the Greycoat Group Plc itself It will be secured on a property called Brettenham House The margin will be point over LIBOR in years one to four point over LIBOR in years five and six and point over LIBOR thereafter The credit will mature in June The sources said that a commitment fee of basis points will be payable on the available tranche with basis points on unavailable amounts Reuter
