GENRAD GEN OFFERS SOFTWARE FOR TESTING CONCORD Mass March GenRad Inc said it introduced a software product that allows for development of test programs for its X line of in circuit functional test systems on Digital Equipment Corp s DEC VAX based computers GenRad said the product named ATG provides for off line development of test systems at four to eight times the speed of its current X test program It said ATG is priced at dlrs and is available immediately Reuter
