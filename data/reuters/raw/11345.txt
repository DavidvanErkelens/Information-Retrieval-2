SILICON GRAPHICS SGIC SELLS CONVERTIBLE DEBT NEW YORK March Silicon Graphics Inc is raising mln dlrs via an offering of convertible subordinated debentures due with a pct coupon and par pricing said lead manager Morgan Stanley and Co Inc The debentures are convertible into the company s common stock at dlrs per share representing a premium of pct over the stock price when terms on the debt were set Non callable for two years the issue is rated B by Moody s Investors Service Inc and CCC plus by Standard and Poor s Corp Alex Brown and Sons Inc co managed the deal Reuter
