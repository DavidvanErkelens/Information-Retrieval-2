ITALIAN TREASURY CERTIFICATE OFFER CLOSES EARLY ROME April The Italian Treasury said it had closed its latest offer of year variable coupon certificates CCTs early after receiving subscriptions totalling billion lire The amount of the offer scheduled to close April had not been prefixed The Treasury said it had closed the offer early in view of the market s response and having taken into account the estimated state borrowing requirement for the current month The CCTs were priced at pct for an effective net annual yield on the first coupon payable April of pct Terms were identical to those on the preceding offer REUTER
