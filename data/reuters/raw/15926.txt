EXPORT BONUS WHEAT FLOUR TO IRAQ USDA WASHINGTON April The Commodity Credit Corporation CCC accepted a bid for an export bonus to cover a sale of tonnes of U S wheat flour to Iraq the U S Agriculture Department said The department said the bonus awarded was dlrs per tonne and the wheat flour is for shipment July The bonus was awarded to The Pillsbury Company and will be paid in the form of commodities from CCC stocks An additional tonnes of wheat flour is still available to Iraq under the Export Enhancement Program initiative announced January the department said Reuter
