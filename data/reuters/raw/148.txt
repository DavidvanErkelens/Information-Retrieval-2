R P SCHERER SCHC SETS PREFERRED STOCK OFFER TROY MICH Feb R P Scherer Inc said it registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission a proposed public offering of mln shares of convertible exchangeable preferred stock at dlrs a share In addition the company said it is offering preferred shares to Richard Manoogian a Scherer director at dlrs a share Manoogian said he will buy the shares Proceeds from the offering will be used to repay debt and to fund research and development it said Underwriters are led by Goldman Sachs and Co Reuter
