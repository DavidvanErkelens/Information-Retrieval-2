DAI NIPPON TO ISSUE THREE DOMESTIC CONVERTIBLES TOKYO April Dai Nippon Printing Co Ltd DPRI T said it will issue three convertible yen bonds on domestic markets It will issue a billion yen year bond through public placement with Yamaichi Securities Co Ltd as lead manager It will also issue a billion yen year bond and a billion yen six year bond through public placement both with Nomura Securities Co Ltd as lead manager Coupon and conversion price for the par priced bonds will be set at Dai Nippon s next board meeting Payment will be due on June The year bond will mature on May the year on May and the six year on May Dai Nippon s share price rose to yen on the Tokyo Stock Exchange today REUTER
