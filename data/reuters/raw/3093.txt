DONALD TRUMP TO BUY CROSBY RESORTS RTB STOCK NEW YORK March Casino hotel operator and real estate developer Donald Trump said he has agreed to purchase the Class B common stock of Resorts International Inc held by the family and estate of late Resorts chairman James Crosby for dlrs per share Trump said he is also considering a tender offer for remaining Class B shares at the same price but has no interest in Resorts Class A shares Trump said the shares he has agreed already to buy represent a pct interest in the Class B stock The estate itself owns of the Class B shares There are also about Class A shares outstanding but Trump said I have no interest in the Class A shares Each Class B share has times the voting power of a Class A share In Atlantic City Resorts said in a statement that the Class B shares being sold to Trump represents pct of the combined voting power in Resorts It said closing is dependent on receipt of necessary regulatory approvals and other matters and after closing Trump will tender for remaining Class B shares at the same price Reuter
