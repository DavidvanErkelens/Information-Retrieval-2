QUEBEC CAISSE DESJARDINS BRINGS EUROYEN BOND LONDON March La Caisse Centrale Desjardins Du Quebec is issuing a billion yen eurobond due April paying five pct and priced at pct Yamaichi International Europe Ltd said as joint book runner for the deal Tokai International Ltd is other joint book runner The bond is available in denominations of one mln yen and will be listed in Luxembourg Fees comprise pct selling concession and pct for management and underwriting combined Reuter
