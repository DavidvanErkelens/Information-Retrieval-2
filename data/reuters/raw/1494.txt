NORTHROP NOC GETS MLN DLRS CONTRACT ROLLING MEADOWS Ill March Northrop Corp said its defense systems division based here has been awarded production and development contracts totaling mln dlrs to update the radar jamming system on the U S Air Force s F Eagle frontline tactical fighter Work under a mln dlrs production contract for a major system update of the AN ALQ internal countermeasure set is expected to be completed in April it said A separate mln dlrs contract for full scale development test equipment and program support for the AN ALQ receivers is included in the contract award Northrop said Reuter
