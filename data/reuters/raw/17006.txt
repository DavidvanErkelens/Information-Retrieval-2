MINT REVIEWS OFFERS ON LBS COPPER WASHINGTON April The U S Mint received offers from seven firms at prices ranging from dlrs per lb for payment by standard check and dlrs per lb for wire transfer payment in a review of offers on lbs of electrolytic copper it is seeking to purchase Philipp Brothers N Y led with the lowest offers of for wire transfer payment and dlrs per lb to be paid by check followed by Cerro Sales Corp N Y with dlrs per lb on one mln lbs for wire payment and dlrs per lb on one mln lbs for standard payment Firms in submitting offers elect to be paid by standard check or wire transfer with awards based on whichever of the two methods is more cost advantageous at that time Cerro Sales also offered prices for wire payment of dlrs per lb on one mln lbs and dlrs per lb on lbs Cerro s standard payment offers included dlrs per lb on one mln lbs and dlrs per lb on lbs Cargill Metals Minneapolis offered dlrs per lb for wire payment and dlrs per lb for standard payment while Elders Raw Materials Darien Ct offered dlrs per lb for wire payment and dlrs per lb for standard payment on increments of lbs each Other offers for wire transfer payment include dlrs per lb on lbs submitted by Deak International N Y dlrs per lb on the entire quantity by Diversified Metals Corp St Louis and dlrs per lb by Gerald Metals Stamford Ct Other standard payment offers include dlrs per lb on lbs by Diversified Metals and dlrs per lb on the entire quantity by Gerald Metals The Mint said the copper is for delivery the week of May to Olin Corp East Alton Ill The offers have a minimum acceptance period of three calendar days it said Reuter
