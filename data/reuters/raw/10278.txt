JAPAN ADOPTS STOP GAP BUDGET TOKYO March The government adopted an billion yen stop gap budget for fiscal starting April to compensate for the delay in the passage through the Parliament of the full budget The provisional budget includes billion yen in public works spending to help stimulate the economy and meet overseas demand for Japan to import more and reduce its huge trade surplus officials said They said the stop gap budget will be in effect for days through May and is the largest ever The budget includes billion yen in new government bond issues Officials said the spending on public works accounts for about two sevenths of the billion yen in public works outlay set aside in the pending formal budget The planned full budget totals billion yen The government had to work out the stop gap budget because budget debates have been delayed by an opposition boycott of Parliamentary business in protest against a planned five pct sales tax REUTER
