NIPPON OIL AND FATS ISSUES EQUITY WARRANT EUROBOND LONDON June Nippon Oil and Fats Co Ltd is issuing a mln dlr equity warrant eurobond due July with an indicated coupon of pct and par pricing lead manager Yamaichi International Europe Ltd said The issue is guaranteed by Fuji Bank Ltd and final terms will be fixed on June The selling concession is pct while management and underwriting combined pays pct The issue is available in denominations of dlrs and will be listed in Luxembourg The payment date is July and the warrants are exercisable from July until June REUTER
