ADB APPROVES MLN DLR LOANS FOR TWO PROJECTS MANILA April The Asian Development Bank ADB said it had approved two loans amounting to mln dollars for agricultural projects in Bangladesh and Nepal It said both loans are repayable over years including a year grace and carry a service charge of one pct a year The bank said it approved mln for an agricultural inputs program for Bangladesh aimed at sustaining foodgrain production It said the loan will cover the entire foreign exchange component of the project s total cost of mln The ADB said it approved mln dlrs to support an agricultural credit project in Nepal The loan will benefit Nepal by increasing foodgrain output augmenting production of milk and other dairy products and expanding energy generation in rural areas it said REUTER
