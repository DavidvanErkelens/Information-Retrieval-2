AID DONORS PROMISE SENEGAL BILLION DOLLARS PARIS April Aid donors have promised Senegal billion dollars in soft loan aid over the next three years to be split between project investment and balance of payment requirements the World Bank said in a statement It said after a two day meeting of the Consultative Group for Senegal held here under its auspices the aid was to support the second phase running to mid of Senegal s courageous and far reaching medium and long term adjustment programme Reuter
