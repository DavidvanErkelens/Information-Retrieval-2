MIDLAND BANK UNIT ISSUES MLN FRENCH FRANC FRN PARIS March Midland Bank International Financial Services is issuing a mln franc floating rate note due April at par lead manager Societe Generale said The deal guaranteed by Midland Bank Plc will pay interest based on rates of French week Treasury bills plus basis points in the first five years and plus points for the rest There is a short first coupon of pct payable June The note is accompanied by warrants priced at francs exercisable for five years into an pct year non callable bond The note is callable at any payment date after the first year Denominations are of and francs Co leads are Midland Bank SA and Samuel Montagu REUTER
