FINLAND FILES FOR MLN DLR DEBT OFFERING WASHINGTON April The Republic of Finland filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission for a shelf offering of up to mln dlrs of debt securities including notes and bonds and or warrants to buy debt securities on terms to be determined at the time of the sale The offering is in addition to mln dlrs of debt securities already registered with the SEC but unsold Proceeds from the sale will be used to finance capital investment and the promotion of productive investments and exports designed to strengthen the country s balance of payments the government said No underwriter was named Reuter
