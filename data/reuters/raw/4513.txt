IOWA BEEF LIFTS LOCKOUT AT DAKOTA CITY CHICAGO March Iowa Beef Processors Inc is lifting a lockout at its Dakota City Nebraska processing plant and plans to resume operations March United Food and Commercial Workers UFCW Union spokesman Allen Zack said Iowa Beef mailed a letter to members of UFCW Local informing them a lockout imposed by the company December would be lifted and meatpackers could return to work under Iowa Beef s revised last and best final offer Zack said Iowa Beef had closed the plant indefinitely in mid December because it said it had no alternative to threats by meatpackers to disrupt operations About members of Local are affected by the shutdown A year labor contract at the plant expired December Reuter
