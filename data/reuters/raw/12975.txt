CUSTOMS LEVIES BOND ON JAPANESE SHIPMENTS WASHINGTON April The Customs Bureau has begun to levy on bond on certain Japanese goods pending a final decision by President Reagan on April on imposing the planned pct tariff on the shipments the bureau said A bureau official said bonding began on March and if the penalties are imposed as expected after a period of comment they will retroactive to that date The penalties are to be levied on variety of electronic items in retaliation for Japan s failure to comply with a July agreement to end dumping semiconductors in third nations and to open its home market to U S goods reuter
