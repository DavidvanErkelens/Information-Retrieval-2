ADDITIONAL CCC CREDIT GUARANTEES FOR HUNGARY WASHINGTON March The Commodity Credit Corporation CCC has authorized an additional mln dlrs in credit guarantees for sales of vegetable protein meals to Hungary for fiscal year the U S Agriculture Department said The additional guarantees increase the vegetable protein meal credit line to mln dlrs and increases the cumulative fiscal year program for agricultural products to mln dlrs from mln it said The department also announced an extension of the export period from September to December for sales of vegetable protein meals To be eligible for the credit guarantees all sales must be registered before export but not later than September Reuter
