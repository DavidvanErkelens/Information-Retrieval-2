GILLETTE CANADA ISSUES MLN FRENCH FRANC BOND LONDON March Gillette Canada Inc is launching a mln French franc bond due April paying nine pct and priced at Banque Paribas said on behalf of Banque Paribas Paris the lead manager Morgan Guaranty Ltd will co lead manage the issue which will be sole in denominations of francs and be listed in Luxembourg Fees are pct for selling and pct for management and underwriting combined The bonds are non callable and payment is April REUTER
