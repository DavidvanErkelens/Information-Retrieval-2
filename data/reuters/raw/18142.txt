AIR CANADA POSTS TURNAROUND IN FIRST QUARTER MONTREAL June Air Canada owned by the Canadian Government said it recorded a net profit of mln dlrs in the first quarter compared to a mln dlr loss in the first quarter last year The company said the turnaround was due to improved passenger yields reduced unit costs and good control over capacity levels It said overall operating revenue rose eight pct compared to the year earlier level while total passenger revenue rose by nine pct Reuter
