U S REGULATORS TO CONSIDER CBT EVENING SESSION WASHINGTON March The Commodity Futures Trading Commission CFTC said that on April it will consider the Chicago Board of Trade s proposal to establish night trading The Chicago exchange hopes to begin night trading at the end of the month The proposed trading session which would be the first in the United States would extend from to local time to GMT CFTC also said that on April it will consider the Philadelphia Board of Trade s application to trade futures on the Australian dollar and the New York Cotton Exchange s application to trade the five year U S Treasury Index Reuter
