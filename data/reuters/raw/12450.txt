LUCAS INDUSTRIES ISSUING CONVERTIBLE EUROBOND LONDON April Lucas Industries Plc LUCS L said its Lucas Industries Inc unit is issuing a mln dlr convertible eurobond due April paying an indicated coupon of to pct and priced at par The issue is callable at pct declining by one pct per annum to par thereafter but is not callable until unless the share price reaches pct of the conversion price There is a put option after seven years which will be priced to give the investor a yield to the put of to pct The issue is guaranteed as to interest and the put premium by Lucas Industries Plc The issue is being lead managed by J Henry Schroder Wagg Ltd which has already formed a group of co managers The selling concession is pct while management and underwriting each pay pct The issue is available in denominations of dlrs while the payment is April Lucas said it intended to use the proceeds of the issue for general corporate purposes REUTER
