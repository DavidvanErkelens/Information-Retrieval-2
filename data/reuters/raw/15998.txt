GRAIN SHIPMENTS TO THE USSR USDA WASHINGTON April There were tonnes of U S corn shipped to the Soviet Union in the week ended April according to the U S Agriculture Department s latest Export Sales report That compares with tonnes shipped in the prior week There were no wheat or soybean shipments during the week The USSR has purchased tonnes of U S corn as of April for delivery in the fourth year of the U S USSR grain agreement Total shipments in the third year of the U S USSR grains agreement which ended September amounted to tonnes of wheat tonnes of corn and tonnes of soybeans Reuter
