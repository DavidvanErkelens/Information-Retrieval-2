SPAIN S PRIVATE BANK STAFF STRIKE IMPACT UNCLEAR MADRID June Spain s private bank staff started a one and a half day strike over wage demands but its impact was not yet clear Spokesmen for several of the main banks said strikers at their Madrid headquarters appeared to be a small minority They had yet to receive reports from branches Unions called out the employees to press for cuts in working hours and an eight pct wage rise Banks implemented a pct wage increase last month REUTER
