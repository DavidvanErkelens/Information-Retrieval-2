NIPPON SHOKUBAI ISSUES MLN DLR WARRANT BOND LONDON April Nippon Shokubai Kagaku Kogyo is issuing a mln dlr equity warrant eurobond due April paying an indicated coupon of pct and priced at par lead manager Nomura International Ltd said Final terms will be fixed on April and the issue is guaranteed by Daiwa Bank Ltd The selling concession is pct while management and underwriting combined pays pct The non callable bond is available in denominations of dlrs and will be listed in Luxembourg The payment date is April and the warrants are exercisable from May until April REUTER
