U S TREASURY SELLING BILLION DLRS OF NOTES WASHINGTON March The U S Treasury said it will auction billion dlrs of two four and seven year notes next week to raise a total of billion dlrs of new cash The financing will begin with the monthly auction on Tuesday March of billion dlrs of two year notes That will be followed on Wednesday and Thursday by respective quarterly sales of billion dlrs of four year notes and billion dlrs of seven year notes to refund billion dlrs of two year and four year notes maturing March The two year notes mature March The four years mature March with the seven years due April The two year notes will be issued in minimum denominations of dlrs and the four and seven year notes will be in minimum denominations of dlrs The two year notes and the four year notes will be issued March and the seven year notes April The Treasury said non competitive bids will be accepted for all three issues Reuter
