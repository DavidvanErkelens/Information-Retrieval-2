WORK ON GREEK ALUMINA PLANT TO START END MAY ATHENS April Preparatory work for construction of a mln dlr alumina plant near the village of Aghia Efthymia in the Greek province of Fokida will begin at the end of next month and the plant will be operational by Industry Undersecretary George Petsos said Greece yesterday signed contracts with the Soviet Union for the joint venture project the biggest investment in the country for years Petsos said the Soviet Union which had initially agreed to buy tonnes would now purchase the plant s entire output of tonnes a year Reuter
