REGENCY CRUISES SHIP SELLS SHIP INTEREST NEW YORK March Regency Cruises Inc said it agreed to sell a pct interest in the corporation that owns the M V Regent Sea cruise ship for mln dlrs to Monmouth International SA which owns the other pct The company said it also extended a mln dlr secured loan to Monmouth to finance completion of the renovation of another vessel the M V Regent Star which is scheduled to begin operating in late June Regency Cruises which operates both ships received a five year extension to November to the Regent Sea s original charter agreement it said It also received a reduction to dlrs from mln dlrs of its total charter guarantee for the Regent Sea and Regent Star Regency also reported earnings of dlrs or cts a share on revenues of mln dlrs It began operations in November In addition the company said its bank Irving Bank Corp V agreed to waive a one mln dlr counter guarantee for the bank to provide a mln dlrs guarantee for Regency s Federal Maritime Commission bond The company said after April the exercise price of its warrants will return to two dlrs until they expire April The price had been reduced to dlrs for the three weeks ending April Reuter
