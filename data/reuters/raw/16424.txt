MITEL MLT HAS TWO NEW PRODUCTS OTTAWA April Mitel Corp said it introduced two new digital telephone switching systems and additional applications software for its current product line The new SX s digital private branch exchange is a smaller version of the SX directed at line capacities of to telephone extensions The other new product is the even smaller SX for to telephones with software specifications designed for the hotel and motel industry Mitel said it has developed new software packages to extend the data capabilities of the SX to high speed applications found in an IBM type environment Reuter
