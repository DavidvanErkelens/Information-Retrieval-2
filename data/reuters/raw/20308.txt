SEA GOLD OIL ANNOUNCES PRIVATE PLACEMENT VANCOUVER British Columbia Oct Sea Gold Oil Corp said it has agreed to a private placement financing of common shares of its unissued capital stock with Gulf International Minerals Ltd GIM V at cts per share to raise dlrs Canadian The company said one of the conditions of the placement was the granting of a first right of refusal to Gulf in the event that the company should choose to enter into a joint venture agreement on any of its mineral claims currently held in the Skyline area If Gulf should sell any of the shares the first right of refusal will be null and void the company said Proceeds will be used for the continued exploration and development of the Sea Gold s mining properties Reuter
