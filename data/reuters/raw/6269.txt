SENATE BUDGET GROUP SHELVES REAGAN ECONOMIC DATA WASHINGTON March The Senate Budget Committee at its first budget drafting meeting of the year discarded President Reagan s economic assumptions upon which he claims his budget would show a billion dlr deficit In its place the committee agreed to accept the non partisan Congressional Budget Office CBO economic assumptions in its deliberations for drafting a budget The CBO says Reagan s budget even if it were adopted without change would show a billion dlr deficit in fiscal and thereby miss the Gramm Rudman budget law target of billion dlrs Reuter
