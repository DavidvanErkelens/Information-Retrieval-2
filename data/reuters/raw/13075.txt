ANADARKO PETROLEUM APC SELLS CONVERTIBLE DEBT NEW YORK April Anadarko Petroleum Corp is raising mln dlrs via an offering of convertible subordinated debentures due with a pct coupon and par pricing said sole manager Kidder Peabody and Co Inc The debentures are convertible into the company s common stock at dlrs a share representing a premium of pct over the stock price when terms on the debt were set Non redeemable for two years the debentures have a mandatory sinking fund beginning in calculated to retire pct of the issue prior to maturity Kidder said Moody s and S and P ratings are pending Reuter
