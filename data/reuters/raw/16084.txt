PACIFIC DUNLOP PACA ME SETS ONE FOR TEN ISSUE MELBOURNE April Pacific Dunlop Ltd said it will make a one for ten bonus issue from its asset revaluation reserve to shareholders registered June The bonus involves the issue of about mln shares the diversified rubber and plastics group said in a statement The new shares will not rank for the deferred annual dividend of not less than cts that Pacific Dunlop will pay in August but will rank pari passu thereafter it said As previously reported it did not pay an interim dividend for the first half to December pending a review of the new dividend imputation legislation REUTER
