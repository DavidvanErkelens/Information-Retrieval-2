venezuelan bank begins signing debt contracts Caracas april the central bank began signing contracts by which it will provide dollars at a preferential rate for the payment of billion dlrs of foreign debt under a new plan announced last december A statement from the bank said twelve companies have already been called to sign the agreements and the process is expected to continue in the coming weeks Under the new eight year repayment plan private sector debtors may obtain dollars at the preferential bolivar rate by paying a premium of of bolivars on principal and up to bolivars on interest Reuter
