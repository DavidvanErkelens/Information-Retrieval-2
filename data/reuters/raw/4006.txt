UNUSUAL TEXAS INSTRUMENTS TXN PREFERRED PRICED NEW YORK March In a novel type of financing Texas Instruments Inc marketed a three part mln dlr issue of convertible money market preferred stock through Shearson Lehman Brothers Inc as sole manager Shearson which originated the new convertible concept said each of the three tranches totaled mln dlrs In the first a pct dividend was set on the stock with a strike price of dlrs that represented a pct premium over the common stock price when terms were set Also included were pct dividend preferred with a dlr strike price and pct premium and pct dividend preferred with a dlr strike price and pct premium Texas Instruments common closed at dlrs up Ronald Gallatin managing director at Shearson said that demand for the offering was unbelievable especially for the first tranche He said that Shearson originated the concept of auction money market preferred stock three years ago The conversion feature of this issue is the new wrinkle Commenting on the first tranche Gallatin noted that the original pricing talk called for a dividend in the four to pct area This was gradually cut to pct because of intense demand saving the issuer money in financing costs The Shearson official said that virtually all buyers of the first tranche received less than they wanted He said the latter two tranches were less strongly oversubscribed Like non convertible money market preferred stock the new version allows investors to redeem their holdings every seven weeks Investors then can maintain their holdings sell them or offer to hold on to the securities if the auction dividend is at least at a level they specify in advance Gallatin said the securities were sold to a broad range of investors including major insurance companies banks money managers and pension funds Reuter
