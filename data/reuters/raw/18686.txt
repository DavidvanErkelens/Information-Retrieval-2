CONSECO CNSC O EMERGES FROM BANKRUPTCY LITTLE FERRY N J June Conseco Industries Ltd said an order of confirmation of its reorganization plan has been signed in its Chapter bankruptcy proceedings terminating its status as a debtor in possession Conseco said under the plan general creditors and debenture holders will receive pct of their claims over a two year period and president Charles J Trainor will pay dlrs for new shares The company said debenture holders will have days to convert their debentures into common shares It said it has arranged for two mln dlrs of new bank financing Reuter
