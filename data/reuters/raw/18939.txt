PNC FINANCIAL PNCF O RAISES LOSS RESERVE PITTSBURGH June PNC Financial Corp said it will raise its loan loss reserve by mln dlrs to about mln dlrs reducing second quarter results by mln dlrs or cts per share on a fully diluted basis The company said that after the charge it still expects to report a second quarter profit and expects a first half profit of about mln dlrs It added that it expects normal operating earnings in the second half The company earned mln dlrs in the second quarter and mln dlrs in the prior first half It said the increase in loss reserves was due to its appraisal of the worldwide economy particularly in less developed countries that are experiencing payment problems The company said the part of its loss reserve allocated to such countries is about pct of its loans to those countries It said the move would not result in any change to its current dividend policy Reuter
