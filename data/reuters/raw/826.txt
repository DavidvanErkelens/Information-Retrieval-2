KIDDER UNIT SELLS CMOS INCLUDING FLOATERS NEW YORK March Kidder Peabody Mortgage Assets Trust Five a unit of Kidder Peabody and Co Inc is offering mln dlrs of collateralized mortgage obligations in seven classes including floating rate and inverse rate tranches Sole manager Kidder Peabody said the first floating rate class has an initial rate of pct that will be reset quarterly at basis points over three month LIBOR with an pct cap Totaling mln dlrs this floating rate class contains a so called catchup provision that allows investors to recapture possible lost interest according to Kidder Peabody For instance if three month LIBOR rises to pct during the life of the floating rate tranche and falls back below the pct cap investors would be paid back dollar for dollar the amount of interest they would have received if the CMOs did not carry a maximum interest rate a Kidder officer said Kidder Peabody introduced this concept on February The other floating rate class totals mln dlrs and has an initial rate of pct that will be reset quarterly at basis points over three month LIBOR with a pct cap This tranche does not have a catch up provision the Kidder officer said The inverse rate tranche totals mln dlrs and has an initial rate of pct The rate will be reset quarterly according to the formula of minus the product of times three month LIBOR Yields on the remaining fixed rate CMOs the balance of the mln dlr issue range from to pct or to basis points over comparable Treasuries The issue is rated a top flight AAA by Standard and Poor s Reuter
