UTILICORP UCU DIVISION CUTS GAS RATES KANSAS CITY Mo April Utilicorp United said the Iowa Utilities Board has ordered its Peoples Natural Gas division to reduce its rates to customers by dlrs Utilicorp said it will request a reconsideration of the order within days and the Board will have days to act upon the request The company said the reduction was the result of a rate request filed by Peoples in May for an mln dlr increase The Board granted Peoples an interim increase of mln dlrs in August Utilicorp said Reuter
