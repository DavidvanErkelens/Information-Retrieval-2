NATIONWIDE ISSUING INDEX LINKED LOAN STOCK LONDON April Nationwide Building Society said it is issuing mln stg of index linked loan stock due by way of a placing on a yield basis in the domestic sterling market The issue s real rate of return price and the amount of the first interest payment will be fixed at gmt today The real rate of return will be the sum of pct plus the gross real rate of return on the pct index linked Treasury stock due The issue will be partly paid with stg pct payable on acceptance and the balance on July REUTER
