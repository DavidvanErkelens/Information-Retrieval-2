HOKKAIDO TAKUSHOKU BANK ISSUES CONVERTIBLE BOND LONDON April Hokkaido Takushoku Bank Ltd is issuing a mln dlr convertible eurobond due March paying an indicated coupon of two pct lead managed by Takugin International bookrunner Yamaichi International Ltd said The par priced issue is callable from at pct declining by pct per annum to par thereafter but is not callable until unless the share price exceeds the conversion price by pct Final terms will be fixed on April The selling concession is pct while management and underwriting each pay pct The payment date is April and there is a short first coupon REUTER
