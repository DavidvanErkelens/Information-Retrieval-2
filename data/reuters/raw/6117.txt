ARGENTINA CROP WEATHER SUMMARY USDA NOAA WASHINGTON March Precipitation diminished over Argentina s summer crop areas in the week ended March the U S Agriculture and Commerce Departments Joint Weather Facility said In its International Weather and Crop Summary the agency said crop areas in Cordoba Santa Fe and Buenos Aires received only light scattered showers three to millimeters The beneficial drier weather allowed drying of fields for summer crop harvesting following February s heavy rainfall it said Reuter
