JAPAN MACHINERY ORDERS FALL IN APRIL TOKYO June Japan s private sector machinery orders excluding shipbuilding fell pct in April from March to a seasonally adjusted billion yen after rising pct in March the government s Economic Planning Agency said April orders rose pct from a year earlier after a pct year on year rise in March an agency spokesman told Reuters Seasonally adjusted private sector orders excluding those for shipbuilding and electric power firms fell pct in April from March to billion yen after a pct rise in March from February April orders fell pct from a year earlier after being unchanged in March The April drop was due mainly to a pct decrease in orders from machine tool industries and a pct drop in orders from car makers the spokesman said REUTER
