SALLIE MAE ANNOUNCES AUSTRALIAN DLR NOTE OFFER WASHINGTON March The Student Loan Marketing Association announced an offering of Australian mln dlrs approx U S mln in two year fixed rate notes priced at par and bearing interest at pct per annum Sallie Mae said semiannual interest payments and principal at maturity will be paid in Australian dollars adding it will enter into a separate currency and interest exchange agreement to convert its payment obligations to U S dollars The notes offered in U S capital markets by Salomon Brothers Inc are due April and are for settlement April with the first interest payment being made Oct Reuter
