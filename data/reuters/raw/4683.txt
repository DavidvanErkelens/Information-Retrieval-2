ELDERS HALVES NOTE FACILITY AMOUNT AMENDS TERMS HONG KONG March The proposed note issuance facility for Elders IXL Ltd has been reduced to mln Australian dlrs from the original mln dlrs lead manager Westpac Finance Asia Ltd said Terms of the facility have also been amended It will still be half underwritten and half uncommitted but the underwriting margin has been raised to basis points over the Australian dollar bank bill rate The margin was originally set at basis points over bank bill rate for up to mln dlrs and at basis points for the remaining mln dlrs There is a basis point utilisation fee if the underwriters are required to pick up more than pct of the paper issued and a five basis point fee if they are to pick up pct of the paper No utilisation fee will be charged if they are to pick up less than pct Underwriting fee is pct Under the facility notes in denominations of dlrs will be issued with maturities ranging from one to six months Syndication deadline has been extended to March from today to allow banks more time to consider the facility REUTER
