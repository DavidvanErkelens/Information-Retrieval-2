ADB APPROVES LOANS FOR AGRICULTURAL PROJECTS MANILA April The Asian Development Bank ADB said it approved two loans totalling mln dlrs for agricultural projects in Bangladesh and Nepal Both loans are repayable over years including a year grace and carry a service charge of one pct a year The bank approved mln dlrs for an agricultural inputs program for Bangladesh aimed at sustaining foodgrain production The loan will cover the foreign exchange component of the project s total cost of mln dlrs The ADB also approved mln dlrs for an agricultural credit project in Nepal to increase foodgrain and dairy produce output REUTER
