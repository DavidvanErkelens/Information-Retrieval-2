ELSINORE ELS MAKES DEPOSIT FOR INTEREST PAYMENT NEW YORK March Elsinore Corp said it deposited about mln dlrs with trustee Manufacturers Hanover Trust Co to cover accrued and unpaid interest on the pct senior mortgage bonds due of its unit Elsinore Finance Corp The interest has not been paid for January and February and will be paid April to bondholders of record on April the parent company said Elsinore said it has guaranteed the payment of the bonds The interest payment is being made in line with the terms of a previously announced agreement in principle it reached with an unofficial committee of senior bondholders Reuter
