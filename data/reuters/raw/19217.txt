BAII DETAILS ONE BILLION FRANC DOMESTIC BOND PARIS June Banque Arabe et Internationale d Investissement BAII will issue a one billion franc non callable pre placed bond in three tranches on July lead manager Banque Nationale de Paris BNP said Banque Indosuez will manage the mln franc A tranche issued at par with a life of nine years paying interest based on the annualised money market rate TAM plus pct The mln franc B tranche managed by Banque d Arbitrage et de Credit BAC will be issued at par with a life of years paying interest at TAM plus pct The mln franc C tranche managed by BNP and Banque Demachy et Associes will be issued at par with a life of years paying interest at TAM plus pct REUTER
