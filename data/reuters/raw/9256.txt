YUGOSLAVIA SEEKS MLN ECU LOAN FROM EC BELGRADE March Yugoslavia is seeking a loan of mln ECUs from the European Community for transport system modernisation Deputy Finance Minister Boris Skapin was quoted by the official Tanjug news agency The community has offered Yugoslavia only mln ECUs he said The loan is to help finance completion of Yugoslavia s north south motorway railway modernisation and export oriented industrial projects Skapin told reporters in Brussels yesterday the requested loan accounted for only pct of total funds needed for these projects Tanjug said REUTER
